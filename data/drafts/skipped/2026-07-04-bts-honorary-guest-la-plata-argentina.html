BTS has been named 'Honorary Distinguished Guests' of La Plata, Argentina, where the group is set to perform in October 2026. Big Hit Music announced on July 4 that the La Plata City Council unanimously passed a resolution appointing BTS to the honorary title at its regular session held on July 2 (local time).

The 'Honorary Distinguished Guest' designation is the highest level of official recognition granted under La Plata city ordinance to distinguished visitors and internationally acclaimed figures in culture and the arts.

The city council cited BTS's cultural impact, their contribution to promoting the rights of youth around the world, and their international activities in the cultural sphere as the reasons for the selection.

Local outlet Infocielo highlighted the fact that the council voted unanimously in favor — noting it as an exceptional occurrence — and praised BTS's influence. According to the outlet, La Plata Mayor Julio Alak has been providing full city-level support, including reviewing security and traffic systems to handle the large movement of audiences and coordinating accommodation regulations with the hotel association.

BTS will perform at Estadio Único de La Plata (La Plata Stadium) in La Plata, the capital of Buenos Aires Province, on October 21 and 23–24 — three dates in total. This marks the first time a Korean artist has performed at the venue. The concerts are part of their world tour 'ARIRANG'.
