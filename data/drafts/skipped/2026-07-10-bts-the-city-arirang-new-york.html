BTS is set to light up New York City with an offline fan event called 'BTS The City Arirang - New York,' running from July 24 to August 3, 2026 (local time). Big Hit Music announced the event on July 10, confirming it will take place across multiple locations in New York.

'The City Arirang - New York' is scheduled to run around BTS's world tour 'Arirang' concerts, which will be held at MetLife Stadium near New York on August 1 and 2.

Grand Central Terminal — a historic landmark and the world's largest train station by daily foot traffic, drawing an average of 750,000 visitors per day — will be transformed with a theme based on BTS's fifth full-length album 'ARIRANG.' Red posters, the key color of the new album, will be displayed throughout the station.

Inside the terminal's Vanderbilt Hall, an 'ARMY Madang' (fan space) will be set up. The space will feature a display of the stage costumes BTS wore during their 2019 performance at Wembley Stadium in London, along with a variety of additional events.
