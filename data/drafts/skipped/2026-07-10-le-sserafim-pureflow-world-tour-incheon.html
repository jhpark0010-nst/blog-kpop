LE SSERAFIM will kick off their second world tour on July 11 and 12 at Inspire Arena in Incheon, their agency Source Music announced on July 10.

The tour is titled PUREFLOW, named after the group's second full-length album 'PUREFLOW' pt.1, released in May. The concerts are designed to carry the messages embedded in that album.

The members said, 'If you listen to the album beforehand, you'll enjoy the show even more.' They added, 'If our first world tour showed the journey we've walked so far, this tour will be a stage that marks a new beginning.'

The concerts will also feature a full-group performance of BOOMPALA, the title track from the second album. Member Kim Chaewon had previously been unable to participate in promotions for the new song due to throat pain.

Following the Incheon dates, LE SSERAFIM will continue the tour in Osaka, Japan on July 25 and 26, and wrap up in Manila, Philippines on December 5 and 6. In total, the group will perform 32 shows across 23 cities in Asia, North America, and Europe over five months.
