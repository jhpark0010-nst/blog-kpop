CORTIS's second mini-album 'GREENGREEN' has surpassed 200 million cumulative streams on Spotify, the world's largest music streaming platform, their agency Big Hit Music announced on June 20. The milestone was reached on June 18, exactly 45 days after the album's release.

The album had previously hit 100 million streams on May 19, meaning it took just 30 more days to double that count. Big Hit Music stated that 'the strong performance of the title track REDRED was the driving force behind this accelerated record.'

REDRED itself crossed 100 million cumulative streams on June 16. The track also continues to chart on Spotify's Weekly Top Songs Global chart, appearing at No. 112 in its eighth consecutive week on the chart.

To mark the 2026 FIFA World Cup in North and Central America, CORTIS also surprised fans by releasing a short-form video of a special World Cup cheer version of 'REDRED,' with the lyrics rewritten as 'Let's go to the Round of 16, Green Green~'.

Next month, CORTIS will kick off their first solo tour, 'PUT YOUR PHONE DOWN,' at Inspire Arena on Incheon's Yeongjongdo island, with the tour spanning Korea, North America, and Japan. The group is also set to attend Paris Men's Fashion Week, which opens on June 23 (local time).
