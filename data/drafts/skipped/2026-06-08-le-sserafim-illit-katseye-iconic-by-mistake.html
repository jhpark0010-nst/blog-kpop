Three HYBE girl groups — LE SSERAFIM, ILLIT, and KATSEYE — are joining forces to release a collaboration single on June 12, 2026. The new digital single, titled 'ICONIC BY MISTAKE,' will drop at 1 PM KST.

The announcement was made on June 8 through the fan platform Weverse by the groups' respective HYBE labels: Source Music, Belift Lab, and HYBE-Geffen Records. The three labels confirmed the release date and time of the upcoming track.

The agencies described the collaboration as a meeting of global icons each loved for their distinct musical color. They also noted that the three teams have actively interacted through challenge shoots and other activities, naturally sparking talk of one day sharing a stage together — which served as the background for this collaboration.

The three groups will also perform 'ICONIC BY MISTAKE' live on Mnet's music program M Countdown on June 11, the day before the single's official release.

The agencies added that they plan to present a highly polished performance stage as well as a variety of content to give global fans a special treat this summer.

LE SSERAFIM and ILLIT each earned love this past spring with their respective new tracks 'BOOMPALA' and 'It's Me.' KATSEYE, meanwhile, took home three awards last month at the American Music Awards in the United States, including New Artist of the Year.
