SEVENTEEN held their fan meeting 'Carat Land' at Incheon Asiad Main Stadium in Seo-gu, Incheon on June 20 and 21, 2026, meeting approximately 58,000 fans over the two days, according to Pledis Entertainment on June 22.

The fan meeting, which sold out quickly through fan club pre-sales, was held at stadium scale. The members interacted with fans across five different stages throughout the event.

The members performed a variety of segments, including a 'Solo Reverse' stage where they reinterpreted each other's solo songs, as well as a segment called 'Challenge We Openly Want to Show.' They also performed more than 20 songs over three hours, spanning past hit tracks such as 'Aju Nice' and 'Pretty U.'

SEVENTEEN shared their thoughts, saying, 'We feel like we have wrapped up one chapter well.' They added, 'It is all thanks to the endless love and support that Carats (the fandom name) have shown us over 11 years. We are grateful to everyone who has been with us, even for just a moment.'
