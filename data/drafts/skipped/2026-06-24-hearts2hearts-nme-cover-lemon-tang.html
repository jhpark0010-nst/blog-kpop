Hearts2Hearts has received high praise from the renowned UK music outlet NME. On June 22 (local time), NME published a 'The Cover' feature on its official website spotlighting Hearts2Hearts — members Carmen, Jiwoo, Yuha, Stella, Jooeun, Aina, Ian, and Yeon — alongside their second mini-album 'Lemon Tang'.

The feature drew significant attention for its casual visual images of the members as well as in-depth interviews covering a wide range of topics.

In the interview, Hearts2Hearts introduced their new song 'Lemon Tang' by saying, 'It's a song that blows away stress. The moment you hear it, your mood lifts — it captures the essence of a summer song that gives you energy while also feeling comfortable, expressed in Hearts2Hearts' own color.'

The group also shared, 'The biggest driving force that keeps us moving forward is the bond between the members. Because there are eight of us, we can create far more diverse possibilities in choreography formations, storytelling, and the way we connect with each other during performances.'

NME praised the group, writing that 'Hearts2Hearts is a team that shows authenticity and dynamism wherever they go, with a clear will to add their own personality and imagination to K-pop beyond existing formulas. There is a sense of ambition to carry on SM's iconic girl group lineage while also creating a new legacy that is uniquely Hearts2Hearts.' The outlet highlighted the members' bright, bubbly energy and diverse charms.

Meanwhile, Hearts2Hearts is set to appear on SBS Power FM '12 o'clock with Joo Hyunyoung' on June 24, followed by performances of 'Lemon Tang' on Mnet 'M Countdown' on June 25, '2026 MyK FESTA' on June 26, MBC 'Show! Music Core' on June 27, and SBS 'Inkigayo' along with the '2026 Busan One Asia Festival (BOF with NOL)' on June 28.
