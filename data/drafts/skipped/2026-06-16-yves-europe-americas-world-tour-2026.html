Yves has successfully completed a large-scale world tour spanning 9 cities in Europe and 14 cities in the Americas, alongside the release of her fourth EP, NAIL. She sold out every date of the 'YVES EUROPE TOUR' and continued to draw an enthusiastic response through the '2026 YVES TOUR THE AMERICAS'.

On stage, Yves performed her signature solo tracks including 'LOOP (feat. Lil Cherry)', 'Viola', 'White cat', 'Soap (feat. PinkPantheress)', and 'DIM', demonstrating a wide musical spectrum. Her unique vocals, polished performance, and sensory-driven staging drew passionate cheers from local audiences.

During the tour, the title track 'NAIL (feat. Lolo Zouaï)' from her fourth EP reached No. 1 on the Canada YouTube Music daily Shorts chart, proving strong global buzz. The distinctive musical world and performances she has steadily built since her solo debut continue to attract international fans and show consistent growth.

Q: Starting with Manchester, UK in April for the Europe tour, then continuing to the Americas in May and June — what were your feelings after meeting global fans in person for about two months? A: 'Seeing the fans from each country with my own eyes and receiving their messages of support, I felt like I was finally confirming the reality of a love that had always felt distant, and I was genuinely grateful. There were moments during the long schedule when I hit physical limits, but every time it was the fans' cheers that brought me back to the stage. As time went on, I found myself truly enjoying the stage, which made it an even more meaningful and happy experience.'

Q: What did you feel most strongly while connecting with local fans, and what moment stands out most in your memory? A: 'They say fans come to resemble the artist, so perhaps that's why there seemed to be many shy and introverted people like me. But once the music started, seeing everyone sing along to every lyric beyond language and nationality was deeply moving. I also found it so lovely to see fans mingling happily with each other before the show, waiting for it to begin. I could feel how much love they wanted to share with me, and I was truly grateful.'

Q: What do you think is the secret behind this global response? A: 'Since my debut I have received a lot of love not only in Korea but also overseas, and it has always felt both surprising and gratifying. I think people appreciate seeing me walk my own path honestly and with conviction, and challenging various genres and concepts without setting limits on myself. Collaborations with various global artists are also likely one of the reasons people take an interest.' Q: What are your plans going forward? A: 'I am proud and grateful to have successfully released the fourth EP NAIL and completed the tour. Now that I am back to everyday life, I want to focus on writing songs and come back quickly with even better music, and I also want to greet fans often through YouTube content and various activities.'
