BLACKPINK's Jennie has finally revealed the identity of the mystery man who set her social media accounts ablaze. On July 21, her label OA Entertainment (ODDATELIER) surprise-dropped a teaser poster for Jennie's new single 'Less than a Lover' (레스 댄 어 러버), unveiling the face that had sparked endless speculation among fans.

The image features the same man Jennie had previously shared in a polaroid photo, which sent the internet into a frenzy. The person, whose face had been hidden behind a heart sticker in the original photo, has been confirmed as Taiwanese model Nah Kai. The two will share a special on-screen chemistry in the upcoming music video.

Earlier, Jennie had posted a polaroid of herself leaning comfortably on a man's shoulder, sparking widespread curiosity online. Because his face was covered by a heart sticker, fans analyzed his build, hairstyle, and follower connections to narrow down the candidates, eventually pointing to Kai as the most likely person. The teaser reveal confirmed that the mystery was all part of the promotional campaign for the new single.

In the released teaser poster, the two are seen facing each other inside a pickup truck against a refreshing summer backdrop. The cinematic, emotionally evocative direction hints at the wistful atmosphere the new song is expected to deliver.

'Less than a Lover' is set to release on July 24 at 1 p.m. KST across major music platforms. The track is a summer song blending lo-fi guitar and vintage electric keyboard sounds. Jennie participated directly in the overall production, including songwriting and music video direction, signaling a broader musical spectrum as an artist.

Having completed her comeback preparations amid enthusiastic global fan support, Jennie expressed her passion for her diverse activities, saying, 'I am grateful for the warm response when I previewed the new song at the Governors Ball Music Festival 2026 in the United States and the Mad Cool Festival in Spain.'
