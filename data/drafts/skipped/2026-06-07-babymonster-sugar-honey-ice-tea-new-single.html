BABYMONSTER will release their new single 'SUGAR HONEY ICE TEA' at midnight on June 8, according to their agency YG Entertainment on June 7.

'SUGAR HONEY ICE TEA' is an uptempo dance track featuring a rhythmic bass sound and a highly addictive hook — a memorable, repeating chorus that leaves a strong impression.

The song contrasts sweet keywords like 'sugar' and 'honey' with a burst of high energy, aiming for the kind of music that everyone can enjoy lightly, like a fizzy carbonated drink.

This marks their first new release in one month, following their mini album 'CHOOM,' which dropped on May 4.

BABYMONSTER will kick off their second world tour, also titled 'CHOOM,' at Seoul's Jamsil Indoor Stadium from June 26 to 28. They will then meet fans across 27 shows in 18 cities spanning Asia including Japan, North America, Europe, and South America.
