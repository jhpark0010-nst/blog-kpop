Former BIGBANG member T.O.P is embarking on his first solo fan meeting tour, 20 years after his debut. According to his agency TOPSPOT PICTURES on June 29, T.O.P will hold his first Asia fan meeting tour titled 'T.O.P PRE-STUDIO 2026.' This marks his first Asia fan meeting tour since his debut in 2006.

Ahead of the tour, T.O.P is set to host a fully complimentary fan meeting for approximately 10,000 members of his official Japanese fan community TOPSX on July 9 at PIA ARENA MM in Yokohama, Japan. All seats will be offered free of charge.

T.O.P debuted with the group BIGBANG in 2006 but departed from the team in 2023. He made his solo debut in 2010 with the digital single 'Turn It Up,' and released his first full-length solo album, 'ANOTHER DIMENSION' (다중관점), in April of this year.

T.O.P also drew attention for his role as Thanos in Season 2 of the Netflix original series 'Squid Game' in 2024.
