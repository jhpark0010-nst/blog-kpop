Jennie has teamed up with trending Taiwanese model Nah Kai. On July 21, Jennie's agency OA Entertainment (ODDATELIER) announced via official social media that Jennie will release her single 'Less than a Lover' on July 24 at 1 PM KST across major music platforms both in Korea and internationally.

The released teaser images evoke a cinematic atmosphere. They capture Jennie and Nah Kai sharing a moment of excitement framed by natural scenery seen through a truck window, completing a romantic summer mood. In particular, curiosity about the male figure in the images has been constant.

A single Polaroid photo released earlier had already sparked widespread buzz. It showed Jennie leaning on someone's shoulder, and speculation about the person's identity continued until the reveal. That person turned out to be Nah Kai, a Taiwanese model born in 2002. He is reportedly more well-known in Korea than in his home country of Taiwan.

Jennie had previously previewed the new song at several major festivals, including the Governors Ball Music Festival 2026 in the United States, Roskilde Festival in Denmark, Open'er Festival in Poland, and Mad Cool Festival 2026 in Spain. The previews generated significant buzz, and an overwhelming number of requests from global fans for an official release led to the single's formal release being confirmed.

The single drops on July 24 at 1 PM KST.
