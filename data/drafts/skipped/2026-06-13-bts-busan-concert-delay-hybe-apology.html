BTS's ARIRANG world tour show in Busan on June 12 was delayed by more than an hour, prompting an apology from their label. The concert, held at Busan Asiad Main Stadium, was originally scheduled to begin at 7:00 PM but did not start until approximately 8:15 PM — about 1 hour and 15 minutes late — due to delays in audience entry. Complaints about the inconvenience spread quickly across online communities.

Label HYBE (Big Hit Music) posted a notice on fan platform Weverse on the night of June 12, stating: 'We sincerely apologize to all audience members who came to watch the ARIRANG world tour on June 12 for the significant inconvenience caused by the delayed start of the concert.'

HYBE explained the cause of the delay: 'We prepared as thoroughly as possible so that there would be no shortcomings in operations, in order to provide a meaningful experience for our audience. However, a combination of factors — including confusion in on-site guidance, bottlenecks in the fan gift distribution queue, and delays in merchandise pickup — led to the delayed start of the main concert.'

The label added: 'We once again sincerely apologize for causing great disappointment and inconvenience to the audience members who waited a long time to see the concert.' HYBE also announced that for the June 13 show, it would 'thoroughly review and improve all aspects of on-site operations, including entry and gift distribution, to prevent the same congestion from occurring.'

BTS held their second Busan concert on June 13, which is also the group's 13th debut anniversary. Meanwhile, the new single 'Come Over,' released the day before, reached No. 1 on the iTunes 'Top Songs' chart in 79 countries — including the United States, United Kingdom, France, and Japan — as of 6:00 AM on June 13. The song was released during '2026 BTS FESTA,' a celebration week for the group's debut anniversary. Their fifth studio album ARIRANG also claimed its ninth all-time No. 1 on Spotify's 'Weekly Top Albums Global' chart, marking two consecutive weeks at the top.
