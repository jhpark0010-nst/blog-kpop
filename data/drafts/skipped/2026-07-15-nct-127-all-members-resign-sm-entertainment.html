All seven members of NCT 127 have renewed their contracts with SM Entertainment. On July 15, SM Entertainment announced that 'members Johnny, Taeyong, Yuta, Doyoung, Jaehyun, Jungwoo, and Haechan have re-signed as NCT 127, on the basis of the solid teamwork they have built together and the deep trust they have developed with the company.'

SM Entertainment added, 'Since their debut on July 7, 2016, NCT 127 has established themselves as representative artists of K-pop over the course of 10 years, supported by the passionate love and support of their fans.' The agency further stated, 'We will continue to fully support NCT 127's activities and be a steadfast partner.'

NCT 127 is a multinational boy group that debuted in 2016 and has received great love through numerous hit songs including 'Kick It,' 'Fact Check,' and 'Cherry Bomb.'

Below is the full statement from SM Entertainment. Hello, this is SM Entertainment. We would like to sincerely thank everyone who has always sent unwavering love and support to NCT 127.

We are pleased to announce that members Johnny, Taeyong, Yuta, Doyoung, Jaehyun, Jungwoo, and Haechan have all re-signed as NCT 127, on the basis of the solid teamwork they have built and the deep trust they have developed with our company.

NCT 127 has established themselves as representative artists of K-pop over 10 years since their debut on July 7, 2016, through the passionate support and love of their fans. We would like to once again thank the members and fans who have shared in that brilliant journey. We will continue to fully support NCT 127's activities and be a steadfast partner. We ask for your continued interest in and love for NCT 127. Thank you.
