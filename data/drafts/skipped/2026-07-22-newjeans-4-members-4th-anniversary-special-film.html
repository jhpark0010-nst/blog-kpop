NewJeans has effectively confirmed their return to the K-pop scene as a four-member group including Minji. On July 22, 2026 — their 4th debut anniversary — NewJeans released a special film on the group's official YouTube channel. It marks the first content created together by all four members (Minji, Hanni, Haerin, and Hyein) in approximately two years, since the Japanese debut single 'Supernatural' released in the summer of 2024.

The video shows the members gathering one by one on a cool moonlit summer night, starting with Minji. They smile brightly, hold each other's hands, and gaze together in the same direction. NewJeans' signature warm and natural atmosphere, along with their signature dreamy vibe, remains very much intact.

Four individual member films were also released alongside the group video. Each clip captures small moments from the daily lives of Minji, Hanni, Haerin, and Hyein. Throughout the footage, the members' unwavering affection for the Bunnies (the group's fandom name) shines through, bringing a smile to viewers' faces.

In her solo film, Minji drives a pickup truck and, after stopping at a gas station, finds a flyer and folds it into the shape of a rabbit. Hanni hums a song under warm sunlight, and a cute rabbit can be seen drawn on the MIDI screen she is working on. Haerin wakes up and heads out carrying a rabbit-shaped bag she made herself; the moment she puts in her earphones, a rabbit-inspired MIDI art piece appears on the playback screen. Hyein stops in front of an ice cream shop called 'The Bunnies Scoop' on her way to go roller skating, and her face is full of excitement as she orders a rabbit-shaped ice cream.

NewJeans plans to mark the meaningful 4th anniversary with the special film and follow-up content. ADOR stated, 'We gathered the members' wish to celebrate their debut anniversary together with fans who have been waiting for a long time,' adding, 'We hope the Bunnies will enjoy it with a warm and welcoming heart.'

NewJeans debuted on July 22, 2022, and went on to set numerous records on major domestic and international charts, regularly appearing on the US Billboard Hot 100, Billboard 200, and the UK Official Charts. The group has not released new music since the Japanese single 'Supernatural' in June 2024.
