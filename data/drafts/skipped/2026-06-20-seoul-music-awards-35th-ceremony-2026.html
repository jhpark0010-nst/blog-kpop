The 35th Seoul Music Awards is being held today, June 20, at 6 p.m. at Inspire Arena in Jung-gu, Incheon. Since last year, the ceremony has shifted its schedule to June, moving beyond its traditional year-end recap role to create a festival atmosphere during one of the most dynamic periods for K-pop trends.

The artist lineup joining the 35th anniversary celebration is impressive, with a total of 20 acts performing. In order of debut, they are: Kwon Eunbi, Dayoung, ATEEZ, Lee Chanwon, Han Roro, LE SSERAFIM, Psycus, BOYNEXTDOOR, ZEROBASEONE, Dragon Pony, Say My Name, Izna, Kiki, Hearts2Hearts, Close Your Eyes, Ahop, Identity, I Did It, Alpha Drive One, and Modisay.

The presenter and MC lineup is equally varied. Shim Eun-kyung, Kwon Nara, Park Ji-hwan, Ryu Kyung-soo, Song Joong-ki, Lee Yu-bi, Arin, Ha Yoon-kyung, Kim Do-hoon, Jo Yu-ri, Kim Jae-won, Ko Yoon-jung, and Heo Nam-jun (listed in order of debut) will be on hand to add to the occasion. Super Junior's Leeteuk, ZEROBASEONE's Park Gunwook, and Kiki's Lee Sol will serve as MCs.

The ceremony can be watched in real time after purchasing a ticket through HelloLive.
