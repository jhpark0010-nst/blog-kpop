Tiffany Young is celebrating double good news: following her marriage to actor Byun Yo-han, she has now announced the release of her very first solo full-length album. On July 13, she shared a schedule poster for her debut solo studio album 'Edge of Calm' via her official social media, revealing a comeback set for August 20.

The poster features the 'Edge of Calm' logo alongside a restrained, composed atmosphere from Tiffany Young herself. Her deep, expressive gaze paired with refined styling hints at the emotional sensibility the new album will bring.

'Edge of Calm' is described as a work capturing the subtle tension and emotional balance that exists just before stillness. The message of 'Edge' — symbolizing tension and immersion — and 'Calm' — meaning tranquility — has been woven throughout the album's sound and visuals.

Notably, Tiffany Young participated directly in the overall production of this album, including concept planning, styling, visual directing, and music video story development, showcasing her full range of abilities as an artist.
