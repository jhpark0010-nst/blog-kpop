BTS will hold an offline event called 'BTS The City ARIRANG' (hereafter 'The City') in London to celebrate their ARIRANG world tour concerts at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium on July 6 and 7 (local time). Big Hit Music announced the event on June 29.

The main highlight of 'The City' kicks off on July 6, the first day of the London concerts, with a landmark lighting ceremony. The London Eye — one of London's most iconic landmarks — will be illuminated in a striking red, the key color of the ARIRANG tour. On the same day, a large boat carrying an ARIRANG logo sculpture will sail along the River Thames.

From July 8 to 10, a special BTS-themed media art exhibition will take place at Outernet London, a multi-purpose cultural venue in the city. An oversized digital LED canvas will be filled with ARIRANG visuals, promising a visually immersive experience for attendees.

In addition, various programs have been prepared for fans, including an '8 Stamp Rally' where participants visit key locations around London, complete missions, and verify their visits.
