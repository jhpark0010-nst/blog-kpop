ZEROBASEONE is launching physical album distribution in the United States. The group officially releases their sixth mini album, 'Ascend-', in the US on June 26 (local time).

'Ascend-' is an album that condenses ZEROBASEONE's musical journey, carrying a message of sharpened identity and unstoppable growth. The album will be stocked at major US retail chains including Target, Barnes & Noble, and Walmart.

ZEROBASEONE is a boy group formed through Mnet's 'Boys Planet.' After wrapping up their project activities with an encore concert in March, the group was reorganized from a nine-member lineup to a five-member unit. 'Ascend-' is the first album released since the transition to the five-member formation in May.
