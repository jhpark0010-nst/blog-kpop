The special collaboration single uniting three HYBE girl groups has achieved strong results on the U.S. Billboard main chart for two consecutive weeks. According to the latest Billboard chart dated July 4, released on June 30, 'ICONIC BY MISTAKE' by LE SSERAFIM, ILLIT, and KATSEYE debuted at #38 last week and now sits at #43 on the main song chart, the Hot 100, marking back-to-back appearances in the upper tier of the chart.

The song has been praised for capturing both global fandom support and mainstream appeal, despite being released as a digital single with no physical album. The track's performance is seen as a testament to the reach of all three groups combined.

The upward momentum on global charts is also notable. 'ICONIC BY MISTAKE' climbed two spots from the previous week to reach #23 on the Billboard Global 200, which is based on data from over 200 countries and regions, setting a new personal best for the track. It also landed at #24 on the Billboard Global Excl. U.S. chart. The song had previously charted in 46 countries and regions on Spotify's Weekly Top Songs chart, spanning Asia, North America, South America, and Europe.

'ICONIC BY MISTAKE' has been recognized as a model example of a girl group collaboration, blending the distinct personalities and talents of all three acts. Highlights include an intro that showcases each group's individual charm, a chorus featuring thrilling synchronized choreography, and lyrics that wittily capture the essence of contemporary pop culture. The music video swept both YouTube Trending Worldwide and the top spot on the trending music chart immediately upon release, and the hook choreography is rapidly spreading through short-form social media challenges.
