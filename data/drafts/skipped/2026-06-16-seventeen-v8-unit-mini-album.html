SEVENTEEN members The8 and Vernon have formed a new unit called V8 and will release a self-titled mini album on June 29, their agency Pledis Entertainment announced on June 16.

V8 is an album that expresses the wandering, confusion, and moments of recovery and growth found within those experiences — all framed around the theme of 'consumed youth.'

The album contains a total of 8 tracks: the title track 'singasong', 'Friend', 'BEAT', 'Mia', 'Coloring', 'girlsnboys', '8DM', and 'rat race', which will be released as a digital-only track.

'singasong' is a hyperpop-based electronic track co-written by the two members and German DJ and music producer Mekatek. The album track 'girlsnboys' is a song with composition, lyrics, and production by Pharrell Williams, a 14-time Grammy Award winner. Singer-producer Bumzu, who has worked with SEVENTEEN for a long time, also participated in the track.

This marks the sixth time SEVENTEEN has released an album as a unit, following Buseoksoon, Jeonghan x Wonwoo, Hoshi x Woozi, S.Coups x Mingyu, and Dokyeom x Seungkwan.

V8 will hold a solo concert on July 11–12 at Hall 1 of KINTEX Exhibition Hall 1 in Goyang, Gyeonggi Province — the first SEVENTEEN unit concert to be held in a standing format. They will also perform in Hong Kong from July 17 to 19.
