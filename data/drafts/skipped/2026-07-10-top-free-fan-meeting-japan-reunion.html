Former BIGBANG member T.O.P had a meaningful reunion with approximately 10,000 fans in Japan. On July 9, he held a fan meeting titled 'REUNION' at PIA ARENA MM in Yokohama, Japan.

The fan meeting was held as a fully free, invitation-only event for members of his official fan community 'TOPSX.' Around 10,000 fans filled the venue, showing their overwhelming support for T.O.P.

T.O.P opened the event by reading a handwritten letter to the fans. He spoke candidly about the past ten years, touching on moments of focusing on music production, his appearance in the Netflix series 'Squid Game,' and his challenge of creating mukbang (eating show) content on YouTube.

He also explained why he chose to hold the 10,000-person fan meeting for free. 'I thought long and hard about how to fill our first reunion in ten years,' he said. 'I wanted to sincerely repay the Japanese fans who quietly waited for me the longest, with a pure heart.'

T.O.P also shared news about new music. He gave the world premiere of the character music video for 'After the Play Ends,' a track from his first full-length album 'ANOTHER DIMENSION,' released in April. He also made a surprise announcement that his next album is already finished, drawing cheers from the crowd.

Fans dressed in red — T.O.P's official signature color — as a dress code and surprised him with a secretly prepared video message. In the latter half of the event, T.O.P walked through the audience seats himself, making eye contact and sharing a close moment with the fans who had waited for him. After the fan meeting, he said, 'I was so excited before going on stage, and now that I've seen you all in person, I want to meet you even more on stage.' He added, 'I will meet you with even better performances going forward.' T.O.P will next kick off his brand tour 'T.O.P PRE-STUDIO 2026' starting in Hong Kong on August 15, where he will meet fans around the world.
