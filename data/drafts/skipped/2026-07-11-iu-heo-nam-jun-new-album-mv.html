IU and actor Heo Nam-jun are set to reunite on screen. According to MyDaily's reporting on July 11, Heo Nam-jun has already confirmed his appearance in the music video for the title track of IU's new album, which is scheduled for release in September.

Filming for the music video is planned for early next month. The two have previously worked together — they appeared in the music video for 'Never Ending Story,' the title track of IU's third remake album 'Flower Bookmark 3,' released in May of last year.

The 'Never Ending Story' music video was produced as a homage to the film '8 Months of Christmas.' IU recreated the emotional performance of Shim Eun-ha from the original, while Heo Nam-jun channeled the spirit of Han Seok-kyu. The upcoming title track MV for the September album will continue along that same emotional thread, picking up the story where 'Never Ending Story' left off.

Reflecting on the filming of that music video, Heo Nam-jun previously shared: 'Every single day leading up to the shoot felt like a party.' He also said: 'Just knowing that someone like IU knows I exist in this world — I felt there was nothing more I could ask for.'

Despite a packed schedule, Heo Nam-jun made time to respond to IU's call. Following the success of his drama 'Brave New World,' he moved straight into his next project 'Gorae-byeol,' and still answered IU's invitation once again. IU is set to release the new album ahead of her concert at Goyang Stadium in September.
