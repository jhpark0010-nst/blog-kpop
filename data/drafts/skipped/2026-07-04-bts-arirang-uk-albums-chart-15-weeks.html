BTS's fifth full-length album ARIRANG has continued its strong run on the UK Official Albums Chart Top 100, climbing two spots from the previous week to land at No. 37. According to the UK Official Chart dated July 3 (local time), the album has now appeared on the chart for 15 consecutive weeks since debuting at No. 1 upon its release in March 2026.

On the UK Official Singles Chart, several K-pop girl group tracks made a notable showing. 'ICONIC BY MISTAKE,' a collaboration by HYBE label girl groups LE SSERAFIM, ILLIT, and KATSEYE, entered the chart for the third consecutive week at No. 44.

The original soundtrack 'Golden' from the Netflix animated film 'K-Pop Demon Hunters' charted at No. 51, while KATSEYE's 'Pinky Up' came in at No. 85.
