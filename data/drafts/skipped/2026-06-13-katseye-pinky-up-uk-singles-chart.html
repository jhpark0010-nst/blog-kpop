K-pop acts continued their strong run on the UK Official Singles and Albums Charts Top 100, with multiple tracks and albums maintaining long-term chart presence as of June 12 (local time).

The OST 'Golden' from the Netflix animated series 'K-pop Demon Hunters,' performed by the Huntrix vocalists, entered the UK Official Singles Chart Top 100 at No. 47, marking its 51st consecutive week on the chart.

KATSEYE, the Korean-American girl group formed through a collaboration between HYBE and Geffen Records, charted at No. 56 with their single 'PINKY UP,' notching their 9th consecutive week on the chart.

On the UK Official Albums Chart Top 100, BTS's fifth studio album 'ARIRANG' held at No. 37 for its 12th consecutive week.

Meanwhile, Michael Jackson placed four songs on the Singles Chart Top 100: 'Billie Jean' at No. 5, 'Beat It' at No. 8, 'Human Nature' at No. 15, and 'I Want You Back' by The Jackson 5 at No. 39. He also placed four albums on the Albums Chart Top 100: 'The Essential' at No. 2, 'Thriller' at No. 6, 'Bad' at No. 11, and 'Off The Wall' at No. 74, demonstrating his enduring popularity.
