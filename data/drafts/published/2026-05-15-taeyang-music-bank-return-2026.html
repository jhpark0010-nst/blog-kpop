BIGBANG's Taeyang is set to appear on KBS 2TV's music show Music Bank for the first time in 11 years. According to his agency The Black Label on May 15, Taeyang will take the stage on the May 22 broadcast of Music Bank to perform 'LIVE FAST DIE SLOW', the title track of his fourth full-length album QUINTESSENCE, which releases on May 18.

The last time Taeyang performed on Music Bank was in 2015 with BIGBANG's 'LOSER' — marking an 11-year gap. As a solo artist, his previous Music Bank appearance dates back even further, to 'I Need A Girl' in 2010, making this return 16 years in the making. Notably, both 'LOSER' and 'I Need A Girl' had each reached number one on Music Bank at the time.

In separate news, SEVENTEEN unit Dokyeom x Seungkwan has added two extra concerts in Taiwan. Pledis Entertainment announced that the duo will hold additional shows at Kaohsiung Arena on July 24 and 26.

Dokyeom x Seungkwan had originally been scheduled for just one performance on July 25, but sold out quickly, prompting the addition of two more dates.

The unit is also holding concerts in Macau on June 6 and 7. Those shows were likewise expanded from one to two performances and have both sold out.
