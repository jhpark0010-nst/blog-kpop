BTS's original content series 'Run BTS 2.0' will be released on April 23 at 9PM KST on the group's official YouTube channel and fan platform Weverse, Big Hit Music announced on April 21.

'Run BTS 2.0' captures everyday moments from BTS members on a trip together. Fans can look forward to seeing a different side of the members, including pillow fights and jokgu (a Korean foot volleyball game).

The original 'Run BTS' series first launched in 2015. According to the agency, the 155 regular episodes released through 2021 have accumulated 140 million views on YouTube.

BTS is set to kick off the North American leg of their world tour 'ARIRANG' at Raymond James Stadium in Tampa, USA, on April 25–26 and April 28 (local time).
