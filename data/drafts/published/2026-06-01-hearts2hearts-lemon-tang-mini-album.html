Hearts2Hearts is making their comeback after four months. The SM girl group is set to release their second mini album 'Lemon Tang' on June 22, and fans are eagerly watching to see whether the group can carry the sharp upward momentum of their spring single 'RUDE!' all the way through this coming summer.

Hearts2Hearts — consisting of members Carmen, Jiwoo, Yuha, Stella, Jooeun, Aina, Ian, and Yeon — will drop 'Lemon Tang' on June 22. The new album is composed of six tracks in total, including the title track of the same name and the single 'RUDE!', which was released back in February.

Just as the album title combines the tartness of 'Lemon' with 'Tang' — meaning a sharp, zesty taste and scent — the record captures Hearts2Hearts' fresh and refreshing charm, offering an energy that sparkles freely like a bright summer day.

With 'RUDE!', Hearts2Hearts proved their soaring popularity through a remarkable career-high run: topping various global charts, winning five music show (music broadcast) trophies, surpassing 100 million views on their first-ever music video, and becoming the first K-pop girl group song released this year to reach 100 million cumulative streams on Spotify, the world's largest music streaming platform.

'RUDE!' also sparked a widespread challenge trend at home and abroad, driven by its playful yet bold point choreography and member Stella's English narration segment, igniting what fans have dubbed the 'Hearts2Hearts boom.' The fact that the song still holds a top position on music charts three months after its release has only heightened anticipation for this new album, which promises to showcase an even sharper, more defined identity for the group.

Meanwhile, the physical album edition of Hearts2Hearts' second mini album 'Lemon Tang' will also be released on June 22.
