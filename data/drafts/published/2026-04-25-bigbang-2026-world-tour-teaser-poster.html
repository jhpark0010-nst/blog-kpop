BIGBANG has unveiled a teaser poster for their 20th debut anniversary world tour. On April 24, YG Entertainment posted the teaser poster on their official social media channels, announcing a world tour set to begin in August. The poster features the text 'BIGBANG 2026 WORLD TOUR.'

Specific details, including the official tour title, have not yet been disclosed.

BIGBANG previously drew significant attention when they took the stage at the 2026 Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival in the United States on April 12 and 19 (local time). The upcoming tour is also generating anticipation as it will be the group's first tour in nine years since 'LAST DANCE' in 2017.

YG Entertainment stated that preparations had already begun early, adding, 'All YG staff will do their best to create a perfect show.'
