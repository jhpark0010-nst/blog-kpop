BLACKPINK members Jisoo and Jennie lit up the global stage on the same day — April 23 (local time) — each showcasing a distinct charm in France and the United States respectively. Jisoo attended the opening ceremony of the '9th Cannes International Series Festival' in Cannes, France, where she received the 'Madame Figaro Rising Star Award.' On the same day, Jennie attended the '2026 TIME100 Gala' at Lincoln Center in New York City.

At the Cannes event, Jisoo drew attention in a lovely pink dress. She chose a romantic silhouette gown with an off-shoulder neckline, completing an elegant look with a bright smile and a neat half-updo hairstyle. Immediately after receiving her award, Jisoo expressed her feelings in French, saying 'Merci beaucoup (thank you),' drawing applause from the local audience.

The organizers introduced Jisoo on their official website as 'a figure who is expanding her filmography across various genres while pursuing both music and acting.' They also mentioned her appearance in the series 'Monthly Boyfriend,' praising her international influence and potential for artistic growth.

In a subsequent interview with local media, Jisoo said, 'I really did not expect this. I think the title of Rising Star Award will be a great source of strength for my future choices.' Regarding her acting career, she added, 'It was a dream I had nurtured since childhood, just like becoming a singer. I am grateful to be able to achieve both.' She also mentioned that she is currently working on a solo album.

Meanwhile in New York, Jennie commanded the red carpet in a striking black dress from French haute couture brand Schiaparelli. The experimental design featured a corset-style bodice, a see-through detail at the waist, and a velvet skirt, creating a mature yet bold atmosphere. An updo hairstyle and elaborate jewelry elevated the overall look. Jennie was invited to the event after being named to TIME magazine's list of the '100 Most Influential People in the World' in the Artists category — reportedly the only K-pop artist on the list. Singer-songwriter Gracie Abrams, who wrote Jennie's tribute, said, 'There is magic at the center of Jennie. She is a star with the power to draw people in wherever she is.'

In this way, the BLACKPINK members proved their global influence once again by attending events representing their respective fields in two different cities on the same day. Jisoo is making her presence felt as an actress, while Jennie continues to expand the group's brand value as an artist and fashion icon.
