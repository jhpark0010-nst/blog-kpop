BTS (RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, Jungkook) has set a new record in 2026, earning their 7th all-time No.1 on the Billboard Global (Excl. US) chart with their song 'SWIM.' The milestone was confirmed in Billboard's latest chart dated May 30, published on May 27.

According to the chart, 'SWIM' — the title track of BTS's fifth full-length album ARIRANG — claimed the top spot on the Billboard Global (Excl. US) for the third consecutive week.

BTS had already surpassed Taylor Swift's record last week to become the artist with the most No.1s on that chart in 2026. This week's ranking marks their 7th all-time No.1, breaking their own new record once again.

All 13 vocal tracks from the album remained on the chart for 9 consecutive weeks. 'SWIM' held at No.10 on the Global 200, maintaining a top-10 position for over two months, and landed at No.55 on the main Hot 100. The song also charted at No.16 on Adult Contemporary, No.12 on Pop Airplay, No.17 on Adult Pop Airplay, and climbed 3 spots to No.26 on Radio Songs compared to the previous week, demonstrating solid mainstream popularity in the United States.

ARIRANG reached No.10 on the Billboard 200 main album chart for the ninth consecutive week. The album also performed well on sub-charts, including No.4 on Top Album Sales and No.17 on Top Streaming Albums. The Vinyl Albums chart saw a 3-spot rebound to No.13.

Meanwhile, BTS placed four albums on the World Albums chart for the third consecutive week. Notably, 'Proof,' released in 2022, sat at No.4 and remained on the chart for an impressive 206 weeks. Also charting were the Japanese fourth studio album 'MAP OF THE SOUL: 7 ~THE JOURNEY~' (No.14), the third studio album 'LOVE YOURSELF 轉 Tear' (No.16), and the fifth mini album 'LOVE YOURSELF 承 Her' (No.25).
