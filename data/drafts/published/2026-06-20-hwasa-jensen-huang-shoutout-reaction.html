MAMAMOO's Hwasa has shared her feelings about receiving a shoutout from NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang. After unveiling a new song, MAMAMOO appeared on the June 19 broadcast of KBS 2TV's 'The Seasons — Sung Si-kyung's Eardrum Boyfriend.'

Host Sung Si-kyung addressed MAMAMOO after their performance, saying, 'I can't not bring this up. You're the hottest thing right now,' and mentioned that Hwasa had recently received a shoutout from NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang.

Hwasa responded with a laugh, saying, 'At first I thought it was AI. When I heard the news I was honored, but it didn't feel real. As time has gone on, the fact that he gave such a sincere shoutout makes me feel grateful every time I go to sleep.'

Jensen Huang had recently appeared on tvN's 'You Quiz on the Block,' where he said, 'I like Hwasa's music. I love Hwasa's music very much. It's incredibly charming.' He also added, ''Good Goodbye' is great, and I love the new album 'So Cute' too.'
