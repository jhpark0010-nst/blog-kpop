HYBE will introduce TUIDE, a new 7-member girl group, in the second half of 2026 through its newly established label ABD. ABD announced on July 20 that it has opened TUIDE's official social media channels and revealed the team's logo.

TUIDE is a girl group consisting of members Seohee, Seoyeon, Elena, Jia, Saki, Sea, and Ihaneui. The group's name is derived from 'Tune the Tide,' a phrase meaning 'to harmonize the flow.'

ABD stated, 'TUIDE's members will freely express their individual personalities while flexibly blending with one another to deliver a harmonious sound.'

Overall producing for TUIDE will be handled by Master Professional Han Seong-su, who has discovered and developed K-pop stars including SEVENTEEN, After School, IZ*ONE, and TWS.
