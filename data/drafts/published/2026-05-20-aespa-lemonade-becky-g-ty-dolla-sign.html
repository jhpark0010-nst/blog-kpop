aespa is showcasing standout chemistry with global artists on their upcoming second full album. Titled 'LEMONADE', the record drops on May 29 at 1 PM KST and features an all-star lineup of collaborators from around the world, including G-Dragon, Latin pop star Becky G, and Billboard chart-topping hip-hop artist Ty Dolla $ign.

G-Dragon previously joined aespa on the pre-release track 'WDA (Whole Different Animal)'. Now, Becky G and Ty Dolla $ign are also confirmed as featured artists on the full album, further elevating the project's global appeal.

'LEMONADE (Feat. Becky G)' will be available exclusively on the digital album. The track blends the aespa members' vocals with Becky G's powerful rap, creating an intense synergy that offers a distinct charm compared to the original version.

The electronic dance track 'Switchblade' features Ty Dolla $ign and boasts an addictive hook. Its lyrics draw a comparison between a switchblade and the ability to transform into different versions of oneself depending on the situation, capturing both flexibility and strength. Ty Dolla $ign's feature harmonizes with aespa's restrained vocal delivery.

aespa's second full album 'LEMONADE' contains a total of 11 tracks spanning a variety of genres, including the aespa-style dance tracks 'SHAKIN'', 'Bite', and 'Roll'.
