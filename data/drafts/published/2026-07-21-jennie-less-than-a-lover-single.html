BLACKPINK's Jennie will release her new solo single 'Less than a Lover' on July 24 at 1:00 PM KST, her agency OA Entertainment (Odd Atelier) announced on July 21. The label revealed the news through its official social media accounts, sharing a teaser image previewing the upcoming release.

'Less than a Lover' is a summer song that layers Jennie's deeper vocals over a languid texture created by lo-fi guitar and a vintage electric keyboard. The track draws attention for its distinctly different color compared to the high-energy performance-driven songs she has shown so far.

Jennie participated in writing the lyrics and co-directing the music video, and was actively involved in sharing her opinions throughout the overall music production process.

She had already previewed the song at major overseas music festivals, including the U.S. Governor's Ball Music Festival 2026, Denmark's Roskilde Festival, Poland's Open'er Festival, and Spain's 2026 Mad Cool Festival.

Jennie will continue her run of major international festival appearances next month, with slots at Lollapalooza Chicago in the U.S. and Summer Sonic 2026 in Japan.
