I.O.I (Im Nayoung, Chungha, Kim Sejeong, Jung Chaeyeon, Kim Sohye, Yoo Yeonjung, Choi Yoojung, Kim Doyeon, Jeon Somi) released the music video teaser for their title track 'Gabzagi' from their third mini album 'I.O.I : LOOP' via official SNS channels at midnight on May 6.

The teaser opens with a striking scene in which Jeon Somi approaches Kim Doyeon and plants an unexpected kiss, immediately drawing viewers in. The contrasting reactions — Jeon Somi bold and unhesitating, Kim Doyeon visibly flustered — leave a powerful impression. The short, film-like sequence heightens the mysterious tension of the title track and amplifies curiosity about the sensory narrative to be told in the full music video.

The title track 'Gabzagi' is a synth-pop track that delicately captures the sudden waves of longing that wash over a person. Member Jeon Somi participated in writing the lyrics, conveying genuine emotion about time shared together and the tender feeling of a relationship reconnecting. Memories of the past rising up amid the rush of daily life seep deeply through the repeating lyrics, while a dreamy outro sound in the latter half is set to awaken long-dormant emotions and leave a lasting impression.

'I.O.I : LOOP' is a new album that will prove I.O.I's enduring presence. Members took a leading role in writing and composing lyrics for many of the tracks, pouring in the group's solid synergy and deepened musical capabilities. Global fans around the world are turning their attention to the strong album quality that the members — each having grown fiercely in their own fields — will create upon reuniting.

I.O.I, having raised comeback excitement to its peak with the bold teaser, will release their third mini album 'I.O.I : LOOP' across major online music platforms at 6 p.m. on May 19. Following that, from May 29 to 31, they will open the Asia tour '2026 I.O.I Concert Tour: LOOP' over three days at Seoul's Jamsil Indoor Stadium, sharing an emotional connection with fans.
