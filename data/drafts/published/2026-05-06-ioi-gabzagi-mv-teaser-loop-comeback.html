I.O.I has released a provocative music video teaser for their new title track. The group — consisting of Im Na-young, Chungha, Kim Sejeong, Jung Chaeyeon, Kim Sohye, Yoo Yeonjung, Choi Yoojung, Kim Doyeon, and Jeon Somi — posted the teaser for 'Gabzagi,' the title track of their third mini-album 'I.O.I : LOOP,' through their official channel on May 6.

The teaser opens with a striking scene in which Jeon Somi approaches Kim Doyeon and plants an unexpected kiss, immediately drawing viewers in. The contrasting expressions of a bold Jeon Somi and a seemingly flustered Kim Doyeon leave a powerful impression.

The short, film-like sequence amplifies the mysterious tension of the title track and heightens curiosity about the sensory narrative to be told in the full music video.

'Gabzagi' is a synth-pop track that delicately captures the sudden waves of longing. Member Jeon Somi participated in writing the lyrics, conveying the sincerity of time spent together and the tenderness of a reconnecting relationship. Memories from the past that surface amid the rush of daily life seep in through the repeating lyrics, while a dreamy outro sound in the latter half is set to awaken dormant emotions and leave a lasting impression.

'I.O.I : LOOP' is a new album that will demonstrate the group's enduring presence. Members took a leading role in writing and composing lyrics for many of the tracks, showcasing the team's solid synergy and deepened musical capabilities. Global fans around the world are paying close attention to the album quality that the members — each having grown fiercely in their own paths — will create upon reuniting.

Having raised comeback excitement to its peak with the bold teaser, I.O.I will release their third mini-album 'I.O.I : LOOP' on May 19 at 6 p.m. KST across major online music platforms. They will then kick off their Asian tour '2026 I.O.I Concert Tour: LOOP' at Seoul's Jamsil Indoor Stadium over three days from May 29 to 31, sharing an emotional connection with fans.
