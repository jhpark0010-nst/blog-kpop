MEOVV (수인, 가원, 안나, 나린, 엘라) is making bold strides in the K-pop scene. On June 8, The Black Label announced that the group's second EP, 'BITE NOW', released on June 1, recorded first-week (초동) sales of 301,700 copies.

This figure surpasses the first-week sales of their previous release, EP 1 'MY EYES OPEN VVIDE'. MEOVV achieved a career high in approximately one year, riding a strong upward momentum in the first week of their comeback.

Known for their trendy music and powerful performances, MEOVV showcased a more diverse range of concepts, genre-spanning new tracks, and a broader musical spectrum with this new album. The title track 'DDI RO RI' drew intense attention with its irresistible addictiveness and high-quality performance, and simultaneously claimed the No. 1 spot on YouTube Music Video Trending Worldwide upon release, capturing the attention of global K-pop fans.

The title track 'DDI RO RI' samples Bach's 'Toccata and Fugue in D minor', blending it with MEOVV's own musical color to create a song where classical elegance and freshness coexist. In this new track, MEOVV projects an unrivaled presence that commands attention from the moment they appear, expressing an instinctive and intense wildness.

The new album 'BITE NOW' contains a total of 5 tracks, including the title track 'DDI RO RI'.
