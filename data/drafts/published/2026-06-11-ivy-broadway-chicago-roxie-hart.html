Singer and musical actress IVY is set to take the Broadway stage. According to Shinsicompany on June 10, IVY will perform the role of Roxie Hart in the Broadway production of Chicago, the home of the musical form in the United States.

Chicago is the longest-running American musical in Broadway history. It swept six categories at the 1997 Tony Awards, including Best Musical Revival, and also won the Grammy Award for Best Musical Theater Album — a true legend of Broadway.

IVY first took on the role of Roxie Hart in the Korean production of Chicago in 2012, and has since completed 6 seasons and nearly 600 performances through 2024. Through consistent effort and self-discipline, she delivered her best performance each season. Last year, she received an audition invitation from the Broadway production, and after rigorous preparation, she has finally earned her place on the Broadway stage.

It is extremely rare for a musical actress active in Korea to be cast as a lead in a Broadway production. Moreover, since Chicago is the very work that helped IVY grow as a musical actress, the fact that 12 years of effort and dedication on the Korean stage are now bearing fruit on Broadway — the birthplace of the show — adds a particularly special sense of emotion.

IVY shared her feelings, saying: 'I am honored and happy to be taking the Broadway stage as Roxie Hart, my first lead role and the character I love most. Over the course of one year, there were three video auditions and a long period of waiting. That is how precious this opportunity is to me, and I also feel a great sense of responsibility. I will prepare thoroughly with the mindset of representing the Republic of Korea. I ask for your warm support and interest.'

Chicago is a work in which singing, choreography, and nuanced emotional expression are all essential. Accordingly, IVY is said to be undergoing intensive training with a focus on English dialogue and pronunciation as she prepares for the Broadway stage. Meanwhile, IVY debuted as a singer in 2005 with her first album 'My Sweet And Free Day' and went on to produce hit songs including 'Sonata of Temptation,' 'If This Is How It's Going to Be,' 'I Must Be a Fool,' 'A-HA,' and 'Goodbye Tears,' before becoming a musical actress.
