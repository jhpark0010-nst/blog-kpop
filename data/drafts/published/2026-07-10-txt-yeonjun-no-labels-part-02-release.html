TOMORROW X TOGETHER (TXT) member Yeonjun released his second solo mini album 'NO LABELS: PART 02' at 1 p.m. on July 10, according to his label Big Hit Music.

The album includes six tracks: title track 'Ice Cream', 'Vanilla', 'Even If I'm a Little Clumsy, Again', 'No More Disco', and 'Long Way Long Ride'.

Yeonjun participated in creating the choreography for 'Ice Cream' and contributed to the lyrics of 'Even If I'm a Little Clumsy, Again' and 'Long Way Long Ride'. 'Ice Cream' is a summer song depicting a relationship where two people are sweet to each other but maintain a certain distance.

Yeonjun said, 'I felt I made the right choice selecting Ice Cream as the title track, because it's the song that best reflects my color in this album.' He added, 'It's a song that can show both my outer and inner self.'

He also said, 'Just like my previous work, I show myself as I am, but this time I wanted to capture more diverse music and a more relaxed, chill side of me.'

The day before the release, Yeonjun held an album launch party in Mapo-gu, Seoul, where he performed his new songs for fans for the first time. He is also set to appear on ABC's 'Good Morning America' Summer Concert Series on August 7 (local time) for a solo performance. Yeonjun said, 'Rather than setting grand goals, I hope to grow just one step further,' and added, 'Through this album, I want to become more honest with myself and more true to who I am.'
