TXT member Yeonjun will perform at Z100's Summer Bash Presented by Wells Fargo on August 6 (local time) at Hudson Yards in New York, label Big Hit Music announced on July 14. He will be the first Korean artist ever to appear at the event.

Z100's Summer Bash is an outdoor concert hosted by Z100, a radio broadcaster under iHeartMedia. In addition to Yeonjun, pop stars including Charlie Puth and Bella Kay are also set to take the stage.

Yeonjun is currently promoting his second solo mini-album, 'NO LABELS: PART 02', which was released on July 10.

In other music news, four-member band Geukdong Asia Tigers will release a remake of No Brain's 'Bioneun Bame Hongdae' (Rainy Night in Hongdae) at 6 p.m. on July 14. According to No Brain's label Rockstar Music and Live, the song is a tribute to Hongdae as a symbol of indie culture. The release is part of No Brain's 30th debut anniversary project.

Geukdong Asia Tigers said, 'We dreamed of music watching No Brain, so we are deeply honored to take part in this remake,' adding, 'We will continue to quietly follow that path.'

Singer Tiffany Young will release her first solo full-length album, 'Edge of Calm', on August 20, her label Pacific Music Group announced. Tiffany Young revealed the release schedule poster through her official SNS the previous day. The album captures the subtle tension, focus, and emotional balance at the moment just before calm — with 'Edge' symbolizing tension and immersion, and 'Calm' representing emotional balance and serenity. Tiffany Young participated in the overall concept planning, styling, visual directing, and music video story work for the album. She debuted with girl group Girls' Generation in 2007 and has been active as both a singer and actress. In 2016, she also stepped into the solo music scene with 'I Just Wanna Dance'.
