NCT 127 is making their return. According to MyDaily reporting on July 7, NCT 127 will release their seventh full-length studio album on August 24. This marks a comeback of approximately two years since their sixth full album 'Ppiggeodeok (Walk)', which was released in July 2024.

Five members will participate in the new album: Johnny, Taeyong, Yuta, Jaehyun, and Haechan.

NCT 127 entered a hiatus from group activities beginning in April 2024, when leader Taeyong enlisted in the military. Taeyong subsequently completed his service with the Navy Music Honor Guard's public relations unit in December of last year, and Jaehyun was also fully discharged from the Army Music Corps in May. Doyoung and Jungwoo enlisted in December of last year and are currently still serving.

Since their debut in 2016, NCT 127 has built an identity around the concept of 'NEO,' showcasing intense performances and a distinctive musical style. Their previous release, the sixth full album 'Ppiggeodeok (Walk)', achieved seven consecutive million-seller records and earned them a triple crown on music broadcast programs (music shows).

As NCT 127 returns with a new full album in the year of their 10th debut anniversary, attention is gathering on whether they will once again solidify their own unique musical world.
