BLACKPINK's Jennie performed as the only K-pop artist to headline the main stage at the 2026 MAD COOL FESTIVAL, a major music festival held in Madrid, Spain on July 9 (local time), according to her agency OA Entertainment.

Jennie delivered high-energy performances of 'Mantra,' 'ExtraL,' and 'like JENNIE,' alongside more emotional tracks including 'Handlebars' and 'Seoul City.'

Mad Cool Festival is celebrating its 10th anniversary this year. Jennie appeared on the lineup alongside prominent pop stars such as Charlie Puth and Zara Larsson.

Jennie had previously headlined Denmark's Roskilde Festival and Poland's Open'er Festival, making Mad Cool Festival the third consecutive major European music festival where she has taken the stage.

She is also scheduled to perform at Lollapalooza Chicago in the United States and SUMMER SONIC 2026 in Japan.
