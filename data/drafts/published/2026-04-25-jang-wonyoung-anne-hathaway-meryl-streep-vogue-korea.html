Hollywood actresses Meryl Streep and Anne Hathaway were captivated by the one-of-a-kind aura of K-pop icon Jang Wonyoung. On April 24, a special conversation between the two acclaimed actresses — who star together in 'The Devil Wears Prada 2' — and Jang Wonyoung was revealed through Vogue Korea's official video content.

During the interview, Jang Wonyoung led the atmosphere with her fluent English and polished manner, showcasing her full ability as an interviewer. In particular, both actresses showered praise on Jang Wonyoung's almost unreal visuals. After seeing her photo shoot, Anne Hathaway exclaimed, 'Oh my, she's so beautiful,' while Meryl Streep also praised her, saying, 'She looks like a single flower in bloom.'

The warm atmosphere continued from the very first meeting. When Jang Wonyoung greeted them in fluent English, Anne Hathaway responded, 'Nice to meet you, your outfit is so cute.' She also referenced a character from the film, saying Jang Wonyoung looked just like 'Andy Sachs' that day, showing a sense of closeness.

Anne Hathaway was also visibly moved by Jang Wonyoung's effort to break the language barrier. She said, 'It must not be easy to do an interview in English, and I really appreciate it,' adding, 'We haven't had the chance to learn Korean yet,' conveying both gratitude and a sense of apology. Jang Wonyoung responded by confessing her deep love for the film.

Recalling the iconic transformation scene from the original movie, Jang Wonyoung said, 'Do you remember the walking-to-work scene in the first film? The one where the outfit changes with every cut. I was so influenced by that scene that I actually started wearing a different outfit every day afterward — as if I had become Andy.' Her words drew smiles from both actresses.

When it was revealed that Jang Wonyoung, born in 2004, was only two years old when the original film was released, both actresses were visibly stunned and expressed amazement at the connection that transcended generations. Through this meeting, Jang Wonyoung once again proved her standing as a global star, combining outstanding hosting skills with her flower-like beauty.
