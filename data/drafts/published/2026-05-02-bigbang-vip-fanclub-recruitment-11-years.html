BIGBANG is recruiting members for its official fan club V.I.P for the first time in 11 years, ahead of the group's 20th debut anniversary comeback. YG Entertainment announced this on May 2, 2026.

BIGBANG revealed a poster on its official SNS at 8:19 PM the previous day, previewing the recruitment of V.I.P 6th generation members. This marks the first official fan club recruitment since the 5th generation was opened in 2015, an 11-year gap.

The released poster features the 'BaengBong' — BIGBANG's official light stick — which symbolizes the bond between BIGBANG and their fans.

The 6th generation fan club recruitment will be conducted through BIGBANG's official community on the fan platform Weverse... Correction: through BIGBANG's official community on the fan platform Vstar (b-stage).

YG stated, 'We prepared the official fan club recruitment in order to breathe more closely with the fans who have protected BIGBANG's name for so long.' They added, 'Please look forward to the new 20th anniversary journey that the artist and V.I.P will write together.'

BIGBANG recently took the stage at the '2026 Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival' in the United States, where they announced, 'We have finished preparing a new album. We are also planning to start a world tour from August.'
