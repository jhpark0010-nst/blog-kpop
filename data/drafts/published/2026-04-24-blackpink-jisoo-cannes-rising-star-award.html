BLACKPINK member and actress Jisoo won the Madame Figaro Rising Star Award at the 9th Cannes International Series Festival. The award was presented at the festival's opening ceremony held in Cannes, France on April 23 (local time).

Jisoo took the stage wearing a pink dress and, after receiving the award, shared her feelings with a bright smile, saying 'Merci beaucoup (thank you)' in French.

The organizers introduced Jisoo on their official website, noting that 'she is focused on expanding her filmography through works across various genres and formats, while pursuing both music and acting activities.'

The organizers went on to mention her appearance in the Netflix original series 'Monthly Boyfriend' and explained their reason for selecting her, describing her as 'a promising figure drawing attention not only in Korea but in the global entertainment industry, for her international influence, presence, and artistic growth.'
