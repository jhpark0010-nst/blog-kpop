ILLIT (Yunah, Minju, Moka, Wonhee, Iroha) has surpassed 100 million views with the music video for 'It's Me.' The MV crossed the 100 million mark at approximately 3:14 PM on July 18, making it the group's second music video to reach that milestone.

The achievement came 79 days after the music video was released on April 30. This is a faster pace than ILLIT's debut track 'Magnetic,' which was the group's first MV to surpass 100 million views. 'It's Me' continues to rank in the upper tier of YouTube's Global Top Music Videos chart, which tracks the most-watched music videos worldwide.

'It's Me' is a techno-genre track in which the narrator boldly declares 'I'm your favorite!' at the moment when uncertainty about defining a relationship deepens after a first date. The song has received praise for its exciting, addictive melody and intense full-power performance that delivers a thrilling rush. The music video captures ILLIT in quirky, all-out moments as they struggle to get the answer they want.

'It's Me' is the title track of ILLIT's fourth mini-album 'MAMIHLAPINATAPAI.' ILLIT is set to release their second Japanese single, 'I Got Your Back,' on July 26.
