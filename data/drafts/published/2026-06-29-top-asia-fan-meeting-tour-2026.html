T.O.P is set to connect with fans across Asia through his first-ever solo Asia fan meeting tour. According to his agency TOPSPOT PICTURES on June 29, T.O.P will hold 'T.O.P PRE-STUDIO 2026', a special fan meeting tour that will bring him together with fans around the world.

The teaser poster unveiled for the tour depicts a silhouette moving toward a single beam of light in darkness, rendered in a sensory monotone style. The title text 'T.O.P PRE-STUDIO 2026' is set against a rough-textured backdrop, visually capturing T.O.P's distinctive charisma and heightening anticipation for the upcoming tour.

This tour carries special significance as it marks T.O.P's first Asia tour in 20 years since his debut in the entertainment industry in 2006. Having proven his enduring influence by topping major charts worldwide with his first solo full-length album 'ANOTHER DIMENSION' (다중관점), all eyes are on what kind of musical connection he will share with fans in each country.

The tour kicks off on July 9 at Pia Arena MM in Yokohama, Japan, where T.O.P will host a fully free invitation event for 10,000 seats, open exclusively to members of TOPSX, his official Japanese fan community. T.O.P plans to use this occasion as a starting point to actively expand his connection with fans around the world.

Specific cities, detailed schedules, and ticketing information for 'T.O.P PRE-STUDIO 2026' will be announced sequentially through official platforms at a later date.

T.O.P debuted with BIGBANG in 2006. In 2017, he was indicted on charges of marijuana use and received a sentence of 10 months in prison with a two-year suspended sentence. He officially departed from BIGBANG in 2023.
