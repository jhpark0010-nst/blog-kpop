BLACKPINK's Jennie has been generating major buzz ahead of her new solo single 'Less than a Lover', set for release on July 24, by showcasing sizzling on-screen chemistry with her music video co-star — Taiwanese model Kai — in a pair of videos that look just like a real-life couple moment.

On July 23, Jennie posted two videos to her personal account. The clips appear to have been filmed during the music video shoot for 'Less than a Lover'. Male model Kai, who had already made his presence known through earlier teaser content, appears alongside Jennie throughout both videos. In one scene, the two sit side by side on a beach, gazing out at the ocean as the sun sets, enjoying a relaxed, unhurried moment together.

Jennie peels an orange and hands it to Kai, who naturally takes a bite — a warm, affectionate exchange that had many viewers comparing the pair to a real couple.

In the second video posted to her account, the two get even closer. Kai joins Jennie aboard a classic yacht on the open blue sea and wraps his arms around her from behind in a tight embrace. The scene that follows — Jennie leaning into him, facing the camera as the sea breeze blows, looking every bit like a movie still — only heightened anticipation for the full music video.

OA Entertainment had previously announced the release of Jennie's new solo single 'Less than a Lover' on July 21 via their official account, along with a teaser poster. At the time, the identity of the man whose face was hidden behind a heart sticker sparked widespread curiosity, and it was later confirmed to be Kai, a Taiwanese model currently active in South Korea.

Jennie is a member of BLACKPINK, known for hit songs such as 'DDU-DU DDU-DU' and 'How You Like That'. As a solo artist, she has previously released 'SOLO' and her first full-length album 'Ruby'. Her new single 'Less than a Lover' dropped at 1:00 PM on July 24.
