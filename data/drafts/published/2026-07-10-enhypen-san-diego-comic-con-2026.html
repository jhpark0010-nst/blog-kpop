ENHYPEN will attend San Diego Comic-Con 2026 (SDCC 2026), the world's largest pop culture festival, held at the San Diego Convention Center in California from July 23 to 26 (local time). Their label Belift Lab announced the news on July 10.

The group will visit the exclusive 'Dark Moon' booth on July 23 and 24, participating in a large-scale panel talk, a solo performance, and various other events where they will meet fans.

'Dark Moon' is a story intellectual property (IP) originally planned and developed by HYBE. Works created in collaboration with ENHYPEN include 'Dark Moon: The Blood Altar,' 'Dark Moon: Two Moons,' and 'Children of Bamfield by Dark Moon,' all of which have been released to date.

The 'dark fantasy' narrative centered on vampires connects with ENHYPEN's music, deepening immersion and creating synergy between the two.

On July 23, ENHYPEN members are set to discuss vampires — a central theme running through both ENHYPEN and 'Dark Moon' — alongside Twilight director Catherine Hardwicke, actors Peter Facinelli and Ashley Greene, and renowned critic Damian Holbrook.

Prior to attending SDCC 2026, ENHYPEN will perform their world tour concert at Snapdragon Stadium in San Diego on July 21.
