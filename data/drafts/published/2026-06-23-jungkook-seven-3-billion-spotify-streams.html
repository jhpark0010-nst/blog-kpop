BTS member Jungkook's solo single 'Seven' has surpassed 3 billion streams on Spotify, the world's largest music streaming platform, his label Big Hit Music announced on June 23. This makes Jungkook the first Korean artist ever to reach 3 billion streams on a single track.

Additionally, 'Seven' is the only song released worldwide in 2023 to have achieved this milestone.

'Seven' is a passionate serenade about wanting to be with a loved one every single day. The track blends a catchy melody, warm acoustic guitar sounds, and the rhythms of UK garage — an electronic music genre that originated in early-1990s Britain.

The song's music video surpassed 600 million views on YouTube in April of this year.

Since its release, 'Seven' has achieved worldwide popularity, reaching No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 main singles chart in the United States and No. 3 on the UK Official Singles Chart Top 100. It also entered Spotify's Weekly Top Songs Global chart for 153 consecutive weeks and Billboard's Global (excluding the US) chart for 152 consecutive weeks, setting the longest-running record among Korean solo artists on both charts.

Jungkook's group BTS is currently conducting the ARIRANG world tour, the largest-scale K-pop world tour to date.
