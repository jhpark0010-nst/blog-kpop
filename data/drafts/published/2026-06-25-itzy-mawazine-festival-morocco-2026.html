ITZY headlined the Mawazine Festival in Rabat, Morocco on June 23, 2026, according to an announcement by JYP Entertainment on June 25. The world-renowned festival also featured performances by Tiësto, Nicky Jam, and Rema.

ITZY opened their set with 'TUNNEL VISION' and went on to perform 18 songs over 85 minutes, including 'Motto,' released in May, and the viral hit 'THAT'S A NO NO.'

JYP Entertainment reported that the crowd responded with enthusiastic cheers and mass sing-alongs throughout the performance.

ITZY is currently in the middle of their third world tour, also titled 'TUNNEL VISION.' Upcoming tour dates include Kaohsiung, Taiwan on June 27–28, Bangkok, Thailand on July 4, and London, UK on September 11.
