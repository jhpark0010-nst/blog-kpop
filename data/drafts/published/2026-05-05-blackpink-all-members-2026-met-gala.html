Photos from BLACKPINK's appearance at the 2026 Met Gala have been released. All four members — Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa — attended the event together, marking the first time the full group has been present at the Met Gala at the same time.

Babita Mandava posted a bathroom mirror selfie on her SNS on May 5 (Korean time) with the caption 'Felt cute, might delete later.' The photo showed BLACKPINK's Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa posing alongside actress Rachel Sennott. Dressed in glamorous gowns, the group enjoyed the moment with playful expressions.

This is the first time all BLACKPINK members have attended the Met Gala together, and the full-group photo has been drawing significant attention. Each member expressed their individual style through outfits from the global fashion brands they serve as ambassadors for.

On the same day, the official Met Gala SNS account also posted a photo featuring all four BLACKPINK members.

The Met Gala is a global fashion charity event that began in 1948 and is held every year on the first Monday of May at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York. Because phone use is restricted at the event, bathroom selfies have become an unofficial tradition.

This year's event was held under the theme 'Fashion is Art' and was attended by Anna Wintour, Beyoncé, Nicole Kidman, and Venus Williams, among others. In addition to BLACKPINK, a number of Korean artists were also present, including Lee Jae, aespa's Karina and NingNing, actor Ahn Hyo-seop, and model Jung Ho-yeon.
