KATSEYE, the Korea-U.S. joint girl group under HYBE, will kick off their new concert tour, THE WILDWORLD TOUR, on September 1 (local time) in Dublin, Ireland. Their label HYBE-Geffen Records announced the news on May 14.

Following the Dublin opener, KATSEYE will visit major cities across Europe and North America, including London and Manchester in the UK, Paris, Amsterdam, Cologne, as well as Miami, Atlanta, Washington D.C., Los Angeles, and Mexico City. In total, the tour spans 27 cities across 10 countries.

The new tour follows the release of their third mini-album WILD, set to drop in August.

The label stated, 'The scale of the performances has expanded significantly compared to their first solo tour.' They added that KATSEYE previously sold out all 16 shows of their tour 'The BEAUTIFUL CHAOS,' which visited 13 cities in North America from November to December last year.

KATSEYE is also set to perform at the American Music Awards, taking place on May 25 at the MGM Grand Garden Arena in Las Vegas. The group has been nominated in three categories at the ceremony: New Artist of the Year, Best Music Video, and Breakthrough Pop Artist.
