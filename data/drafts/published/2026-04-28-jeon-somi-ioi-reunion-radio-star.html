Jeon Somi is set to appear on MBC's Radio Star, airing Wednesday, April 29 at 10:30 p.m., to share behind-the-scenes stories from the I.O.I 10th anniversary reunion project, new song spoilers, and memories from her Produce 101 days. The episode is themed 'It's Me Who Reunited Again — We Rise Together!' and also features Lee Jong-hyuk, Yoo Sun, and Lee Dae-hwi.

Jeon Somi first gained public attention when she finished in first place on Produce 101 in 2016, debuting as center and the youngest member of I.O.I. After I.O.I concluded activities in 2017, she went on to build a career as a solo artist. On the show, she discusses the ongoing reunion project marking the group's 10th anniversary.

She reveals that the members' group chat is still active, and shares behind-the-scenes stories and the overall atmosphere of the reunion process, showing that the team's bond remains unchanged. She also talks about Chungha, who took the lead in organizing the reunion.

Jeon Somi also shares that she has already completed album jacket photography and recording, and is now waiting for her full comeback 'seal to be lifted.' She honestly talks about practicing four to six hours a day and admits she felt the difference in stamina compared to ten years ago when the members got together again after a long time.

She also reveals an episode from the I.O.I days, when the members held a monthly dorm meeting and she fell asleep mid-meeting and was scolded by fellow member Kim Sejeong. Jeon Somi also describes herself as a maximalist and a fan of unique items, sharing that she bought 60 to 70 towels when she first started living alone and collected various items from abroad.

Additionally, she shares a surprise behind-the-scenes story about appearing as a reenactment actor on the show 'Surprise' to promote a new song. She also reveals that she was recently cast in a lead role in a Hollywood film and has finished filming, and that she auditioned in person during music show promotions and was cast for a shoot in Thailand.
