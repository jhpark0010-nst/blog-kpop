Former BIGBANG member T.O.P will hold a solo fan meeting in Japan, meeting approximately 10,000 local fans free of charge. His agency, Top Spot Pictures, officially announced on June 2 that the event will take place on July 9, 2026 at 6:00 PM at PIA ARENA MM in Yokohama, Japan.

T.O.P specially planned this fan meeting as a gift for fans who have continued to show him unwavering love and support. The event is open to members of his official paid Japanese membership, and a standout feature is that all seats will be offered as free invitations at the large arena venue, which can accommodate approximately 10,000 people.

The event will be hosted by Furuya Masayuki, known as a leading Korean Wave MC in Japan, who is expected to showcase great chemistry with T.O.P. T.O.P is currently putting the finishing touches on preparations, arranging a variety of programs and candid talk segments in special corners for local fans who have long awaited the chance to connect with him.

Ticket reservations for the fan meeting will open for official fan club members starting June 5, with priority pre-booking beginning then. Detailed schedules and ticketing information can be found through the official Japanese fan community.
