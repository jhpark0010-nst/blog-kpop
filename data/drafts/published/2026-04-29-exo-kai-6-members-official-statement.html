EXO's Kai made headlines online after directly stating the current number of EXO members on a broadcast. On the April 27 episode of SBS variety program 'Ani Geunde Jinjja!' (But Really Though!), Kai appeared on the show while fellow cast members expressed their desire to perform as guests at EXO's upcoming world tour concert.

Lee Sang-min declared, 'EXO's concert starts in April. We'll go on as guests then,' to which Kai waved his hand and replied, 'The venue is a secret. The concert might get cancelled.' Tak Jae-hoon joined in, saying, 'Kai, I don't usually ask for favors, but let me lean on you just this once,' and Lee Sang-min added, 'Just turn a blind eye for us.' Kai then joked, 'I'll say just one thing — my contract renewal season is coming up soon. I might not be Kai anymore, but become Keoi,' drawing laughter from everyone.

When Tak Jae-hoon asked, 'How many EXO members are there besides you? Don't you just need to convince the members?' Kai answered, 'Right now? Five (excluding me),' drawing immediate attention.

The remark quickly spread across online communities once it aired. EXO debuted in April 2012 as a 12-member group, but later reorganized into a 9-member group after three Chinese members departed. Subsequently, the sub-unit ChenBaekXi (Chen, Baekhyun, and Xiumin) became embroiled in a contract dispute with their agency and have been absent from group activities, leaving EXO effectively operating as a 6-member lineup — Suho, Chanyeol, Kai, D.O., Sehun, and Lay.

Kai's remark drew particular attention because he himself directly stated the number of currently active members. Online reactions included comments such as 'EXO officially confirmed it,' 'That's a relief,' 'Now it's officially 6,' and 'EXO is 6 members.'

Meanwhile, EXO held their 'EXO PLANET #6 – EXO'rDIUM in Seoul' concerts at KSPO Dome in Seoul Olympic Park from the 10th to the 12th of this month, selling out all seats including restricted-view seats across three days and drawing approximately 32,000 attendees. The group is now set to embark on a world tour spanning 14 regions around the globe to meet fans worldwide.
