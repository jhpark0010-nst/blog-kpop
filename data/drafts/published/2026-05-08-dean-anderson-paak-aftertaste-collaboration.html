Singer DEAN released a collaboration single titled 'AFTERTASTE' with Korean-American pop star Anderson .Paak on May 8, according to his label Universal Music Korea. The track marks the first collaboration between the two artists in 11 years, since 'Put My Hands On You' in 2015.

'AFTERTASTE' combines powerful drums and smooth synthesizer string sounds with DEAN's sweet vocals and Anderson .Paak's direct emotional expression. The two singers convey a feeling of actively reaching out toward love in the song.

DEAN is a singer-songwriter and producer who represents the domestic alternative R&B genre, known for songs such as 'instagram' and 'D.' Anderson .Paak is a Korean-American artist who has won 9 Grammy Awards.

TWICE member Jihyo has participated as a featured artist on American R&B singer Genevieve's new song 'Hvnly,' according to their agency JYP Entertainment. Released at 1 p.m. on May 8, 'Hvnly' is an alternative R&B pop track noted for its soulful atmosphere. Genevieve is a solo artist who has drawn attention in the American R&B market with songs such as 'Love Quotes' and 'Baby Powder.' TWICE, the group Jihyo belongs to, will begin a European tour starting in Lisbon, Portugal on May 9 (local time).

The 2026 Pentaport Rock Festival has announced its first lineup, which includes Khruangbin, Silica Gel, The Volunteers, and Jang Pil-soon, among 28 acts revealed on May 8. Khruangbin is a three-member band based in Houston, Texas, that has drawn attention since its formation in 2010 for a distinctive sound blending funk, soul, and psychedelic rock. Also set to perform are The Lemon Twigs, a New York-based brother duo who reinterpret 1960s–1970s pop and rock sounds in a modern style, and Never Young Beach, a band representing Japan's indie music scene.

The 2026 Pentaport Rock Festival will be held at Incheon Songdo Moonlight Festival Park from July 31 to August 2.
