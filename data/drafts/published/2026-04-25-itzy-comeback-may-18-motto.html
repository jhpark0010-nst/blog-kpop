ITZY is making their comeback with a new mini album titled 'Motto' on May 18, 2026 — approximately six months after their previous release. JYP Entertainment announced the comeback on April 22 via official social media channels, sharing a trailer, tracklist, and scheduler for the new mini album. The group's last release was 'TUNNEL VISION' in November 2025.

According to the scheduler, ITZY will begin releasing concept photos starting April 27, followed by individual content under the title 'What is your Motto,' album spoilers, and a music video teaser in sequence. On May 18, the release date, a countdown live will be held at 5 PM KST to celebrate the comeback with their fandom MIDZY (official fandom name). A US physical album and a remix version of 'Motto' will also be released at 1 PM KST on April 22.

The new mini album 'Motto' contains a total of 8 tracks: the title track 'Motto,' 'Glitch,' 'you And I,' and five solo songs first performed at individual stages during the group's third world tour — 'Pocket' (Yeji), 'Asylum' (Lia), 'LOOK' (Ryujin), 'Undefined' (Chaeryeong), and 'Tangerine' (Yuna). The album features contributions from domestic and international songwriters including Shim Eun-ji, Lee Woo-min 'collapsedone,' KASS, and Justin Reinstein.

ITZY kicked off their new world tour in February at the Jamsil Indoor Stadium in Songpa-gu, Seoul. At that time, they unveiled the performance of 'THAT'S A NO NO' from their second mini album for the first time, and the dance challenge for the song — nicknamed 'Daechoo Nono' — has been gaining significant attention on social media. Riding the momentum of the world tour, ITZY's new release 'Motto' will be available on major music streaming platforms at 6 PM KST on May 18.
