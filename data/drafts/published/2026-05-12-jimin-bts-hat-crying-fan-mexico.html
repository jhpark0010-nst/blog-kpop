BTS Jimin moved fans around the world with a heartwarming act of kindness during the Arirang World Tour finale at GNP Seguros Stadium in Mexico City on May 10 (local time). While performing, Jimin noticed a young girl in the front row crying uncontrollably and paused to respond.

Despite the energy of the live show, Jimin did not miss the small girl's distress. He walked straight to the edge of the stage and crouched down. He then took off the hat he was wearing, handed it to a staff member, and asked them to pass it on to the fan.

Jimin looked at the fan with sincere eyes and whispered 'Don't cry' before returning to the performance.

The girl, upon receiving the surprise gift, embraced her mother and wept with emotion for a long moment. She then broke into a bright smile and held the hat up high in joy. The video capturing this miraculous moment was shared on social media by the girl's mother, Grecia Ortega.

Grecia posted the video with the short but powerful message 'Gracias Jimin,' adding the caption: 'He probably doesn't know. But today he taught my daughter that all dreams come true.' The video also included the Spanish subtitle 'Y el tan dulce y conmovido de verla' — meaning 'And he was so moved just seeing her' — vividly conveying Jimin's warmth in that moment.

The clip sparked an explosive reaction online. Fan @soloistsyd wrote, 'He is such a precious person,' while fan @jusstalover praised his remarkable stage presence, saying, 'The speed at which Jimin moves is just incredible. Without even catching his breath.' Jimin's connection with Mexico is also reflected in the numbers — he currently holds 97 million streams on Mexico's Spotify chart, the most of any K-pop solo artist. On the first night of the concert, he also spoke in fluent Spanish, telling the 50,000 ARMY in attendance, 'I had no idea you would be this amazing. I love you more than anyone.'
