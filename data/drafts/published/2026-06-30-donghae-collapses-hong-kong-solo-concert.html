Super Junior's Donghae suffered a recurrence of his herniated disc condition during his Asia tour, collapsing on stage in a distressing incident. According to a June 29 report by Taiwanese outlet ET Today, Donghae collapsed during his first-ever solo concert 'ALIVE' held in Hong Kong on June 28, shocking fans in attendance.

While performing, Donghae endured severe pain for some time with his eyes covered before ultimately collapsing on stage and being unable to move easily. Staff who recognized the situation immediately rushed to support him and moved him off the stage so he could rest in an emergency.

After barely making it back on stage, Donghae personally explained the urgent situation — that he had tried to stand but had no strength in his legs at all, forcing him to temporarily halt the performance. Despite being in a condition where he could barely hold himself upright, he showed tremendous determination by completing the remaining schedule seated in a chair. He went around the entire venue making eye contact with fans in each section, and performed the interrupted songs again three separate times.

Overcome with emotion at having shown fans a painful sight contrary to the promises he had made at the opening, Donghae tearfully said, 'You took precious time out of your day to come see my concert, and I feel so deeply sorry for ruining your day.' His words moved everyone present.

After the concert, Donghae posted a handwritten letter in Cantonese on his personal account for local fans, once again expressing his apologies. He wrote, 'Originally, I wanted to properly repay the love you have given me through this concert, but I am truly so sorry and regretful that I could not show you my best.' He added, 'After returning to Korea, I will immediately receive treatment and take good care of my health, and I promise to come back to see you next time in the best possible condition.'
