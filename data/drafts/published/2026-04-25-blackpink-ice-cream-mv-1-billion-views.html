BLACKPINK's 'Ice Cream' music video has surpassed 1 billion views on YouTube. According to YG Entertainment on April 16, the music video crossed the 1 billion view mark at approximately 4:12 PM the previous day. It is the group's 10th video to reach 1 billion views, which is the most among any K-pop artist.

'Ice Cream' is a pop track distinguished by its simple rhythm and upbeat sound. The song, which marked BLACKPINK's first attempt at a cute concept, was praised for expanding the group's global fanbase and musical spectrum through a collaboration with pop star Selena Gomez.

The song also achieved remarkable results on major global charts. It debuted at No. 1 on the iTunes Worldwide Chart upon release, and reached No. 13 on the US Billboard Hot 100 — setting a new personal best for a K-pop girl group — before charting for eight consecutive weeks.

The album that includes the track, BLACKPINK's first full-length studio album 'THE ALBUM,' reached No. 2 on both the US Billboard 200 and the UK Official Albums Chart, breaking the previous record for the highest chart position by a K-pop girl group, and remained on each chart for 10 and 7 consecutive weeks respectively.

Meanwhile, BLACKPINK has now produced a total of 50 videos with over 100 million views, cementing their unrivaled status as 'YouTube Queens.' Their official YouTube channel became the first by any artist worldwide to surpass 100 million subscribers, and their cumulative view count has exceeded 41.7 billion.
