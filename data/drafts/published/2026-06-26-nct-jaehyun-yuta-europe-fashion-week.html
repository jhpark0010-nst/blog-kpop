NCT members Jaehyun and Yuta attended European Fashion Week events, continuing their relationships with major global fashion brands. Both members represented their respective brand partnerships at high-profile shows held in Italy and France in late June 2026.

Jaehyun attended the Prada Spring/Summer 2027 Menswear Show held in Milan, Italy on June 21 (local time). As a Prada Global Ambassador, this marked his fifth invitation to a Prada show.

Yuta attended the Louis Vuitton Men's Spring-Summer 2027 Show held in Paris, France on June 23. As a Louis Vuitton Friend of the House, Yuta has now been invited to the Louis Vuitton show for four consecutive years.

Meanwhile, Jaehyun held his Asian fan concert tour 'JAEHYUN FAN-CON TOUR in SEOUL' last month following his discharge from military service. Yuta met audiences in March with the film 'Specials'.
