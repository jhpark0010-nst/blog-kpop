NCT's Taeyong and ITZY are both releasing new albums on May 18, 2026 at 6 PM KST — Taeyong with his first full-length studio album and ITZY with a new mini album dropping at the same time.

SM Entertainment announced that Taeyong wrote, composed, and produced all 10 tracks on his debut full-length album 'WYLD'. The album marks his return approximately two years after his hiatus due to mandatory military service, and reflects a deeper musical sensibility.

The title track 'WYLD' is a hip-hop song inspired by the instinctive movements of wild animals. Taeyong said through his agency, 'I felt that WYLD was the perfect song to unleash the energy and instincts that had built up during two years away.'

He added, 'I tried to include only the songs I was most confident would show who I am right now. That's why I worked on each track more carefully than ever.'

ITZY is releasing their new album approximately six months after 'TUNNEL VISION', which came out in November last year. JYP Entertainment stated that their 12th mini album 'Motto' contains a total of 8 tracks, including the title track 'Motto' and 'Glitch'. The title track 'Motto' is described as a song with a refreshing feel and a trendy melody.

ITZY said through their agency, 'We are excited to show a more grown version of ourselves. This was an album where we could express the love we received through concerts and solo activities. We hope our feelings come through.'
