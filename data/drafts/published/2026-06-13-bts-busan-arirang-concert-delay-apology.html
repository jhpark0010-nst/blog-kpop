BTS has issued an official apology after their concert at Busan Asiad Main Stadium in Yeonje-gu, Busan on June 12 began approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes behind schedule. The show, part of the BTS WORLD TOUR 'ARIRANG' IN BUSAN, was originally set to start at 7:00 PM but did not begin until 8:15 PM.

In response to the delay, BTS posted an apology on Weverse, the fan community platform, stating: 'We sincerely apologize to all audience members for the great inconvenience caused by the delayed start of the concert.'

They continued: 'We prepared as thoroughly as possible so that we could provide a meaningful time for our audience, but a combination of factors — including confusion in on-site guidance, bottlenecks in the fan gift distribution queue, and delays in merchandise pickup — resulted in the delay of the concert start. We once again sincerely apologize for the great disappointment and inconvenience caused to all audience members who waited so long to see the show.'

BTS also made a promise regarding the following day's concert: 'To ensure that the same congestion does not occur at the next day's concert, we will thoroughly review and improve all aspects of on-site operations, including entry and gift distribution.'

Below is the full text of the statement posted by BTS on Weverse. Hello, this is HYBE. We sincerely apologize to all audience members who came to see the BTS WORLD TOUR 'ARIRANG' IN BUSAN concert held on June 12 for the great inconvenience caused by the delayed start.

We prepared as thoroughly as possible so that we could provide a meaningful time for our audience, but a combination of on-site guidance confusion, bottlenecks in the fan gift distribution queue, and delays in merchandise pickup resulted in the delay of the concert start. We once again sincerely apologize for the great disappointment and inconvenience caused to all audience members who waited so long. We will thoroughly review and improve all aspects of on-site operations, including entry and gift distribution, to ensure the same congestion does not occur at the next day's concert. For inquiries related to the concert, please contact the NOL Ticket customer service center.
