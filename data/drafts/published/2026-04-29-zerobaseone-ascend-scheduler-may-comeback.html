ZEROBASEONE has officially kicked off comeback preparations by releasing the scheduler image for their sixth mini album 'Ascend-'. The group posted the scheduler on their official social media at midnight on April 29, signaling the start of a full promotional rollout ahead of the May 18, 2026 release.

ZEROBASEONE — consisting of members Sung Hanbin, Kim Jiwoong, Seok Matthew, Kim Taerae, and Park Gunwook — will begin revealing content on April 30 with the first concept photo. Following that, fans can look forward to a concept film, tracklist, album preview, music video teaser, and album sampler. Additionally, May 1 holds extra significance as it marks the birthday of ZEROSE, the group's official fandom name, making the countdown even more meaningful.

The scheduler image itself is drawing attention for its classic styling — suits paired with ties set against a minimal design — building anticipation for the group's overall concept. Global K-pop fans are curious about ZEROBASEONE's transformation across three distinct concepts: 'Cover Story,' 'Scenario,' and 'A-Senses.'

ZEROBASEONE had previously announced special promotional plans that are raising excitement further. The group will release the new music video ahead of the album drop and is also set to meet fans the day before the official release through an unprecedented comeback premiere stage, expected to bring the pre-release energy to its peak.

With 'Ascend-', ZEROBASEONE plans to open a new chapter and weave their musical journey so far into a single narrative. Having established themselves as a top-tier fifth-generation act both domestically and internationally, all eyes are on how they will demonstrate an even more mature side with this new release.

ZEROBASEONE's 6th mini album 'Ascend-' will be released across major online music platforms on May 18 at 6 p.m. KST.
