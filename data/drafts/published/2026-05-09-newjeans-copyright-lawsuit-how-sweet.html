NewJeans has been hit with a copyright infringement lawsuit in the United States. According to Billboard, four songwriters including Audrey Amacost filed a suit against NewJeans and their label, demanding royalty distribution over the group's May 2024 single 'How Sweet.'

The plaintiffs allege that 'How Sweet' copies their demo track 'One of a Kind.' They claim they previously pitched a topline (main melody) to the NewJeans team, but it was not adopted. They argue that the first verse melody and composition of 'How Sweet,' released afterward, are similar to 'One of a Kind.'

In response, NewJeans' label ADOR stated: 'We confirmed with VANA, who was in charge of composing and producing this song, and their position is that there was no plagiarism.' ADOR added: 'ADOR and the members plan to respond to the lawsuit in accordance with VANA's position.'
