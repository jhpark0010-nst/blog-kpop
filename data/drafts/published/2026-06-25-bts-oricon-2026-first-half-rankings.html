BTS has taken the top spot in two categories of Oricon's 2026 first-half rankings. According to the 'First-Half Ranking 2026' released by Oricon on June 25 — covering the period from December 8, 2025 to June 7, 2026 — BTS's fifth full-length album 'ARIRANG' ranked No. 1 in both the 'Album Ranking' and the 'Combined Album Ranking.'

The 'Album Ranking' tallies physical album sales only. The 'Combined Album Ranking' is a comprehensive ranking that adds digital album downloads and a portion of streaming plays, converted into points, to physical sales. BTS was the only K-pop act to reach the top of the first-half rankings. The group also placed No. 3 in the 'Digital Album Ranking' with 'ARIRANG.'

Multiple K-pop boy groups also placed high in the rankings. In the 'Combined Album Ranking,' ENHYPEN's 'THE SIN : VANISH' came in at No. 4, and TOMORROW X TOGETHER's '7TH YEAR: When the Wind Paused Briefly in the Thorns' placed No. 9. TOURS's 'NO TRAGEDY' ranked No. 10, and ALPHADRIVE1's 'EUPHORIA' came in at No. 13.

In the 'Album Ranking,' ENHYPEN placed No. 4, TOMORROW X TOGETHER No. 7, and TOURS No. 9, continuing their strong showing. In the video category, Stray Kids recorded No. 10 in both the 'Music DVD-Blu-ray Ranking' and the 'Blu-ray Ranking' with 'FAN CONNECTING 2024 SKZOO TOY WORLD.'

Localized groups operating in the Japanese market under Korean agency systems also drew attention. HYBE Labels Japan group &TEAM placed No. 3 in both the 'Combined Album Ranking' and the 'Album Ranking' with 'We on Fire.' NiziU, the girl group created by JYP Entertainment, ranked No. 15 in the 'Combined Album Ranking' with 'GOOD GIRL BUT NOT FOR YOU.'

INI, a boy group from CJ ENM, placed No. 7 in the 'Combined Single Ranking' and No. 3 in the 'Single Ranking' with 'PULSE(All 4 U/OURS/DUM/EAT UP).' JO1, another boy group under the same agency as INI, ranked No. 9 in the 'DVD Ranking' with 'WHEREVER WE ARE,' a DVD capturing their Tokyo Dome concert.
