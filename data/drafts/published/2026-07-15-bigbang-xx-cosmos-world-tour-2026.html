BIGBANG has officially revealed the title of their large-scale world tour commemorating their 20th debut anniversary. On July 15, YG Entertainment posted two new posters for the tour on official social media. The main poster unveiled the official tour title 'BIGBANG 2026-2027 WORLD TOUR < XX : COSMOS >' for the first time, while the routing poster featured an updated schedule with a newly added show in Manila, Philippines.

The main poster combines a rough monotone noise texture with a mysterious visual, leaving a striking impression. The imagery, which evokes an unknown signal captured from somewhere deep in the cosmos, hints at the grand worldview and unique concept that this world tour will unfold.

The title reveal carries added significance in that it reflects ideas from the members themselves. The initials that make up 'COSMOS' had been hidden like Easter eggs throughout the previously released posters. It was designed so that fans could examine each poster one by one and piece together the tour name, and the full meaning was revealed alongside this official title announcement, delivering a distinctive sense of fun.

This title simultaneously encompasses the brilliant 20-year journey BIGBANG has walked and the new musical milestone they will head toward going forward. YG stated, "'XX' represents BIGBANG's time as they mark their 20th debut anniversary, while 'COSMOS' is not simply the universe in a spatial sense, but a cosmic concept encompassing connection, relationships, and continuity." They added, "Just as it means 'everything that existed in the past and present and will exist in the future,' it symbolizes the musical journey BIGBANG has written over the past 20 years and the time they have built together with their fans."

This concert has drawn intense reaction even before ticketing opened, as it marks BIGBANG's first solo world tour in nine years, held to celebrate their 20th anniversary. The show is set to distill 20 years of musical narrative based on the message symbolized by 'COSMOS,' while also charting the new journey ahead. An overwhelming-scale performance is expected, blending beloved hit song stages that have resonated across generations with the musical depth accumulated over 20 years and BIGBANG's unparalleled production.

BIGBANG will kick off their 20th anniversary world tour spanning North America, Europe, Oceania, and Asia with 'BIGBANG 2026-2027 WORLD TOUR < XX : COSMOS > IN GOYANG,' held at Goyang Stadium from August 21 to 23. All three Goyang shows, including additional seats, have sold out completely, proving their enduring ticketing power. With the addition of the Manila, Philippines show, the tour has expanded to a total of 19 cities and 33 performances.
