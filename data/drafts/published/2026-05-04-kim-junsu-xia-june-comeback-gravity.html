Kim Junsu (XIA) has confirmed his comeback for June 2, 2026. His agency Palm Tree Island announced on the 4th that 'Kim Junsu revealed the promotion scheduler for his 5th full album GRAVITY through his official SNS at midnight (00:00).' This marks his first full album release in approximately 10 years, since his 4th full album 'XIGNATURE' in May 2016.

According to the released scheduler, Kim Junsu will sequentially unveil a variety of content starting with a prologue film on the 6th, followed by concept photos, a concept film, a tracklist, an album preview, a music video teaser, and a highlight medley.

GRAVITY tells the story of an unchanging attraction and relationship — as if, despite having passed through different stretches of time, one is ultimately drawn back to a single point. The title track of the same name is an electronic house genre track characterized by an intense bass line and synthesizer sound.

Meanwhile, Kim Junsu will hold the '2026 XIA 6TH ASIA TOUR CONCERT GRAVITY IN SEOUL' at KSPO DOME in Seoul for three days, from June 12 to 14, immediately following the album release.
