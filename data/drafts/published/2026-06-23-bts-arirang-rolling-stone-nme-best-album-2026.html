BTS's fifth studio album 'ARIRANG' has been consecutively named to 'Best Albums of 2026' lists by major international music publications, Big Hit Music announced on June 23.

Starting with US pop culture outlet Complex on June 12 (local time), followed by UK daily The Telegraph on June 13, US music publication Rolling Stone on June 15, and UK music outlet NME on June 16, 'ARIRANG' was included in each publication's best albums of 2026 lineup.

Rolling Stone described BTS's comeback as 'undoubtedly the biggest news in the global music market in 2026,' adding that 'through ARIRANG, they have brilliantly expressed a distinctly Korean sensibility through their music.'

NME introduced BTS as 'the biggest boy band in the world,' while The Telegraph praised them, stating 'BTS are the face of K-pop.'

BTS wrapped up their 'ARIRANG' world tour performances in Busan on June 12 and 13. They are set to kick off their European tour leg starting in Madrid, Spain on June 26 and 27, with 10 shows across 5 cities.
