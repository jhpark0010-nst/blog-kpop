TWS achieved a career high just one day after releasing their fifth mini album. According to Hanteo Chart, the album 'NO TRAGEDY' by TWS (Shin Yu, Dohoon, Youngjae, Hanjin, Jihoon, Kyungmin) sold 833,138 copies on its release day of April 27, debuting directly at number one on the daily album chart. This figure far surpasses the cumulative sales of their fourth mini album 'play hard,' which reached 746,731 copies as tallied by Circle Chart through last month.

The streaming response has also been strong. As of midnight on April 28, multiple tracks from the new album entered Melon's Top 100, including the title track 'Neol Ttaragа (You, You)' at number 55, 'I'll Be Every Possibility of Yours' at number 75, and 'Why You So Bad?' at number 100. On Japan's Line Music real-time Song Top 100 (as of 11 p.m. on April 27), every track on the album charted, signaling a strong start for TWS on both domestic and international streaming charts.

The music video for 'Neol Ttaragа (You, You)' has also been receiving positive reactions. The visuals capturing the energy of radiant youth and the members' striking looks have become a talking point. The addictive chorus hook 'Dda-rum' has emerged as a standout moment for K-pop fans. While retaining TWS's signature refreshing quality, the group's more mature presentation drew praise such as 'a music video that trendily captures the fresh sensibility between boyhood and young adulthood' and 'the TWS boom is already here.'

TWS held the 'NO TRAGEDY' comeback showcase (a live event marking their return with new music) at Yes24 Live Hall in Gwangjin-gu, Seoul on April 27, kicking off their promotional activities in earnest. They performed a total of four songs, including the title track, 'I'll Be Every Possibility of Yours,' 'Back To Strangers,' and digital single 'Today We Meet Again,' showcasing their deepened musical capabilities. The showcase was simultaneously live-streamed via global superfan platform Weverse, with fans from 142 countries and regions including China, the United States, Japan, and Indonesia tuning in.

After wrapping up the successful showcase, TWS said, 'Above all, we will create activities that can become precious memories for our 42s (fandom name). We are happy to meet our fans again on a stage we have been eagerly awaiting.' They also stated, 'We will become a TWS that you can be proud of.'

TWS will begin their music show promotions with Mnet's 'M Countdown' on April 30. Various content appearances have also been announced: Kyungmin on web variety show 'Limousine Service' on April 28, Dohoon on 'Jeonkwaja' and Shin Yu and Youngjae on 'Bu Seungkwan's Bibbidi Bobbidi Boo' on April 30. On May 4, Shin Yu and Jihoon are scheduled to appear on JTBC variety program 'Tokpa-won 25' to show off their fresh wit.
