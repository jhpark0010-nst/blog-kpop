LE SSERAFIM leader Kim Chaewon will temporarily halt all activities just three days before the group's comeback, following a sudden decline in health. Agency Source Music announced the news on May 19 via a fan community platform, stating it was providing an update regarding 'member Kim Chaewon's health condition and schedule.'

According to Source Music, Kim Chaewon recently experienced pain in her neck area and received hospital treatment. Medical staff advised that she needs to rest for a certain period and monitor her recovery. Source Music stated, 'We place the health and safety of our artists as our top priority, and after comprehensively considering the medical opinion and the artist's condition, we determined that it is necessary for her to focus on sufficient recovery so she can continue activities in a more stable condition.'

As a result of this decision, Kim Chaewon will be absent from all officially scheduled events for the time being. She will not be seen at university festival stages that had already been confirmed, the Spotify PURE FLOWERS LIVE event, or terrestrial and cable music broadcast (music show) appearances scheduled during LE SSERAFIM's second full-length album comeback week. Source Music added, 'We ask for your generous understanding,' and noted that 'future schedules will be managed flexibly depending on her recovery status.'

The news of Kim Chaewon's absence carries particular weight as LE SSERAFIM's second full-length album 'PUREFLOW pt.1' is set to release on May 22 at 1 PM KST. With the team's leader stepping back just three days before the album drop, LE SSERAFIM will need to carry out their initial comeback activities as a four-member unit for the foreseeable future.

Below is the full official statement from Source Music: 'Hello, this is Source Music. We are providing an update regarding LE SSERAFIM member Kim Chaewon's health condition and schedule. Kim Chaewon recently received hospital treatment for neck pain and was advised by medical staff to rest for a certain period and observe her recovery progress. Placing the health and safety of our artists as our top priority, and after comprehensively considering the medical opinion and the artist's condition, we have determined that it is necessary for her to focus on sufficient recovery so she can continue activities in a more stable condition. Accordingly, Kim Chaewon will unfortunately be unable to attend previously announced schedules, including university festivals, Spotify PURE FLOWERS LIVE, and music broadcasts. We ask for your generous understanding. We will actively support the artist so she can focus entirely on her recovery, and future schedules will be managed flexibly depending on her recovery status. We will do our utmost to help Kim Chaewon recover quickly and return in good health. We sincerely thank all fans who always send LE SSERAFIM your unwavering love and support.'
