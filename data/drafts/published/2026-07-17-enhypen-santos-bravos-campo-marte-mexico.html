ENHYPEN and Latin pop group Santos Bravos appeared at CAMPO MARTE 26, a large-scale festival held in Mexico City, Mexico on July 15 (local time) to celebrate the 2026 FIFA North and Central America World Cup. HYBE announced the news on July 17.

CAMPO MARTE 26 is a large-scale multi-cultural festival combining football cheering, Mexican culture, food, and music. ENHYPEN and Santos Bravos delivered an enthusiastic performance at the festival spanning approximately two hours.

HYBE noted that, as the stages of two acts with great local popularity had been announced in advance, the area around the Music Pavilion venue was packed with a large crowd of audiences early on. ENHYPEN performed a total of 11 songs, including 'Knife', 'XO', 'Outside', and 'Stealer'. The members shared their feelings in Spanish, saying, 'Seeing your reactions, we feel happiness and joy too,' and added, 'That is why today is truly meaningful.'

Santos Bravos took the opening stage ahead of ENHYPEN. They performed 6 songs including their debut track '0%', 'MHM', 'KAWASAKI', and 'WOW'. Santos Bravos also unveiled for the first time on-site a special performance video for their Brazilian funk-based title track 'VELOCIDADE'.

Santos Bravos is the first boy group introduced by HYBE Latin America, having debuted in October of last year. The group is a multinational five-member act consisting of Drew (USA), Kenneth (Mexico), Alejandro (Peru), Kaue (Brazil), and Gabi (Puerto Rico).
