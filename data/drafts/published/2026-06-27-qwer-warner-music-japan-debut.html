Band QWER has signed a contract with major Japanese record label Warner Music Japan and will begin full-scale activities in the local market, their agency 3Y Corporation announced on June 27.

Up until now, QWER had been steadily making inroads into the Japanese market by releasing a Japanese-language version of their debut track 'Discord' and performing on local festival stages.

The group will officially kick off their Japan activities starting with the release of 'SHOW DOWN,' the original soundtrack for Fuji TV's anime series 'Dungeon King,' on July 8.
