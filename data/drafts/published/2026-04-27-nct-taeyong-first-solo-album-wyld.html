NCT's Taeyong will release his first solo full-length album, 'WYLD', on May 18, according to his agency SM Entertainment. The announcement was made on April 27, confirming the album's title and release date.

The album consists of 10 tracks in total, including the title track of the same name, 'WYLD'. Taeyong participated in writing lyrics for all 10 songs and composed music for 9 of the 10 tracks, and is also credited as a producer on the album.

Having steadily built his own musical identity through solo activities, attention is now focused on this first full-length album as a milestone that positions him as an all-around musician.

Taeyong's first solo full-length album 'WYLD' will also be released as a physical album on May 18. Pre-orders began today, April 27, at various online and offline music stores.
