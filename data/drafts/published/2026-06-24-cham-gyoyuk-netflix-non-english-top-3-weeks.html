Korean Netflix series Cham Gyoyuk has topped the non-English TV shows chart for a third consecutive week. According to Netflix's official Top 10 site Tudum, the series recorded 11.8 million views (calculated by dividing total watch time by runtime) for the period of June 15 to 21, ranking first among all non-English shows globally.

Cham Gyoyuk premiered on June 5 and reached the number one spot in the non-English show category within just three days of its release. It has now held that position for three straight weeks. The series ranked first in 19 countries including South Korea, Japan, Vietnam, and Peru, and entered the Top 10 in 85 countries.

Starring actor Kim Mu-yeol, Cham Gyoyuk is based on a webtoon of the same name. It follows a fictional agency called the 'Teachers' Rights Protection Bureau' as it works to correct an education system that has collapsed due to students, teachers, and parents who have crossed the line. In South Korea, the show has sparked public debate, with voices calling for the fictional bureau's concept to be introduced into real education policy. Overseas, it has been well received as a satisfying action-driven story of justice and revenge.

Riding on Cham Gyoyuk's popularity, director Hong Jong-chan's and Kim Mu-yeol's previous collaboration Juvenile Justice has re-entered the non-English TV Top 10 four years after its original release. The series recorded 1.2 million views to land at number 10, appearing in the Top 10 in 16 countries worldwide.

Also in the non-English TV category, Brave New World starring Im Ji-yeon and Heo Nam-jun ranked sixth with 2.7 million views. In the English-language films category, K-Pop Demon Hunters entered the Top 10 for its 53rd consecutive week, holding at sixth place with 3.6 million views, the same ranking as the previous week. In the non-English films category, Husbands starring Gong Myung and Jin Seon-kyu ranked second with 5.7 million views. The film is a comic action story about an ex-husband and a current husband who unexpectedly team up to rescue a wife kidnapped by a criminal organization.
