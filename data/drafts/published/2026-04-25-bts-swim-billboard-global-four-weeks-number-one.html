BTS has held the top position on the Billboard Global charts for four consecutive weeks. According to the latest Billboard chart dated April 25, announced on April 21, the title track 'SWIM' from BTS's fifth full-length album ARIRANG claimed No. 1 on both the Global 200 and Global (Excluding U.S.) charts for the fourth week in a row, demonstrating worldwide popularity.

All 13 vocal tracks from ARIRANG — excluding the bell sound of the Sungdeok Royal Bell — maintained their positions on the Global 200, while all of them landed within the top 50 on the Global (Excluding U.S.) chart, marking the beginning of a sustained long-term run.

The album's performance on the main charts is equally strong. ARIRANG ranked No. 3 on the Billboard 200, while 'SWIM' reached No. 10 on the Hot 100. Album tracks 'Body to Body' (No. 69), '2.0' (No. 88), and 'Hooligan' (No. 90) also remained on the Hot 100 for a full month. BTS additionally claimed No. 1 on Digital Song Sales for four straight weeks, No. 2 on Top Album Sales, No. 3 on Vinyl Albums, No. 4 on Top Streaming Albums, and No. 5 on Artist 100.

Meanwhile, BTS kicked off the BTS WORLD TOUR 'ARIRANG' at Goyang Stadium's main arena on April 9. The tour spans 85 shows across 34 cities, the highest number of shows ever for a single Korean artist. BTS has already met more than 240,000 fans in Goyang and Tokyo, and will continue the North American leg at Raymond James Stadium in Tampa, Florida on April 25, 26, and 28.
