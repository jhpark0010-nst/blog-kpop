TVXQ's U-KNOW Yunho is holding his solo concert 'U-KNOW PROJECT 26 : SCENE#1' at Jamsil Indoor Stadium in Seoul from July 17 for three days, according to his agency SM Entertainment. This marks the first solo concert of his career, 23 years after his debut in 2003.

At the concert, U-KNOW Yunho will also unveil the stage performance of his new single 'Time's Tickin',' which is set for release on July 20.

Speaking through his agency, U-KNOW Yunho said, 'It's my first solo concert in 23 years since my debut, and there's an excitement that comes with the word 'first.'' He added, 'I've been dreaming of a concert like this for a long time, and I can't believe it's actually unfolding right before my eyes. I really want to express my gratitude to everyone.'

He also described the concept of the show: 'This concert features 'young Yunho' appearing and meeting 'future Yunho.' I tried to tell a story about the past and the present through the coordinates of time. It's a performance that combines concerts, musicals, dramas, and films — all the experiences I've had throughout my career — into a single genre.'

U-KNOW Yunho has been planning a solo concert since his military service from 2015 to 2017. Although opportunities arose over the years, he felt he needed to build up more experience and ability before taking the stage alone.

He concluded, 'I felt that now is the right time to fully express on stage everything I've experienced and felt. Thank you for always waiting. From here on, I'm ready to take off.'
