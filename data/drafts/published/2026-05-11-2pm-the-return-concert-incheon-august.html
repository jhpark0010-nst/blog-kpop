2PM will hold a full 6-member solo concert titled 'THE RETURN' at Inspire Arena on Yeongjong Island, Incheon, on August 8–9, 2026, JYP Entertainment announced on May 11.

2PM revealed the news through an LED display at their Japan debut 15th anniversary concert held at Tokyo Dome the previous day.

At the Tokyo Dome concerts held on May 9–10, the members performed hit songs including 'Heartbeat,' 'Hands Up,' and 'Uri Jip' (Our House). It was their first time on the Tokyo Dome stage in 10 years, since their pre-enlistment concert in 2016.

2PM will continue the momentum domestically with the August Incheon concert. It will be their first full-group concert in South Korea in approximately three years, since 'It's 2PM' in September 2023.

JYP stated, '2PM will fulfill the long wait of their fans while carrying the energy that set Tokyo Dome ablaze onto the stage at Inspire Arena.'

2PM debuted in 2008 with '10 Out of 10' and went on to achieve wide popularity in Korea and across Asia with hit songs such as 'Again & Again,' 'Heartbeat,' and 'Without You.' They became known by the nickname 'beast idols' for their masculine appeal and high-difficulty choreography.
