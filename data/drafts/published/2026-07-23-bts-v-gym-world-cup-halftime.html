BTS member V showcased his remarkable dedication to self-care by heading straight to the gym immediately after successfully completing the 2026 World Cup halftime show performance on July 19 (local time).

On July 22, bodybuilder Choi Han-jin shared a mirror selfie taken with V on his social media. He added the caption: 'To think he went straight to work out the moment he arrived in Korea after the World Cup performance. Incredible, Taehyung.'

In the photo, V appeared in a relaxed outfit consisting of a red sleeveless top paired with wide training pants. He completed the look with a cap and sunglasses, and his toned arm muscles and broad shoulders, visible through the sleeveless top, drew attention.

BTS had taken the stage at the final match halftime show of the 2026 North America World Cup on July 19 (local time), meeting fans from around the world. As co-headliners alongside global pop stars including Madonna, Shakira, and Justin Bieber, BTS performed their signature track 'Dynamite' and electrified the stadium.

After wrapping up his overseas schedule, V returned to Korea through Incheon International Airport on the morning of July 21. His choice to go straight to the gym instead of resting — even after a long-haul flight and a major performance — drew admiration from fans.

Fans who saw the post responded with comments such as 'He is truly serious about self-management,' 'What a great routine,' and 'It is amazing how consistent he is, even with working out,' sending their support.
