The schedule for additional seat reservations for BIGBANG's Goyang concert, held in celebration of the group's 20th debut anniversary, has been confirmed. On July 7, YG Entertainment announced that a number of seats — including cancellations and newly added sections — will be opened for the 'BIGBANG 2026 WORLD TOUR IN GOYANG with Coupang Play.' Fans will now be able to book floor seats as well as seats on the 2nd and 3rd levels.

The concert had already demonstrated BIGBANG's overwhelming ticket power: the pre-sale sold out rapidly, and the general ticketing for all three shows sold out in just 22 minutes after opening. Approximately 210,000 people flooded the ticketing platform simultaneously during the general sale, reaffirming the group's enduring status as a K-pop legend group. This figure is the highest recorded for any single domestic concert or event in Korea so far this year.

Fierce competition is expected for the additional seats as well. V.I.P (the official fandom name) members who have completed pre-sale membership verification can book on July 13, while general ticketing for non-members will open on July 14.

BIGBANG will kick off their 20th anniversary world tour at Goyang Sports Complex from August 21 to 23 — three consecutive days — before heading to a total of 18 cities across North America, Europe, Oceania, and Asia for 32 shows. This world tour, their first in approximately nine years, is set to encapsulate BIGBANG's 20-year musical journey, with performances at large-scale stadiums and dome-class venues around the world.
