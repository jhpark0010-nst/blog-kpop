NCT's Taeyong has signaled an exceptional command of genres ahead of his debut full-length album. His first studio album, 'WYLD', will be released on major music platforms on May 18 at 6 p.m. KST, and is composed of a total of 10 tracks including the title track of the same name, 'WYLD'.

Among the tracks, 'Hypnotic' is a hip-hop song that blends jazz and breakbeat to evoke a free-spirited sensibility. Built on a beat featuring lo-fi textured piano, a heavy bass, and fast-paced drum sounds, the track showcases Taeyong's addictive hook.

Also included is 'Run', a dynamic pop and alternative rock track driven by an intense overdrive riff over a racing drum beat and clap sounds. Taeyong's crisp vocals, set against a nostalgic band sound, are intricately balanced with a retro sensibility and a forward-looking arpeggiated synth line.

Taeyong's first full-length album 'WYLD' will also be released as a physical album on May 18.
