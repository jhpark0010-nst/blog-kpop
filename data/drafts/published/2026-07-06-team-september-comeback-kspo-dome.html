&TEAM has officially announced a new album release and their first concert at KSPO Dome. The nine-member boy group held the Korean leg of their Asia Tour at Incheon Inspire Arena on July 4 and 5.

During the encore stage of the July 5 concert, the group revealed that their Korean mini album 2, titled 'Mark on Me,' will be released on September 8. They also announced encore concerts at Seoul's KSPO Dome (formerly known as the Gymnastics Arena) on October 3 and 4.

The members said, 'Thanks to LUNÉ (the fandom name), we have been able to grow step by step.' They added, 'In about two months we will be back with a new album, so please look forward to a new side of &TEAM that you have never seen before.'

&TEAM is a Japan-based localized nine-member boy group under HYBE Labels Japan, who debuted on December 7, 2022. The group consists of Korean member Yuma (의주), Japanese members K, Fuma, Yuma, Jo, Harua, and Taki, Taiwanese member Nicolas, and Maki, who holds dual Japanese-German nationality.
