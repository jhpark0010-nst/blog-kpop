BOYNEXTDOOR (Sungho, Riwoo, Myungjae, Taesan, Leehan, Woonhak) has officially announced the release of their first full-length album, 'HOME', scheduled for June 8 at 6 PM KST. The announcement was made on May 8 at 10 PM through the album's official website. The site is available in four languages, improving accessibility for global fans, and features a design reminiscent of the group's pre-debut homepage, evoking a sense of nostalgia.

The website incorporates BOYNEXTDOOR's signature symbols — a door and a mailbox — along with various interactive elements for fans to enjoy. Pressing the doorbell triggers a cheerful ringtone as the door swings open, revealing a calendar and clock marked with the comeback date, as well as a framed display of the album title. Visitors can also press the door lens on the front door to take photos with a variety of decorative frames.

A letter inside the mailbox adds a playful touch with witty messages. It reads: 'This letter was sent from the future to announce the release of BOYNEXTDOOR's full album HOME. It will be a day when BOYNEXTDOOR's songs echo through the streets, flood your feeds, and fill every seat in the audience.' The letter also carries the message: 'It must go viral.'

BOYNEXTDOOR has been on a rapid rise, having made three comebacks in South Korea last year. Their first digital single 'Only Today I LOVE YOU', released in January of last year, topped numerous annual charts. Their 4th mini album 'No Genre' and 5th mini album 'The Action' each surpassed one million first-week sales (the number of copies sold within the first week of release), achieving three consecutive million-selling releases.

Having continued to deliver strong results by personally crafting music that resonates with listeners, anticipation for the group's first full-length album is running high. The six members are set to carry out promotional activities for the new release through a sequential schedule.
