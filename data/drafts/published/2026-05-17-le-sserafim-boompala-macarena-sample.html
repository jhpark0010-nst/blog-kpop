LE SSERAFIM has dropped hints about 'BOOMPALA,' the title track of their second full-length album. Members Kim Chaewon and Kazuha appeared on tvN's variety show 'Amazing Saturday,' which aired on May 16, ahead of the album's release on May 22.

On the show, the two members dressed as characters from the popular animated series 'Crayon Shin-chan' — Shin-chan and Shiro — for a 'best friends special,' showcasing warm chemistry throughout the episode.

Ahead of the May 22 release of their second full album 'PUREFLOW' pt.1, they shared spoilers about the new record to build anticipation. They revealed that 'the title track BOOMPALA samples Macarena, and the choreography also incorporates Macarena's signature point move.' The 'Amazing Saturday' panelists responded, saying 'it'll be easy to follow along' and 'even Dong-yeop hyun, the oldest one here, will enjoy it.' The duo was sharing that the track samples 'Macarena,' a global hit that held the No. 1 spot on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 for 14 consecutive weeks. When Kim Chaewon and Kazuha gave a brief performance preview, the entire cast begged them to show more.

The pair's playful variety show charms also shone through. In a game where players had to guess song titles letter by letter, they fumbled and drew good-natured scolding from their teammates, drawing laughs. Kazuha, even after being named last place in a dictation segment, struck a pose fully focused on promoting 'BOOMPALA,' adding to the fun. Kim Chaewon caught a hint about a 'Yes' section that no one else had picked up on, earning her moment in the spotlight.

Wrapping up the filming, they shared: 'The games were fun, and we got to eat delicious food, so it was a great time. We're especially glad we got to show everyone the BOOMPALA stage. Please look forward to it a lot.'

LE SSERAFIM's second full album 'PUREFLOW' pt.1 — featuring members Kim Chaewon, Sakura, Huh Yunjin, Kazuha, and Hong Eunchae — will be officially released on May 22 at 1 p.m. The album includes a total of 11 tracks, led by the highly addictive title track 'BOOMPALA.' The new album captures the five members' changes and growth as they face their fears, as well as their deepened bond with one another.
