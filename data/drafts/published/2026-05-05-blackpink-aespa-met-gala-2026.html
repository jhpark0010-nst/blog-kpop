BLACKPINK and aespa members Karina and Ningning were among the K-pop stars who made a splash at the 2026 Met Gala, the world's largest fashion charity event. The Metropolitan Museum of Art Costume Institute Gala photo call took place on May 4 (local time) at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York.

The Met Gala, which began in 1948, is held every year on the first Monday of May and is renowned as a global fashion event and charity fundraiser attended by a host of celebrities. This year's Met Gala was also attended by global stars including Beyoncé, Sam Smith, and Doja Cat.

All four BLACKPINK members drew attention with outfits that showcased their individual styles. Jennie and Jisoo completed their looks with sky-blue and pink dresses respectively, each styled with an updo. Rosé and Lisa wore dresses in contrasting colors — black and white.

In addition to BLACKPINK, aespa's Karina and Ningning also attended the Met Gala, along with Lee Jae, who provided the vocal performance for Huntrix — the girl group in the Netflix animated film 'K-Pop Demon Hunters' — and actor Ahn Hyo-seop, who voiced the character Jinwoo in the film.
