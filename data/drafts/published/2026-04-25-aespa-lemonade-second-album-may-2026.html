aespa is set to open a new chapter with their second full-length album, 'LEMONADE'. According to SM Entertainment on April 20, the album is scheduled for release on May 29, 2026, and will feature a total of 10 tracks. This marks aespa's first full album in approximately two years, following 'Armageddon' in 2024.

Since their debut, aespa has been beloved for their distinctive musical identity, often described as a sharp, bold 'metallic' sound. Last year, the group showcased an expanded musical spectrum through the single 'Dirty Work' and their sixth mini-album 'Rich Man', making fans eager to see what new attempts and changes 'LEMONADE' will bring.

Pre-orders for aespa's second full album 'LEMONADE' are now open starting April 20 at various online and offline music stores.
