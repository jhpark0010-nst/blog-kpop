K-pop group AHOF is keeping the momentum of their Asia tour alive with a concert in Kuala Lumpur on July 4. The group takes the stage at JIOSPACE in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia for '2026 AHOF 1ST TOUR : THE FIRST SPARK,' meeting fans in the city.

'THE FIRST SPARK' is AHOF's first-ever Asia tour, which kicked off in Seoul in May before successfully wrapping stops in Osaka and Tokyo. Kuala Lumpur marks the fourth concert city on the tour, where the group will share a special time with local fans.

At the concert, AHOF will perform a diverse lineup of stages featuring previously released tracks as well as 'Sugar High,' the pre-release single from their upcoming third mini album, and album track 'Our Story,' set to be released soon. The group is expected to prove their standing as a global act through solid performances and spectacular staging.

After the concert, AHOF will dive into full comeback activities. The group is set to release their third mini album 'RUN TO YOU' on July 8. They are currently rolling out a variety of promotional content — from concept photos to a highlight medley — building anticipation among fans around the world.
