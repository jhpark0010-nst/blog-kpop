ILLIT is ready to become everyone's favorite. The group releases their 4th mini album 'MAMIHLAPINATAPAI' on April 30 at 6 PM KST, delivering music that feels unfamiliar yet irresistible, fresh yet instantly familiar.

The title track 'It's Me' captures ILLIT's bold and confident charm in full. At the moment when defining a relationship with someone you like becomes a dilemma, the members declare without hesitation, 'You know your favorite is me!' This cheeky directness completes ILLIT's signature lovable quality.

'It's Me' marks ILLIT's first foray into the techno genre. The intense beat that pounds the heart, an exciting melody, and a fast-paced, sweeping arrangement stimulate the listener's dopamine. Singer-songwriter youra and The Deep translated ILLIT's quirky, lively personality into sensory-rich lyrics.

ILLIT's appeal reaches its peak on stage. On April 28, the second teaser for the 'It's Me' music video revealed part of the performance. Unique movements — from lively footwork to poses radiating confident aura — catch the eye, while the members' striking visuals and playful facial expressions add to the fun. Their performance, which freely shifts between cute and chic, delivers an immersive experience that keeps eyes glued to the screen. The five members' explosive energy will be showcased for the first time on Mnet's M Countdown on April 30.

Alongside the comeback, a special festival for all generations will be held. On May 5, Children's Day, the 'ILLIT Seoul Children's Grand Park Festival' will take place, with 'It's Me' as the main theme. Various experience booths will be set up for fans and all visitors to enjoy freely, and ILLIT will perform their new song live to give the audience an unforgettable day.

A pop-up store to immerse fans in the new album's vibe will also open from May 3 to 8 on the first floor of the HYBE headquarters in Yongsan-gu, Seoul. The pop-up will display the outfits the members actually wore during concept photo shoots and props that appear in the music video. Merchandise reflecting ILLIT's kitschy yet trendy charm and various events for visitors are also planned, drawing fans to the venue.
