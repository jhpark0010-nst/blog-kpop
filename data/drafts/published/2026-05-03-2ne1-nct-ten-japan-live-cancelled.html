The joint live concert by 2NE1 (CL, Sandara Park, Park Bom, and Minzy) and NCT's TEN has been abruptly cancelled. The show, titled 'K*Pop Masterz Presents 2NE1 & TEN SPECIAL LIVE in JAPAN 2026,' was set to take place on May 20 and 21 at K Arena Yokohama.

The organizers posted an official cancellation notice on their website on May 1. In the statement, they said, 'We have confirmed that it will be difficult to fully provide the necessary stage production equipment, and we have regrettably decided to halt the show.'

The organizers also stated, 'We are looking into arranging an alternative event, but we are unable to provide confirmed details at this time. We will first proceed with cancelling all performances and processing ticket refunds.'

They further added that travel expenses, transportation costs, and accommodation fees incurred due to the cancellation would not be compensated.

The two acts had been widely promoted together, with the event billed as 'a concert realized through years of mutual respect between a legend who has led K-pop and a globally active, highly skilled artist.' Despite the extensive promotion, the show ultimately did not go ahead.
