All 13 members of SEVENTEEN — S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Woozi, The8, Mingyu, DK, Seungkwan, Vernon, and Dino — have renewed their contracts with their current agency, Pledis Entertainment.

On July 13, Pledis Entertainment announced, 'SEVENTEEN have come together in agreement to sign a second contract renewal with us.' The agency added, 'All members not currently serving in the military have completed the signing, and members currently fulfilling their military service will sign sequentially in accordance with their service conditions.'

Sharing the news, SEVENTEEN stated, 'We are happy to be able to deliver good news to our CARATs (CARAT — the group's fandom name). Under the belief that there is nothing we cannot achieve together, and a shared desire to go further as a team, all 13 of us have decided to stay together.' They continued, 'We ask our fans to join us as members of TEAM SVT in our promise to challenge eternity.'

Pledis Entertainment also said, 'We plan to continue providing systematic and full support for SEVENTEEN's group, unit, and individual activities.' The agency added, 'We will faithfully serve as a reliable supporter and partner so that each member can further express their individuality and strengths, and so that the team's positive energy can spread even more widely across the world. We look forward to the day SEVENTEEN stands before you again as all 13, and we will lay the groundwork for a successful resumption of full-group activities.'

SEVENTEEN debuted in 2015 and signed their first early contract renewal with Pledis Entertainment in 2021. It is remarkable for a large-scale group to have once again achieved a full contract renewal with not a single member departure.
