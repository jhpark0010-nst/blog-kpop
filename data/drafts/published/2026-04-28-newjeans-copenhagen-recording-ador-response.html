NewJeans' label ADOR responded on April 27 to claims that the group is preparing a new album in Copenhagen, Denmark, describing the activity as 'part of the pre-production process to capture NewJeans' new musical narrative.'

ADOR added, 'The members are preparing their activities according to their current condition and schedules optimized for each of them,' and stated, 'We plan to officially inform you about the members' future plans at the best time.'

Recently, online communities have seen a series of reported sightings of NewJeans members Hanni, Haerin, and Hyein in Copenhagen. This was followed by speculation that NewJeans may be recording a new album after a booking under the name 'ADOR' was found on the schedule of a recording studio located in Copenhagen.

NewJeans, who had been in a dispute with their label, saw Hanni, Haerin, and Hyein return to ADOR first in November of last year. Minji's situation has not yet been decided, and Danielle continues a legal dispute following her departure from the group.
