TWICE is holding a pop-up store at Flat1399 Studio in Gangdong-gu, Seoul, from July 2 to 12, according to their agency JYP Entertainment. The event celebrates the group's upcoming Seoul finale concerts.

The pop-up store is being held to mark the Seoul finale of TWICE's world tour 'THIS IS FOR,' which takes place at KSPO Dome in Seoul on July 10 through 12. Including the finale performances, the world tour wraps up across 44 cities worldwide with a total of 81 shows — the largest scale in the group's history.

The pop-up store features a variety of merchandise and exhibition spaces centered on TWICE's official character, TWICE Labubu. Fan-participatory content has also been prepared, including a random song quiz and social media (SNS) events.

Girl group BbGirls will release a new single, 'BODY WAVE,' on July 16, according to their agency GLG. The track marks their first new song in one year and two months since their remake 'Hope' (originally by Byun Jin-sub), released in May of last year. The new song is a summer track aimed at the hot season, carrying BbGirls' bright energy.

Singer Jang Hanaeum will release their fifth digital single, 'I Can't Be Nervous Around a Friend,' on July 2 at 6 p.m., according to agency ESN. The new track is a rock song featuring a clean band sound and a refreshing vocal tone, with Jang Hanaeum participating in the lyrics. The song captures the process of recognizing love while facing unfamiliar feelings toward a friend. The single includes two tracks: the title song and 'i&i.' Jang Hanaeum debuted in 2023 and expanded their fanbase last year by appearing on Mnet's audition program 'Boys Planet.'
