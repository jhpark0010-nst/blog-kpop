aespa has dropped their pre-release single 'WDA (Whole Different Animal)' featuring G-Dragon, heating up anticipation for their upcoming comeback. The group — consisting of Karina, Winter, Giselle, and Ningning — released the track across major music platforms at 6 PM KST on May 11.

'WDA (Whole Different Animal)' is a hip-hop-based dance track driven by a grand synth bass and a heavy hook that delivers an overwhelming impact. The lyrics reflect aespa's growth and mark the opening of a new chapter in their lore (universe). G-Dragon participated in the song as a featured artist and also handled the rap-making for his own part, completing a powerful synergy with aespa.

The music video, released simultaneously on the SMTOWN YouTube channel, features beings who wear aespa's faces but are clearly not aespa themselves — set in an era where the boundary between reality and the digital world has grown so blurred that authenticity can no longer be proven. Amid this chaos and conflict, aespa ultimately breaks through the fracture (Complaexity) and proves their own existence once more.

Since their debut, aespa has built a narrative in which real selves and digital selves coexist, with ae-aespa (AI-aespa) — their virtual alter egos — serving as a core pillar of their lore. The music video was designed to naturally confront viewers with the question 'What is real?' through direction that captures the unsettling sensation of a world where familiar images are endlessly replicated and consumed, and the line between original and copy, reality and virtual, has faded.

Additionally, starting at 5 PM KST on May 11 — one hour before the single's release — aespa held a 'WDA Countdown Live' on their YouTube and TikTok channels, talking about the new song and communicating with fans to amplify excitement for their second full album comeback.

aespa's second full album 'LEMONADE' will be fully released digitally at 1 PM KST on May 29, and will also be available as a physical album.
