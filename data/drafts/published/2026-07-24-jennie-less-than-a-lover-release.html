BLACKPINK member and solo artist Jennie is releasing a summer song set to color this season with a refreshing vibe. On July 24 at 1 PM KST, she drops the single 'Less than a Lover' — both the audio and music video — across global music platforms and her official YouTube channel.

'Less than a Lover' is a track that Jennie personally co-wrote and co-composed while thinking about summer. The song captures the excitement and lingering feeling of a relationship that sits somewhere between lovers and friends, expressed through the phrase 'Less than a Lover.' It is an alternative pop track that paints a dreamy, summer-night romance over a languid texture created by lo-fi guitar and a vintage electric keyboard.

Jennie had previously performed the new song at Poland's Open'er Festival, one of the world's major global festivals, introducing it as 'a song I wrote thinking about summer.' Having already generated major buzz with that festival preview, 'Less than a Lover' is now officially released today, July 24.

Jennie was deeply involved in the overall production of the new single, including directing the music video, infusing it with her own unique aesthetic sensibility. Filmed in Spain, the music video captures Jennie's summer sensibility in a bright and refreshing mood throughout both the song and visuals, set to cement her image as a 'Visionary' artist.

Teaser images and music video teasers trended in real time across various social media platforms immediately after release, with global fans pouring out positive reactions and expressing high anticipation for the new track. Furthermore, after the pre-save page for 'Less than a Lover' opened on Spotify and Apple Music, a surge of traffic temporarily crashed the sites, proving the worldwide interest in Jennie's new music.

Having already demonstrated her global popularity with 'Dracula (JENNIE Remix),' a collaboration with Tame Impala that held the No. 5 spot on the Billboard Hot 100 and No. 1 on the Pop Airplay chart, Jennie continues her relentless musical journey with 'Less than a Lover,' aiming once again to top global charts. Following this release, the 1996-born artist will also take the stage at major festivals including Lollapalooza Chicago in the United States and SUMMER SONIC 2026 in Japan.
