TVXQ's U-Know Yunho will hold his first solo concert at the Ticketlink Live Arena in Olympic Park, Songpa-gu, Seoul, from July 17 to 19, SM Entertainment announced on April 30.

The concert is titled 'U-KNOW PROJECT 26 : SCENE#1' and is themed around a journey of self-discovery, exploring Yunho's own ego and identity. The show is designed to provide audiences with a deeply immersive experience.

Going beyond the format of a conventional concert, the show is planned as a multi-genre entertainment production that incorporates elements of musicals and theater.

U-Know Yunho's group TVXQ held concerts at Nissan Stadium in Yokohama, Japan on April 25 and 26.
