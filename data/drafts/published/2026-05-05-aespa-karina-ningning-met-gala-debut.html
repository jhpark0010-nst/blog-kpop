aespa members Karina and Ningning made their Met Gala debut on May 4 (local time) at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York. The two attended the world's largest fashion charity event as brand ambassadors for Prada and Gucci, respectively, walking the red carpet for the first time at the prestigious annual gala.

Karina wore a custom white satin dress featuring a tablier neckline adorned with jet beads and silver crystal embroidery. She paired the look with a clutch in the same fabric, silver leather sandals, and Prada Fine Jewelry Coeur Vivant earrings set with white gold, peridot, amethyst, and diamonds, further highlighting her elegant charm.

Ningning wore a Gucci couture black dress embodying a layered femininity where delicacy and strength coexist. Inspired by the organic forms of nature, the gown featured three-dimensional pleated ruffles and black crystal embroidery details that completed a dramatic silhouette, simultaneously expressing lightness and volume through a design that revealed and concealed the body with movement.

This year's Met Gala was held under the theme 'Superfine: Tailoring Black Style.' Main host Anna Wintour was joined by co-hosts Beyoncé, Nicole Kidman, and Venus Williams. Hollywood actors Hugh Jackman, Amanda Seyfried, Emily Blunt, Julianne Moore, and Naomi Watts, pop stars Katy Perry and Doja Cat, and approximately 450 or more global stars from the worlds of fashion, art, sports, and politics attended, adding to the event's splendor.

Meanwhile, aespa will release the pre-release single 'WDA (Whole Different Animal)' across music platforms on May 11 at 6 p.m. The group's second full-length album 'LEMONADE,' which includes 'WDA' among a total of 10 tracks, will follow on May 29 at 1 p.m.
