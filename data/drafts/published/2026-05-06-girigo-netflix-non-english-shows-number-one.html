Netflix original series 'Girigo,' led by a cast of rookie actors, has topped the global Netflix chart for non-English shows. According to the Netflix Top 10 data compiled on the official Netflix site Tudum, 'Girigo' recorded 7.5 million views (calculated by dividing total watch time by runtime) for the week of April 27 to May 3, ranking first among non-English shows.

The series entered the Top 10 in 64 countries, including South Korea, Japan, Thailand, Singapore, Egypt, Qatar, and Brazil.

'Girigo' is a story about five high school students desperately trying to escape a curse embedded in a wish-granting app called 'Girigo.' The show drew attention as a Korean occult school thriller featuring fresh faces including actors Jeon So-young, Kang Mi-na, Hyun Woo-seok, Lee Hyo-je, and Baek Seon-ho.

The series climbed to 4th place in the non-English show category just three days after its release, then reached the global number one spot in its second week.

Also in the non-English shows category, 'Sold Out Today As Well' placed 3rd. In the English-language films category, 'K-Pop Demon Hunters' came in at 5th place with 3.9 million views, marking its 46th consecutive week in the Top 10.
