Blizzard Entertainment announced on June 26 that K-pop girl group LE SSERAFIM will perform at the closing ceremony of BlizzCon 2026, set to take place in the United States this September.

BlizzCon is Blizzard's annual fan event and new title showcase. This year's edition runs from September 12 to 13 (local US time) at the Anaheim Convention Center in Anaheim, California.

LE SSERAFIM will take the main stage on September 13 for the closing event, ahead of the group's upcoming US tour.

LE SSERAFIM previously collaborated with Blizzard's flagship title Overwatch in 2023, performing their English single 'Perfect Night' as part of that partnership.

Blizzard noted that tickets for BlizzCon 2026 are currently sold out, but additional tickets may be purchased through the resale site Tixr.
