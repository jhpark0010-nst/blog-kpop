YG Entertainment's chief producer Yang Hyun-suk has revealed the 2026 activity plans for BABYMONSTER and TREASURE. On April 30, YG posted an official announcement titled 'BABYMONSTER, TREASURE, AND UPCOMING ROOKIES | YG ANNOUNCEMENT' on its official blog, introducing tracks from BABYMONSTER's third mini album 'CHOOM,' set for release on May 4, and sharing plans for TREASURE's new album.

The first track on BABYMONSTER's third mini album is 'MOON,' a dark yet swag-filled hip-hop song. Yang said, 'YG has recently been actively running song camps,' and added, 'This is our first collaboration with the overseas production team behind this track — they are a team that handles sound design in a very refined and distinctive way,' heightening curiosity about the song.

The title track 'CHOOM' has been in development for a long time in order to deliver a performance worthy of its name. While choreography is typically commissioned from two to three teams, this time the number was exceptionally expanded to ten teams. Yang himself, who has been behind the choreography for numerous YG hit songs, directly participated in the chorus section to elevate the quality. Also introduced were 'I LIKE IT,' an upbeat track with a fast tempo, and 'LOCKED IN,' a slow song that highlights the members' vocal abilities.

Yang also announced that a total of four music videos will be released sequentially, starting with the title track 'CHOOM.' Following the new album, a digital single tentatively titled 'SUGAR HONEY' is planned for June, with music videos for album tracks 'I LIKE IT' and 'MOON' scheduled for July and August respectively.

TREASURE's new mini album, which will open a new chapter for the group, has been confirmed for release on June 1 and will include four hip-hop-based tracks. Yang said, 'TREASURE debuted during the pandemic and faced many difficulties in the global market,' and expressed confidence by adding, 'This album is personally my favorite result we have produced.'

At the end of the video, Yang said, 'BABYMONSTER's album, which they have worked on with producers for the past year, will be released soon, and TREASURE's album will follow about a month later,' and expressed his hope that 'the joy, emotion, and excitement beyond what I expect will be delivered to fans like a gift.' He also added, 'We will also be sequentially revealing the new artists that YG has been developing.'
