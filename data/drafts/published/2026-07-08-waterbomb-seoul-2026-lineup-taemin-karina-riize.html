Waterbomb Seoul 2026 has officially revealed its final lineup. According to organizer MadeOn, the festival will be held over three days — July 24 to 26 — at KINTEX in Ilsan, Goyang, Gyeonggi Province. The most notable highlight of the announced lineup is the large-scale participation of K-pop artists.

Taemin, aespa's Karina, and RIIZE are among the top-tier K-pop acts set to take the stage, expected to deliver a refreshing and powerful energy throughout the event.

The international DJ lineup also adds considerable weight to the festival. Diplo, the globally renowned producer widely known for his work on BLACKPINK's 'JUMP,' has confirmed his appearance in Korea. Joining him are global electronic music figures Nightmare and Medix, promising an unprecedented musical spectacle spanning diverse genres.

A representative from organizer MadeOn stated, 'This year's Waterbomb is being ambitiously prepared as a high-end multi-cultural festival where music is at the core, and where fashion, food, and lifestyle content can all be enjoyed in one place.' They added, 'By providing an unforgettable multi-sensory experience for both domestic audiences and global visitors coming from abroad, we aim to solidify our standing as a global festival brand.'
