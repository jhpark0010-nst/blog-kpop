BTS — comprising j-hope, Suga, Jimin, Jin, RM, Jungkook, and V — will attend the 2026 American Music Awards. According to MyDaily's reporting, the group is set to appear at the ceremony on May 25 (local time) at 5 p.m. at the MGM Grand Garden Arena in Las Vegas, USA.

At the 2026 American Music Awards, BTS has been nominated in three categories: the top honor Artist of the Year, Song of the Summer, and Best Male K-Pop Artist.

The American Music Awards is one of the three major U.S. pop music awards shows alongside the Grammy Awards and the Billboard Music Awards. This year marks BTS's fifth attendance at the AMAs, having previously attended in 2017, 2018, 2019, and 2021. Notably, in 2021, they became the first Asian act to win Artist of the Year. Having resumed full-group activities following their military service discharge, anticipation is building over whether they can lift the trophy once again.

The group released their fifth studio album 'ARIRANG' in March, reaching No. 1 on the Billboard 200 for three consecutive weeks. A group album topping the Billboard 200 for three straight weeks is the first such achievement since Mumford & Sons' 2012 release, and BTS is the only Korean act to have held the No. 1 spot for a third consecutive week.

BTS is currently in the middle of a North America tour and is set to perform at the halftime show of the 2026 FIFA World Cup final in July.
