aespa's Karina captivated fans with her elegant backless dress look at the Met Gala. On May 6, Karina shared behind-the-scenes photos from the world's largest fashion charity event, the Met Gala, through her personal social media account.

In the photos, Karina wore a luxurious silver-tone dress that exuded a refined and graceful atmosphere. The classic silhouette paired with a black shawl completed an elegant mood, and her clean, understated styling drew immediate attention.

What stood out in particular was her look from behind. The bold backless design highlighted her smooth back line and slender silhouette, revealing a striking contrast to her poised front look. An upswept hairstyle and drop earrings added to the overall sense of sophistication.

In the post, Karina included a devil emoji alongside a mention of the brand she currently serves as ambassador for — a detail being interpreted as a playful nod to the film 'The Devil Wears Prada.'

Meanwhile, aespa is set to release their second full-length album, LEMONADE, on the 29th. The release is expected to showcase further-developed storytelling rooted in the group's signature worldview concept, as well as continued musical growth.
