NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang has confirmed he is a fan of K-pop artist Hwasa. On June 7, the official account of tvN's variety show 'You Quiz on the Block' posted a preview clip titled 'Jensen Huang Shoutout to Hwasa!! Park Jung-min, Kim Nam-gil — is Jensen Huang next?'

In the clip, host Yoo Jae-suk opened the conversation by saying to Jensen Huang, 'I heard you listen to a lot of K-pop while you work.' Jensen Huang responded, 'Who wouldn't love 'Golden' by Jungkook?' — presenting himself as a K-pop fan.

He then mentioned Hwasa specifically, saying, 'I like 'Good Goodbye.' Her new song 'So Cute' is also a great track.' The fact that he named specific song titles drew considerable attention.

When Yoo Jae-suk reacted with envy, saying, 'Hwasa is going to blow up again. Lucky Hwasa,' Jensen Huang praised her as an artist, stating, 'Hwasa has outstanding vocal ability and is also a great dancer.'
