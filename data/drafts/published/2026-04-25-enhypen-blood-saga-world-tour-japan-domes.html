ENHYPEN — members Jungwon, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-ki — will hold 'ENHYPEN WORLD TOUR BLOOD SAGA IN JAPAN' from December 2026 through February 2027, according to their agency Belift Lab on April 24. The Japan leg spans 8 shows across 4 cities, marking a major expansion of the group's touring scale.

The shows kick off at Tokyo Dome on December 1–2, followed by Vantelin Dome Nagoya on December 26–27, Mizuho PayPay Dome Fukuoka on February 6–7, 2027, and Kyocera Dome Osaka on February 19–20, 2027. These four venues — collectively known as Japan's 'Big 4 Domes' — each hold 40,000 to 50,000 seats, reflecting ENHYPEN's massive local popularity and ticket-selling power.

Tour momentum is also building in Latin America. An additional show at Arena CDMX in Mexico City has been confirmed. Originally, only a single performance on July 11 was scheduled, but tickets sold out rapidly during pre-sales, leading to a second date on July 12. The added show marks a strong reception for the group's first-ever visit to Latin America.

Since their debut in November 2020, ENHYPEN have completed three world tours, demonstrating explosive growth. During their previous tour, 'ENHYPEN WORLD TOUR WALK THE LINE,' they became the K-pop boy group to tour three Japanese dome cities in the shortest time after debut, and also performed at Tokyo's Ajinomoto Stadium and Osaka's Yanmar Stadium Nagai. Building on that foundation, ENHYPEN is expected to break their own audience mobilization records and further cement their position at the top of the global stage.

ENHYPEN's fourth world tour, BLOOD SAGA, opens next month with shows at KSPO DOME in Olympic Park, Songpa-gu, Seoul on May 1–3. The full tour then moves through 8 cities in Latin America and North America, 3 cities in Asia, 5 cities in Europe, and the Japan dome run, totaling 31 shows across 21 cities through March 2027.
