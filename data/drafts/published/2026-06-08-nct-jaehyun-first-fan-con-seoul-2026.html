NCT's Jaehyun kicked off his fan-con (fan concert) tour in Seoul. From June 6 to 7, he held the JAEHYUN FAN-CON TOUR 'MONO' in SEOUL at Jamsil Indoor Stadium in Seoul. Both nights sold out, drawing approximately 13,000 fans in total.

Born in 1997, Jaehyun enlisted in November 2024 and was discharged from military service this past May. This fan-con marks his first performance since his discharge and his first solo show in roughly one year and eight months.

At the event, Jaehyun performed a variety of stages accompanied by a live band, including his solo debut track 'Smoke'. He also gave the world premiere of unreleased songs, notably 'Timeless' and '99 Degrees'.

Wrapping up the show, Jaehyun shared: 'This was a performance I prepared because, as I begin activities in earnest, I wanted to greet my fans first. It was a time when I could fully feel all the love you have sent me — from before my enlistment, through my military service, and even now after my discharge.'

He added: 'I realized once again, deeply, just how purely happy I am when I am with you all, and how joyful it is to share a stage together. I will run hard again going forward so that I can repay the time you spent cheering for me and waiting.'

Meanwhile, Jaehyun debuted in 2016 with NCT U and NCT 127, and this year marks the 10th anniversary of his debut. In 2023 he carried out unit activities as DoJaeJung, and in 2024 he made his solo debut.
