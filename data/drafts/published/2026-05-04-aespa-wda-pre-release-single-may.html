aespa will release their pre-release single 'WDA (Whole Different Animal)' on May 11, 2026. The track serves as an advance release from the group's second full-length album, LEMONADE.

According to SM Entertainment on May 4, 'WDA' will become available on major music platforms at 6 p.m. KST on May 11. The music video will also be released simultaneously through the YouTube SMTOWN channel and other platforms.

Also on May 4 at midnight, a 'WDA' teaser image titled 'The Creature' was revealed through aespa's official social media channels. The teaser depicts chaos breaking out in the aespa universe due to the emergence of a singularity, and features the creature born from that event alongside the members, captured in visuals that carry an otherworldly yet subtly tense atmosphere.

Meanwhile, aespa's second full-length album LEMONADE will have all of its tracks released digitally on May 29 at 1 p.m. KST, with a physical album release to follow as well.
