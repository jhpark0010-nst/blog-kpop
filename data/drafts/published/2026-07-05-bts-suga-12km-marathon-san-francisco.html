BTS member SUGA ran 12 kilometers the day after a concert performance, with his consistent running habit once again drawing attention as the secret behind his remarkable stamina. The feat took place during his ongoing world tour schedule.

On July 5, the YouTube channel Bangtan TV uploaded a new vlog titled 'Runner SUGA, Feeling the Heat in San Francisco | SUGA's VLOG', giving fans an inside look at his off-day activity.

In the video, SUGA participates in a marathon held in San Francisco, USA, in the middle of his concert schedule. Before the start, he shared his mindset, saying, 'Let's stick to our own pace and not push too hard.'

He maintained a stable pace of around 5 minutes per kilometer in the early stages, but hit a wall during the toughest section of the course — the uphill stretch. Even while breathing heavily, SUGA kept pushing forward one step at a time.

His finishing record was 12km in just over 1 hour and 4 minutes. After crossing the finish line, SUGA opened up honestly, saying, 'When I hit the uphill, I thought to myself, why am I even doing this?' He then added, 'But once it was over, I felt a little better,' conveying the sense of pride that came after pushing through the difficult moments.
