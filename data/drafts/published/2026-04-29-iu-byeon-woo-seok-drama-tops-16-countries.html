IU and Byeon Woo-seok's romance in the MBC Friday-Saturday drama '21st Century Grand Prince's Wife' is captivating viewers around the world. Since its premiere, the show has ranked at the very top of both viewership ratings and buzz for three consecutive weeks, and it continues to hold the #1 spot in the non-English global category on Disney+ TV shows.

The drama is currently ranked #1 in a total of 16 countries, generating enormous buzz worldwide. The romance between Seong Hee-joo (played by IU) and Lee An-daegun (played by Byeon Woo-seok) is drawing intense interest from domestic and international viewers alike.

The show was selected as the #1 most-buzzed drama in the TV-OTT category for the 4th week of April 2026, as announced by GoodData Corporation's FUNdex index, maintaining the top position for three consecutive weeks. IU and Byeon Woo-seok are holding the #1 and #2 spots respectively in the cast buzz rankings.

Episode 6 recorded a nationwide viewership rating of 11.2% and a 20-54 age demographic rating of 5.9%, continuously breaking the show's own all-time high. The drama ranks #1 among all Saturday programs in the Seoul metropolitan area and the 20-54 demographic. On OTT platforms, it holds #5 globally and #1 in the non-English category on Disney+ TOP 10 TV shows according to FlixPatrol, ranking #1 in 16 countries including South Korea, Japan, and Taiwan, and also performing well in Brazil, Colombia, and Costa Rica.

Episodes 5 and 6 depicted the deepening relationship between Seong Hee-joo and Lee An-daegun after they overcame a crisis in their contract marriage. A kissing scene on a yacht has been widely talked about across online communities.

In episodes 7 and 8, wedding preparations for Seong Hee-joo and Lee An-daegun — now with the king's approval — are set to begin in earnest, promising an even more romantic atmosphere. Anticipation among viewers is growing as the couple's love deepens. Episode 7 of MBC's '21st Century Grand Prince's Wife' airs on Friday, May 1 at 9:40 PM.
