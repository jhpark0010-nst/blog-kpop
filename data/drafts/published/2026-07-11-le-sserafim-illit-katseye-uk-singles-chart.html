HYBE girl groups LE SSERAFIM, ILLIT, and KATSEYE have charted on the UK Official Singles Top 100 for four consecutive weeks with their collaboration track 'Iconic By Mistake.' According to the Official Charts dated July 10 (local time), the song landed at No. 59, dropping fifteen spots from the previous week.

'Iconic By Mistake' is an alternative pop track characterized by an intense beat and unconventional sound. Its highly addictive hook carries the lyric 'thanks to your hatred, I accidentally became iconic,' adding to the fun of listening.

Also on the UK Official Singles Top 100, 'Golden' — the original soundtrack from the Netflix animated film 'K-Pop Demon Hunters' — entered at No. 53, marking its 55th consecutive week on the chart.

On the UK Official Albums Top 100, BTS's fifth studio album ARIRANG climbed sixteen spots from the previous week to reach No. 21, marking its 16th consecutive week on the chart.

ARIRANG also recorded No. 2 on Spotify's Weekly Top Albums Global chart, the world's largest music streaming platform.
