NewJeans member Minji is reportedly in positive discussions about returning to her agency ADOR. On May 7, an ADOR representative told MyDaily that 'internal discussions regarding future activities are ongoing' in relation to Minji's situation.

The representative added, 'There is no separate position we can share at this time regarding specific details,' but noted that 'overall, discussions are continuing in a positive direction.'

Earlier that same day, several photos were posted on the NewJeans official account along with the message 'Happy Minji Day.' The photos showed the process of kneading dough, making cookies, and packaging them.

Recently, Minji visited her own birthday cafe and gifted fans with homemade cookies, photo booth pictures, and handwritten letters. ADOR posted the entire process directly on the official account, raising expectations for Minji's return to NewJeans.

For context, the NewJeans members notified ADOR of their exclusive contract termination on November 29, 2024, citing a complete breakdown of trust in the agency. ADOR subsequently filed a lawsuit to confirm the validity of the exclusive contracts, and ADOR won at the first trial. The members did not appeal the ruling.

Members Haerin, Hyein, and Hanni have each returned to ADOR in turn, while Danielle was dismissed and is currently facing a breach-of-contract and damages lawsuit worth 43.1 billion won. Minji remains at the discussion stage with ADOR.
