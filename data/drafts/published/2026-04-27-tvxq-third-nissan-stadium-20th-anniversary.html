TVXQ successfully completed their third solo concert at Nissan Stadium, drawing a combined total of 130,000 fans over two days. The group held '20th Anniversary LIVE IN NISSAN STADIUM ~RED OCEAN~' at Yokohama Nissan Stadium on April 25 and 26, 2026.

TVXQ became the first overseas artist to perform at Nissan Stadium back in 2013, and in 2018 they made history as the first act to hold a three-day run at the venue. With this third Nissan Stadium concert, they have now achieved the record of being the 'first and most frequent' overseas artist to perform there. This also extends their own record set in 2025 for the most Tokyo Dome and nationwide dome performances by an overseas artist.

During the concert, TVXQ performed a total of 31 songs over approximately three and a half hours. They made use of a sliding deck to expand the stadium into a single unified stage, with lighting, lifts, and pyrotechnic effects deployed throughout the show. Fans responded by waving the T-shaped red light sticks that are a symbol of TVXQ.

TVXQ expressed their heartfelt gratitude, saying, 'This was the best time of 2026. The third Nissan Stadium was something we achieved together with everyone here.' They added, 'It feels like a dream to be able to stand at Nissan Stadium again. If we can make you happy through our music, we will keep finding the strength to sing going forward.'
