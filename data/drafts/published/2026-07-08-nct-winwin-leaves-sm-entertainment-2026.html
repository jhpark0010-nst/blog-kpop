NCT's WinWin is parting ways with SM Entertainment after 10 years. The agency officially announced on July 8 that WinWin's exclusive contract will conclude effective July 9, 2026, and that he will end all NCT activities.

SM Entertainment stated: 'After sufficient discussion with WinWin, we have agreed to conclude the exclusive contract as of July 9, 2026. Accordingly, WinWin will end all NCT activities.'

The agency added: 'We are grateful to WinWin, who has been with us for over 10 years since his trainee days, and we will cheer on WinWin's future as he begins a new journey.'

WinWin debuted as a member of NCT 127 in 2016 and also began activities as a member of WayV in 2019. He subsequently established a personal studio in China in 2021 and appeared in the drama '25 Hours of Romance' in 2023.

Below is the full text of SM Entertainment's official notice. Hello, this is SM Entertainment. We sincerely thank the fans who always send generous support to NCT. After sufficient discussion with our artist WinWin, we have agreed to conclude the exclusive contract as of July 9, 2026. Accordingly, WinWin will end all NCT activities. We are grateful to WinWin, who has been with us for over 10 years since his trainee days, and we will cheer on WinWin's future as he begins a new journey. Thank you.
