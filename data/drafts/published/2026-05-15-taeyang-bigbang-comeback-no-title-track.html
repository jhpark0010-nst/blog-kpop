Taeyang of BIGBANG shared an update on the group's upcoming comeback during a video posted to Epik High's channel on May 14. The video, titled 'Former Coworker Gathering. Taeyang,' features the singer sitting down with Epik High to talk about behind-the-scenes stories from past activities as well as recent news.

During the video, Tablo asked Taeyang for a progress update on BIGBANG's comeback album and requested a spoiler about new songs. Taeyang responded, 'We need to finish it now. Honestly, we need to finish it now,' adding, 'There are several songs, but no track has been decided yet.' Tablo lamented, 'For something like this, you have to call the leader,' to which Taeyang joked, 'G-Dragon probably doesn't know either,' and laughed, 'We're really in trouble.'

Taeyang then called G-Dragon directly on the spot. After Tablo greeted G-Dragon and asked, 'So the title track hasn't been picked yet?' G-Dragon replied, 'It probably hasn't been picked yet.' When Tablo pressed back, 'What do you mean it hasn't been picked — is someone other than you making the BIGBANG album?' G-Dragon explained, 'We keep working on songs, and whichever turns out best becomes the title track.'

Earlier, on April 20, Taeyang announced through his agency that he would be releasing his fourth full-length solo album. The agency revealed album images via official accounts and confirmed a release date of May 18 at 6 p.m. This album marks his comeback roughly three years after 'Down to Earth,' released in 2023, and has heightened anticipation among fans.

Taeyang debuted as the main vocalist of BIGBANG and earned wide recognition for his vocal ability and performances. He has since solidified his standing as a solo artist with hit songs including 'Eyes, Nose, Lips' and 'Ringa Linga.' He married actress Min Hyo-rin in 2018 and welcomed a son in 2021.
