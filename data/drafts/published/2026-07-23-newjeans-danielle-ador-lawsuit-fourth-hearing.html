As NewJeans marks its 4th debut anniversary and moves toward a comeback with a four-member lineup, former member Danielle and ex-ADOR CEO Min Hee-jin continue to face a damages lawsuit worth approximately 33 billion KRW. On July 23, the Seoul Central District Court's Civil Division 31 (presiding judge Nam In-su) held the fourth pleading session in the damages suit filed by ADOR against Danielle, her family, and Min Hee-jin.

ADOR originally filed the lawsuit citing violations of the exclusive contract, among other reasons. The initial claim amount of approximately 43.09 billion KRW was later adjusted to around 33.09 billion KRW following a change in legal representatives and a revision of the claim value.

During the third pleading session, both sides engaged in sharp exchanges over allegations of exclusive contract violations and dual-contract suspicions involving Danielle. ADOR argued that Danielle was the only NewJeans member to pursue independent music and commercial activities while excluding the agency. In particular, regarding an exclusive agreement signed with an overseas entity (AAO) following a performance at ComplexCon in Hong Kong last year, ADOR emphasized that while other members cooperated in resolving the contract issue once they became aware of it, Danielle and her family neither disclosed the fact of signing nor made any effort to correct the situation.

Danielle's side countered, stating, 'This is not a matter of Danielle acting unilaterally — it is an issue that applies equally to all members.' In a separate statement, they added, 'NewJeans members, including Danielle, have never entered into or attempted to enter into a dual exclusive entertainment contract with any specific company,' directly refuting ADOR's claims.

NewJeans had been in a contract dispute with ADOR since November 2024, but concluded their legal battle after losing the first-instance ruling on the validity of their exclusive contracts in October of last year. Following that, Haerin, Hyein, and Hanni returned to ADOR, while Danielle received a notice of contract termination from the agency citing responsibility for leaving the team, and was removed from the group.

Meanwhile, on July 22 — NewJeans' 4th debut anniversary — ADOR surprised fans by releasing a group commemorative content featuring four members: Hanni, Haerin, Hyein, and Minji, who is currently in discussions about her return.
