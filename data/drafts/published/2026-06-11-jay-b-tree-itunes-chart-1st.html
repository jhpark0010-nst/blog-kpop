GOT7's JAY B has made a strong global chart debut with his first new release in approximately 3 years and 8 months. His third mini album 'TR.EE' entered the Worldwide iTunes Album Chart at No. 9 immediately after its release at 6 p.m. on June 10.

The album also claimed the No. 1 spot on the iTunes Top Albums Chart in 10 regions around the world: Brazil, Chile, Costa Rica, Malaysia, New Zealand, the Philippines, Saudi Arabia, Taiwan, Thailand, and Vietnam. In addition, it landed within the top 10 in six more regions, including Hong Kong, Argentina, Ecuador, Indonesia, Mexico, and Austria.

JAY B also made his mark on the song charts. The title track 'Layback,' a well-crafted R&B number, shot straight to No. 1 on the iTunes Top Songs Chart in four countries: Argentina, Brazil, Colombia, and Thailand. With chart dominance spanning Asia, Oceania, and South America simultaneously, JAY B firmly proved his standing as an irreplaceable global top-tier solo artist.

Having steadily built a presence in the global market, JAY B continues to draw enthusiastic responses from music fans overseas with 'TR.EE.' Entering the Worldwide iTunes Chart top 10 at the moment of release and topping charts in major countries, JAY B has once again solidified his unique global brand value as a solo artist.

At his first-ever solo media listening event, JAY B opened up about his long-awaited return, saying, 'It felt like starting over, and I was scared and worried about whether as many people as before would come looking for me.' He also shared his outlook on chart performance, stating, 'Rather than shooting up right after release and then dropping sharply, I want a long run — being loved steadily without a big fall, even if the ranking is a little lower.'
