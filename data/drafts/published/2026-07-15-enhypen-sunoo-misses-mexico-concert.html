ENHYPEN member Sunoo will not be participating in the group's Mexico City concert on July 15, 2026. The announcement was made on the day of the show, confirming his absence from the ongoing BLOOD SAGA world tour stop in Mexico.

ENHYPEN's agency Belift Lab announced via official channels: 'We would like to inform fans that Sunoo will unfortunately be unable to attend today's ENHYPEN World Tour BLOOD SAGA Mexico City concert due to a sudden deterioration in his condition.'

The agency added: 'We ask for your generous understanding. We will do our best to help Sunoo recover so that he can meet fans again in good health.'

ENHYPEN is currently in the middle of their world tour ahead of the release of their eighth mini album, scheduled for next month on the 21st. The tour runs from July 4 to August 1, covering 8 cities across South America and North America, before continuing to Busan on August 8 and 9.
