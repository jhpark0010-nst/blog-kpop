ZEROBASEONE (ZB1) is making a fresh start as a five-member group, with Sung Hanbin, Kim Jiwoong, Seok Matthew, Kim Taerae, and Park Gunwook. This marks their comeback approximately three months after the special album 'RE-FLOW,' which was released in February and contained the final story of the nine-member lineup.

ZEROBASEONE recently sat down with MyDaily ahead of their five-member debut. This activity is the first step they are taking after facing the major change of the group's reorganization. ZEROBASEONE, formed in 2023 through CJ ENM's audition program 'Boys Planet,' restructured from a nine-member lineup to five. This is a different choice from the usual pattern in which project groups end their activities after failing to renew contracts.

The members all agreed that now is the time to prove the team again. Park Gunwook said, 'I think being five members actually puts us in a position where we have to prove ourselves even more,' adding, 'I'm taking it as motivation rather than pressure.' He continued, 'I think this moment can be a turning point in our careers. The goal is to create great stages with the members rather than focusing on results.'

Leader Sung Hanbin also shared, 'Since we personally chose this team, the sense of responsibility has grown.' He added, 'I think a lot about how we can move in a better direction given how the public and fans are watching us.' He further noted, 'Even when recording or practicing choreography, if we see areas that are lacking, the members try to practice more on their own. We are also sharing opinions much more than before.'

The biggest change that came with the shift to a five-member lineup was the team's direction. Kim Taerae explained, 'We had a lot of conversations about how to bring out the strengths of the five of us,' and 'We focused on finding our strengths and areas to improve, as five members can stand out more in terms of performance.' He added, 'We also talked a lot about vocal performance. We want to naturally introduce music and genres we haven't been able to show until now.' Park Gunwook said, 'If we used the word closure before, now I want to break away from that image and show a more mature side.'

Zhang Hao, Ricky, Kim Gyuvin, and Han Yujin, who chose not to renew their contracts, are preparing to debut in a new group called ANDBLE along with Yoo Seung-eon. Even so, the reason the remaining members gathered again under the name ZEROBASEONE was clear. Seok Matthew said, 'As long as I could keep doing ZEROBASEONE, I wanted to stay with the members for as long as possible.' Sung Hanbin added, 'It felt like fate that like-minded members came together. The process wasn't easy, but ultimately the biggest reason was the ZEROs (fandom name). I thought that if we could keep meeting our fans, that alone was meaningful.' Park Gunwook said, 'My desire to keep going, both as a person and as a team, is firm. I've become more confident in the choice I made.' Now reduced from nine to five, the members say this is not an end but a new beginning, and they are ready to take the stage again with a stronger resolve than ever.
