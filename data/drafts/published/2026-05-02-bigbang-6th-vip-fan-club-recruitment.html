BIGBANG is opening recruitment for its 6th official fan club, V.I.P, for the first time in 11 years. On May 1 at 8:19 PM KST, the group posted a 'BIGBANG OFFICIAL 6th V.I.P COMING SOON' poster across their official social media channels, marking the first fan club recruitment since the 5th generation in 2015.

The revealed poster features the 'Bang Bong' (BIGBANG's official light stick), a symbol of the bond between BIGBANG and V.I.P (the fandom name). Countless Bang Bongs shining brilliantly in the darkness caught fans' attention, hinting at a returning wave of yellow light and raising anticipation for the group's 20th anniversary project.

The 6th generation recruitment will be conducted through BIGBANG's official community on the global fan platform Weverse. Having already established a community and social media channels to build a new foundation for communication, BIGBANG is expected to foster an even closer connection with V.I.Ps around the world through this fan club recruitment.

YG Entertainment stated, 'We prepared the official fan club recruitment in order to connect more closely with the fans who have stood by BIGBANG's name for so long.' They added, 'Please look forward to the new 20th anniversary journey that the artists and V.I.P will write together.'

Meanwhile, BIGBANG recently signaled the beginning of their 20th anniversary project with their performance at the 2026 Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival in the United States. Having proven their enduring presence to an enthusiastically responding local audience, the group will embark on a world tour in August to meet fans across the globe.
