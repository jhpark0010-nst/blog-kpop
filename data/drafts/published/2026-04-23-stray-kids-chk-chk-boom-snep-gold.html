Stray Kids' song 'Chk Chk Boom' has received a Single Gold certification from the French National Syndicate of Phonographic Publishing (SNEP), JYP Entertainment announced on April 17. The track achieved 15 million streams in France, earning the group their first-ever Single Gold certification in the country.

'Chk Chk Boom' is the title track of Stray Kids' mini album 'ATE,' released in July 2024. Prior to this, four of Stray Kids' albums — including the full-length album 'FIVE-STAR,' mini album 'ROCK-STAR,' and 'ATE' — had already received Album Gold certifications from SNEP.

Group EVEN will make their first comeback as a five-member unit on April 20 with their single 'Backtalk,' Jellyfish Entertainment announced. The title track carries a message about forging one's own path without yielding to others' judgment or criticism, and all members participated in writing the lyrics together.

EVEN was formed by contestants from Mnet's 'Boys Planet' and had been active as a seven-member group from September 2023 through December of last year. The group was reorganized into a five-member lineup after Keita, Park Hanbin, Lee Junghyun, Moon Junghyun, and Park Jihu signed exclusive contracts with Jellyfish Entertainment, while Yoo Seungeon and Ji Yunseo returned to their original agency, YH Entertainment.

Singer Kim Gitae will release a new song titled 'Vow' on April 29 at noon, according to JMG (Double X Entertainment). 'Vow' is the first track in Kim Gitae's newly launched 'Oriental Series,' and is described as a song that highlights his husky vocal tone.

Kim Gitae plans to sequentially release additional tracks in the series following 'Vow,' including the second installment titled 'Fate.'
