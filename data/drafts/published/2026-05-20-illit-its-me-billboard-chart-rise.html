ILLIT's new single 'It's Me' is on a steep upward climb on the charts. According to the latest Billboard charts (dated May 23) released on the 19th, 'It's Me' — the title track of ILLIT's (Yunah, Minju, Moka, Wonhee, Iroha) fourth mini album MAMIHLAPINATAPAI — entered at No. 32 on the Billboard Global (Excluding U.S.) chart and No. 67 on the Billboard Global 200. These represent jumps of 23 and 37 spots respectively compared to the previous week.

ILLIT's strength is even more pronounced across the sub-charts. 'It's Me' landed at No. 8 on Billboard's World Digital Song Sales chart and No. 15 on Hot Dance/Electronic Songs. The album MAMIHLAPINATAPAI placed at No. 2 on World Albums, No. 10 on Top Dance/Electronic Albums, and No. 14 on Top Album Sales. The fact that both the single and the album have posted consistent results for two consecutive weeks is an encouraging sign.

'It's Me' continues its chart run across both domestic and international platforms. The song swept the upper ranks of major Korean streaming platforms on the latest weekly chart (tallied May 11–17), reaching No. 2 on Bugs, No. 4 on Melon, and No. 8 on Genie. It also proved its popularity in Japan, charting at No. 10 on Spotify Japan's Weekly Top Songs (May 8–14) and No. 16 on Line Music's weekly chart (May 6–12).

Additionally, 'It's Me' ranked No. 13 on the global YouTube-based Weekly Top Songs chart (May 8–14), demonstrating strong streaming power. The track has also been consistently charting on Apple Music's global Top 100 Today chart.

ILLIT's popularity was also on full display at a university festival. ILLIT took the stage at Korea University's festival the previous day, captivating the audience with the addictive melody and powerful performance of 'It's Me.' In particular, they surprised the crowd with a special joint stage alongside LE SSERAFIM members, performing a mashup of 'It's Me' and 'CELEBRATION' that brought the atmosphere to its peak.
