TVXQ's U-Know Yunho is releasing a new solo single, 'Time's Tickin,' at 6 p.m. on July 20, according to his agency SM Entertainment. The single marks his latest solo release and was first performed live just days before its official drop.

The new release includes two tracks: the title song 'Time's Tickin' and an additional track titled 'An Ordinary Story.' U-Know Yunho gave fans their first look at both new songs during his solo concert held at Seoul's Jamsil Indoor Stadium from July 17 to 19.

'Time's Tickin' is a pop dance track that U-Know Yunho co-wrote the lyrics for. The song is characterized by the sound of a ticking clock woven into the music and a signature point choreography that expresses the passage of time.

At the concert, U-Know Yunho said, 'This new album feels like a new game and a new stage for me. I hope everyone enjoys it together with me.'

Following the Seoul concerts, U-Know Yunho will continue his tour across Asia in August and September, with stops in Macau, Singapore, Bangkok (Thailand), Taipei (Taiwan), Jakarta (Indonesia), Ho Chi Minh City (Vietnam), and Hong Kong. He will then perform in Kanagawa, Osaka, and Aichi in Japan from October through November.
