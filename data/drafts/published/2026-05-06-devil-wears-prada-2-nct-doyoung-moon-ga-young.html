The Devil Wears Prada 2 has become a hot topic thanks to the appearance of K-stars in the film. The movie opened on April 29 and topped the North American opening box office, earning nearly three times the opening revenue of the original film.

Actress Moon Ga-young and NCT's Doyoung were revealed to have made surprise cameo appearances in the film. The two briefly appear in a Milan fashion show scene, drawing the attention of audiences.

The scene was reportedly filmed at an actual Dolce & Gabbana S/S season fashion show held during Milan Fashion Week last year. NCT's Doyoung and Moon Ga-young attended the event as global ambassadors for the brand.

Doyoung and Moon Ga-young were seated in the front row of the fashion show alongside legendary actors Meryl Streep and Stanley Tucci, the lead stars of The Devil Wears Prada 2, demonstrating their global influence.

In addition, world-renowned top model Choi So-ra also appeared in the film. Her powerful aura on the actual runway was captured on screen even in a brief moment, offering audiences a special treat.

As K-stars active on the global fashion stage make their way into The Devil Wears Prada 2, domestic audience interest in the film continues to grow. The film tells the story of Miranda (Meryl Streep), editor-in-chief of the legendary fashion magazine Runway, and Andy (Anne Hathaway), who returns as a planning editor after 20 years, as they reunite with Emily (Emily Blunt), now a luxury brand executive, and stake their entire careers on seizing control of the fashion world once again in a completely changed media landscape.
