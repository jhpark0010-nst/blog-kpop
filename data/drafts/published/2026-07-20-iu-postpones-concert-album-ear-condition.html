IU is postponing her September Goyang Stadium concert and the release of her 6th studio album after a worsening of her chronic ear condition, patulous Eustachian tube (이관개방증). Her agency EDAM Entertainment made the official announcement on July 20.

EDAM Entertainment stated, 'We have determined that her current ear condition makes it difficult to proceed with performances,' and added, 'Prioritizing her health above all else, we have decided to adjust the schedule for the Goyang Stadium concert planned for September of this year, along with subsequent concert dates and the 6th studio album release.' The agency further explained, 'Her current symptoms are not significantly interfering with daily life,' and that the decision was made 'in order to present the best possible performance to fans.'

On the same day, IU also shared her feelings directly through the fan communication platform Weverse. She wrote, 'My chronic patulous Eustachian tube symptoms have recently worsened,' and revealed, 'There were repeated instances where I had difficulty finding my condition while singing, and I received opinions from specialists that performing concerts for the time being would be too much.'

She continued, 'The album was completed without major difficulty, but since the preparations were centered around the concert and tour, I have decided to delay the release of the 6th studio album as well.' She also apologized, saying, 'My heart is so heavy and I am so sorry to the Uaenas who have been waiting.'

IU added, 'It is not an illness that makes daily life difficult, so I hope you will not worry too much,' and promised, 'I will focus on treatment and recovery and return with a new album and concerts as soon as possible. I will do my best to make sure we can start 2027 together.'

IU had originally planned to kick off a nationwide tour beginning with the Goyang Stadium concert in September, but has now decided to fully adjust her schedule in order to recover her health. Updated concert and album schedules will be announced officially at a later date.
