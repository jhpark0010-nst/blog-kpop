The legendary Korean variety format 'Parenting Diary' (육아일기) has made a spectacular comeback. Wavve's original variety show 'TXT's Parenting Diary,' starring TOMORROW X TOGETHER, generated overwhelming buzz even before its premiere and has since confirmed a 2-episode extension, riding a wave of intense viewer enthusiasm.

Originally planned as a 10-episode series, the show announced via its official account on the 3rd that it has been restructured into a 12-episode run, following an explosive outpouring of support and requests from viewers. The return of the original K-pop parenting variety format after a gap of 26 years has drawn excitement from both fans who remember the original and the global fandom alike.

When Wavve announced back in April that TOMORROW X TOGETHER would headline the revival of 'Parenting Diary,' public interest immediately peaked. The concept — top-tier idols stepping away from the global spotlight to take on the unfamiliar, demanding realities of hands-on childcare, including diaper changes and feeding baby food — generated huge anticipation before the show even aired. Posters and teaser videos that perfectly recreated the retro aesthetic of the early 2000s were more than enough to stir the emotions of fans of the original.

The result was a major success. 'TXT's Parenting Diary' debuted on the 1st of last month and immediately ranked No. 1 in content on the Wavve platform, becoming the top driver of new paid subscriptions. Remarkably, the new show's popularity also sparked renewed interest in the original program — MBC's 'god's Parenting Diary,' which drew ratings above 30% during its original broadcast run, saw its own viewing hours rise in tandem, once again proving the timeless power of the format.

As the show heads into its final stretch, episode 10 — set to drop on the 26th at 11 a.m. — features a landmark reunion. To mark Parents' Day, the TXT members surprised god members Son Ho-young and Kim Tae-woo with a special visit. The two guests visited baby Yujun's home and reflected on memories from 25 years ago, when they cared for 11-month-old Han Jaemin as first-time 'dads' on the original show. Son Ho-young in particular moved viewers when he remarked, 'It's amazing that Yeonjun is the same age as Jaemin.'

During the visit, Son Ho-young demonstrated the full authority of his legendary 'Super Mom' persona from the original show, instantly reading baby Yujun's temperament and delivering tailored care — leaving TXT members in awe, saying, 'How did you get that smile out of him?' Meanwhile, baby Yujun's frightened reaction upon first seeing Kim Tae-woo's large frame and beard sent the set into fits of laughter. Member Soobin also added to the fun with an adorable moment of tucking his head into a 'leg tunnel.' Beyond the guest appearance, god also connected deeply with the TXT members as true senior figures in both parenting and the entertainment industry, generously taking over childcare duties during the baby's nap time to give their hard-working juniors a well-earned rest. 'TXT's Parenting Diary,' featuring the five members Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun, and Huening Kai, airs exclusively on Wavve every Friday at 11 a.m.
