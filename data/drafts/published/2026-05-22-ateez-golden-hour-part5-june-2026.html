ATEEZ will release their 14th mini album, 'GOLDEN HOUR : Part.5', on June 26 at 1:00 PM KST, their agency KQ Entertainment announced on May 22. The group revealed the new album release plan through a comeback teaser video published the same day.

From July last year through April this year, ATEEZ carried out their world tour 'IN YOUR FANTASY', traveling from Incheon to 12 cities in North America, 3 cities in Japan, and 9 cities across Asia and Australia.

Their 13th mini album 'GOLDEN HOUR : Part.4', released in February, reached No. 3 on the Billboard 200, the main album chart in the United States, and set a new personal record for first-week sales in the US.

ATEEZ is also set to perform as a headliner at British Summer Time Hyde Park, a major music festival held in London, UK, on June 28 (local time).
