LE SSERAFIM has produced the group's first-ever song to surpass 700 million streams. According to global audio and music streaming platform Spotify, 'ANTIFRAGILE' — the title track of LE SSERAFIM's second mini album, performed by members Kim Chaewon, Sakura, Huh Yunjin, Kazuha, and Hong Eunchae — recorded a total of 701,018,94 plays as of the April 25 chart date. It is the group's first song ever to reach 700 million streams.

The track had previously surpassed 600 million cumulative streams in August of last year, and added another 100 million plays in approximately eight months, proving it to be the group's signature song. 'ANTIFRAGILE' carries the message of accepting difficult times as a stimulus for growth and becoming stronger through them. The five members sing lyrics such as 'I'll go higher, to the top of the world I dreamed of / It's okay to fall, I'm antifragile antifragile,' expressing that even if they fall momentarily, they will soon soar again and continue their own journey. The choreography, which makes prominent use of arm muscles, intuitively conveys their determination not to be easily broken.

LE SSERAFIM now has a total of 16 songs on Spotify that have surpassed 100 million streams. 'ANTIFRAGILE' leads at 700 million, followed by 'Perfect Night' at 500 million, 'CRAZY' and 'Smart' at 400 million each, 'EASY', 'UNFORGIVEN (feat. Nile Rodgers)', and 'FEARLESS' at 300 million each, 'Eve, Psyche & the Bluebeard's Wife', 'Sour Grapes', and 'SPAGHETTI (feat. j-hope of BTS)' at 200 million each, and 'HOT', 'Impurities', 'FEARLESS (2023 Ver.)', 'Blue Flame', 'Good Parts (when the quality is bad but I am)', and '1-800-hot-n-fun' each surpassing 100 million streams.

Meanwhile, LE SSERAFIM released their lead single 'CELEBRATION' on April 24. Kim Chaewon and Huh Yunjin are credited on the track, which conveys a message celebrating the acknowledgment of fear and the cultivation of inner strength. On April 25, the group additionally released 'CELEBRATION (Remixes ver)', which includes a Sped Up version, a Slowed + Reverb version, an Instrumental version, and a Karaoke version.

On April 27 at 1 PM KST, LE SSERAFIM released a karaoke-style visualizer on their official YouTube channel and social media, continuing their lively promotional activities.
