BTS is holding BTS FESTA to celebrate the group's 13th debut anniversary. According to their label Big Hit Music, the festival commemorates BTS's debut date of June 13 and will run for approximately two weeks around that date.

This year's BTS FESTA will be held alongside the ARIRANG world tour's Busan concerts, scheduled for June 12 and 13 at Busan Asiad Main Stadium, with a variety of content to be unveiled throughout the event.

The title of this year's BTS FESTA is '13(B)TS.' Big Hit Music explained that the title expresses the meaning of a starting point — adding a new '1' unique to BTS and ARMY (the fandom name) to the 12 years of their journey so far, and moving forward together into the next chapter.

On June 4, a group photo of BTS shot with a family concept will be released. On June 5, a performance video for 'Hooligan,' a track from their fifth studio album ARIRANG, will be unveiled.

Their original content series 'Run BTS 2.0' will drop on June 10 and 11. On June 12, 'Come Over' — a new song previously available only on the deluxe vinyl (LP) edition of ARIRANG — will be released as a digital single.

Additional varied content is also scheduled to be released sequentially throughout the festival period.
