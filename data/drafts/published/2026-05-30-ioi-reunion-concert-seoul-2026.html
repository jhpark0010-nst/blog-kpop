I.O.I kicked off their '2026 I.O.I Concert Tour: Loop in Seoul' on May 29 at Jamsil Indoor Stadium in Songpa-gu, Seoul, taking the stage together for the first time in 9 years. The members could not hide their emotion as they greeted the crowd with 'Yes, I love it! Hello, we are I.O.I!'

Kim Do-yeon said, 'We were able to reunite after 9 years thanks to all of you, the fans,' adding, 'I was so moved by the cheers that broke through my in-ear monitors.' Kim Se-jeong shared, 'I am so happy every single day that I cry,' explaining, 'It is because I keep wondering when I will ever get to enjoy such a beautiful time again.'

The concert runs for three days through May 31. This comeback was made possible to mark the group's 10th debut anniversary. Nine members reunited, with Kang Mina and Joo Kyulkyung not participating. I.O.I was originally formed through Mnet's 'Produce 101' in 2016 and disbanded after just eight months of activity, with members going on to pursue solo careers as singers and actors. After their long-awaited reunion, the group topped major music charts including Melon with their new single 'Gapjagi,' released on May 19.

The reunion concert opened with 'Pick Me,' the theme song of 'Produce 101.' The moment the intro played, the audience erupted in cheers that filled the venue. During the following song 'Dream Girls,' fans chanted along energetically in time with the beat. Jeong Chaeyeon said, 'I thought our roots were in Pick Me, so we chose it as the first song. I was genuinely surprised when everyone cheered the moment it came on.' Im Nayoung added, 'I missed these cheers so much,' and noted, 'It is meaningful that we hit number one on the music charts on the first day of the concert.'

Jeon Somi said, 'I missed everyone so much,' and reflected, 'The last I.O.I concert 9 years ago is still my last concert ever. Even though so much time has passed, performing with my unnies like this feels like it was just 2 years ago.' The group performed a wide setlist including representative tracks 'Very Very Very,' 'Sonagi,' and 'When the Cherry Blossoms Fade,' as well as new songs 'Gapjagi' and 'SPF 100+' (Summer Pop Fantasy). Yoo Yeon-jung said, 'I am reminded of our debut showcase and also our last concert. I incorporated a lot of the same choreography formations we used back then. I think fans will be able to feel that warm, fuzzy feeling.'

Although the members had said 'let's not cry today,' they became visibly emotional as the concert moved into its latter half. Kim Do-yeon's eyes grew red while singing the ballad 'When the Cherry Blossoms Fade,' and a warm round of applause filled the venue after the ballad 'Sonagi.' Im Nayoung told fans, 'I missed the stage so much. I wanted to dance so badly. I am so happy to have this dream come true. It was hard, but once I stood on stage, it did not feel hard anymore.' Yoo Yeon-jung said, 'This 10th anniversary project was not easy. I was worried, but we are receiving more love than we ever imagined. Topping the music charts and performing in front of a full house at a large venue — it still does not feel real.' Jeon Somi said, 'We gathered after 9 years and practiced so, so much. We ran toward one single goal. I was so happy to be able to show everything we prepared through today's concert. Thank you for putting us at number one on the charts. We cried a lot together.' Kim So-hye became emotional, saying, 'After 9 years I got to see the fans again, and my beloved family came here too — I felt so much energy.' Kim Do-yeon promised, 'Just like the concert name Loop, let's be sure to meet again.' A fan named Sunny (19) who traveled from Taiwan said, 'I came to Korea this morning just to see the I.O.I concert,' adding, 'It is not the full 11-member lineup, but just being able to see them perform together after so long is enough to make me so happy.' Following the Seoul concerts, I.O.I will continue their tour in Bangkok, Thailand on June 6–7, and Hong Kong on June 20–21.
