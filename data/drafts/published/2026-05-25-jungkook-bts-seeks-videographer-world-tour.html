BTS member Jungkook is drawing attention after publicly searching for a videographer and editor to accompany him on his world tour. On May 24, he posted a vlog on his personal SNS captioned 'in Las Vegas 23. 5. 2026,' filmed in Las Vegas, USA. The video captured him speeding along a desert road on a motorcycle, immediately catching fans' eyes.

Notably, he also posted on his feed and stories actively calling for a video creator. Jungkook wrote, 'Looking for someone to film. If you can genuinely edit well too? Let's travel the tour together,' and 'Looking for someone who can shoot video and photos amazingly. I'll figure out the equipment somehow! Reach me however you can!'

Fans reacted explosively to the unexpected open call. Playful comments poured in, including 'Where do I send my resume,' 'Editors around the world are having heart attacks right now,' and 'The moment skill matters more than being a fan.' The post, which also drew a comment from BTS leader RM, accumulated over 3 million likes, once again proving Jungkook's global popularity.

The motorcycle featured in Jungkook's video also became a major topic. The bike is reportedly the Ducati Panigale V4 S, a premium Italian motorcycle brand, with a price tag of approximately 60 million Korean won. Jungkook had previously revealed photos of his new bike, showcasing his well-known love of motorcycles.

Meanwhile, BTS — the group Jungkook belongs to — is currently conducting 'BTS WORLD TOUR ARIRANG,' a total of 85 shows across 34 cities worldwide including North America, Europe, South America, and Asia, having kicked off in Goyang in March. This is the largest-ever single tour by a domestic Korean artist.
