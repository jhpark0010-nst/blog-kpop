BABYMONSTER has confirmed the opening of additional seats for their second world tour Seoul concerts. All three dates sold out in an instant, prompting the group and their label to release extra tickets.

On May 8, agency YG Entertainment announced the additional seat release for BABYMONSTER's Seoul concerts, expressing gratitude and saying it was 'thanks to the explosive support of music fans for these shows.'

Following the pre-sale, all three days' worth of seats also sold out immediately during the general ticketing period. After requests for extra seats flooded in, the members' own desire to share the experience with more fans led to the decision to open additional tickets.

In addition to regular seats and restricted-view seats, a limited number of sound check seats will also be available, meaning the additional ticket release is expected to spark another fierce ticketing battle.

BABYMONSTER will hold the '2026-27 BABYMONSTER WORLD TOUR [CHOOM] IN SEOUL' at Jamsil Indoor Stadium in Seoul from June 26 to 28. As the concerts follow the release of their well-received mini album 3 [CHOOM], an even richer setlist is anticipated.

Meanwhile, BABYMONSTER made their comeback on May 4 with mini album 3 [CHOOM]. The release includes a total of four new tracks: the addictive hip-hop-based title track 'CHOOM', the dreamy and distinctive 'MOON', the refreshing dance track 'I LIKE IT', and the R&B pop number 'LOCKED IN'.
