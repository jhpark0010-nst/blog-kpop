Hollywood actor Chase Infinity showed off her genuine ATEEZ fandom on the YouTube channel MMTG (Civilization Express) on July 2. The video, titled 'The Oscar 6-Win Actor Who Stans ATEEZ's San and Wooyoung,' features Infinity meeting ATEEZ members San and Wooyoung in person.

Infinity first gained attention through her film debut in Paul Thomas Anderson's 'One Battle After Another,' in which she played the daughter of the protagonist portrayed by Leonardo DiCaprio. The film received 13 Academy Award nominations at the U.S. ceremony last March and took home 6 awards.

Infinity recalled the audition process, saying, 'I had to do a test where I shaved DiCaprio's face.' She added, 'I had never shaved anyone before, so I was scared I might hurt him.'

In the video, Infinity recited the names of all eight ATEEZ members and had even memorized their zodiac signs. She said, 'I've filled an entire shelf of my bookcase with ATEEZ albums,' and added, 'I carry a photocard and a plushie of San, my favorite member, with me.'

Infinity shared that she first got into K-pop through NCT's 'Boss' in 2018. She said, 'The first K-pop song I ever heard was Girls' Generation's 'I Got A Boy.'' She also revealed, 'During college, I formed a K-pop cover dance crew with my roommates, and a cover video from that time led to my acting casting.'

Meanwhile, Infinity also appeared in the music video for ATEEZ's new song 'BAD,' released on June 26. It was reported that ATEEZ's agency offered her the appearance after learning she was a fan. San said, 'I had no idea she knew us this deeply,' and added, 'From now on, I will watch every project Chase appears in with great affection.'
