RIIZE will release their second mini album 'II' on June 15, SM Entertainment announced on May 22. This marks the group's first comeback in seven months, following the single 'Fame' released last November.

Described as the album that most intuitively captures RIIZE as they are now, 'II' will include six tracks, with the title track 'Do Your Dance' leading the release.

Ahead of the comeback, RIIZE is scheduled to attend a Taiwan professional baseball game by the Chinatrust Brothers at Taipei Dome on May 24, where they will perform a ceremonial first pitch and a special celebratory performance.

In other news, Melon, one of South Korea's leading music platforms, announced a new offline event called 'THE SOUND.' Centered on conversations and listening sessions, the event aims to deliver an in-depth look at artists' music and stories. The first edition will be held on May 31 at Hyundai Card Understage in Yongsan-gu, Seoul, with the featured guest being Wakai Hiroto, guitarist of popular Japanese band Mrs. GREEN APPLE.

Singer-songwriter Lim Jiwoo will release the digital single 'Mianhae (Forgive Me)' on May 23 at noon KST through music streaming platforms, according to MPMG Music. The R&B track depicts the process of gradually breaking someone down by repeatedly saying 'sorry' without emotion, and includes both Korean and English versions. Lim Jiwoo debuted in 2022 and has been praised for her honest, heartfelt music about love.
