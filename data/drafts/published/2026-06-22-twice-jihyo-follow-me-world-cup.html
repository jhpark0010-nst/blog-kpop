TWICE member Jihyo participated as a vocalist on 'Follow Me,' a global collaboration song created for the 2026 North America World Cup, JYP Entertainment announced on June 22.

'Follow Me' was made to connect football fans and music fans around the world ahead of the 2026 North America World Cup, and was officially released on June 12.

The project was led by RedOne, a Grammy Award-winning producer and songwriter.

Participating artists include Jihyo alongside international acts French Montana, Ludmilla, and Adriana C.

The music video released that day features Jihyo together with global football stars including Brazil's Ronaldo, Morocco's Brahim Diaz, and Uruguay's Federico Valverde.
