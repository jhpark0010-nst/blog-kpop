BOYNEXTDOOR will kick off their first world tour, 'KNOCK ON Vol.2,' from July 17 to 19 at the KSPO Dome in Olympic Park, Songpa-gu, Seoul. Their agency KOZ Entertainment announced the news on July 16. The full tour spans 24 cities and 35 shows in total.

The group recently wrapped up promotions for their first full-length album 'HOME' and will continue their second-half activities with the world tour.

The members shared, 'We are curious and excited to see how our fandom WENDOOR will receive this.' They added, 'They say the first time always matters most — we will color this first start intensely, with no regrets.'

They also said, 'We learned a lot from our previous Asia tour, so we are looking forward to seeing how much we can grow this time around.' The members continued, 'We have prepared extensively, so we will give the audience deeply memorable moments. Please look forward to a performance with a clear beginning, build-up, climax, and conclusion.'

The world tour begins with the Seoul shows, then moves to Busan, before heading to six cities in Japan including Kanagawa, Saga, Osaka, Miyagi, Nagano, and Chiba.

Following Japan, the tour will visit 10 locations in North America including Dallas, Pompano Beach, Chicago, New York, Toronto, and Vancouver. It will then travel to six cities in Asia, including Jakarta (Indonesia), Kuala Lumpur (Malaysia), Taipei (Taiwan), and Hong Kong.
