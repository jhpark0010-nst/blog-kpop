TEN, a member of NCT and WayV, has announced a fresh start. On July 6, the creative company ILLIMNT, which TEN co-founded, officially launched. ILLIMNT is described as a creative hub encompassing music, culture, visual art, fashion, original content, and brand partnerships.

With this official launch, ILLIMNT will begin wide-ranging global activities with TEN as its central figure.

TEN, who has been active as a member of NCT and WayV, is set to present a variety of projects this year spanning music, performances, content, and brand collaborations across major global markets including South Korea, Thailand, China, and the United States.

TEN shared his thoughts on the founding, saying, 'ILLIMNT believes in turning the impossible into the possible through creativity.' He added, 'I want to help artists freely explore new possibilities while staying true to their own authenticity, connect people, and create works that deliver emotion and empathy across borders.'

Meanwhile, TEN has been building his profile as a solo artist since releasing his first solo mini-album 'TEN' in 2024, followed by 'STUNNER' and 'Humanity'.
