(G)I-DLE has returned with a new album set to color the height of summer with sensuality. The group — consisting of Miyeon, Minnie, Soyeon, Yuqi, and Shuhua — released their 9th mini album 'We made' on July 6 at 6 p.m. KST, kicking off their full-scale global promotions. The album arrives approximately six months after their digital single 'Mono,' released earlier this year, with Soyeon and Yuqi directly participating in the songwriting to further strengthen the album's pop sensibility.

The title track 'Gimme Dat Love' is a song that depicts the thirst-like feeling of love in the moment of being strongly drawn to each other. It honestly expresses the emotions of love spreading like a gaze, a touch, and warmth. Noted Latin pop producers participated in completing the group's own fiery summer song, including Daramola — who has collaborated with Anitta, Danny Ocean, Sean Paul, Becky G, and Jason Derulo — as well as Samantha Cámara, who has worked with global artists such as Kenia OS, Nicky Jam, and Danna.

The third music video teaser for 'Gimme Dat Love,' unveiled at midnight on the release date, showed people becoming increasingly instinctively drawn to each other after falling in love. In particular, a new lyric — 'quietly before the music stops' — was revealed through subtitles and sound in the teaser, raising anticipation further.

The album includes the title track alongside pre-released tracks 'Mono (Feat. skaiwater)' and 'Crow,' as well as 'Morning' and 'Love Is Pain.' A mono version of 'Mono (Feat. skaiwater)' is also included as a CD-only exclusive track. Among the album's tracks, 'Love Is Pain' — a self-composed song by Yuqi — is a mid-tempo R&B ballad reflecting the lingering feelings and the difficulty of letting go after a breakup, showcasing the members' powerful vocal performances. Another track, 'Morning,' is an upbeat, well-crafted daily life song about finding small moments of happiness within an everyday morning routine.

Meanwhile, (G)I-DLE is currently in the midst of their world tour '2026 i-dle WORLD TOUR [Syncopation].' On July 31, they will take the stage at Lollapalooza in Chicago, USA, meeting local audiences there.
