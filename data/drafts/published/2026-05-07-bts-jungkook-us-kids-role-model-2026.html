BTS member Jungkook has been named a 2026 featured subject in Brain Candy Books, an educational book series published by American educational publisher Capstone. The series spotlights influential figures who serve as role models for today's children, and Jungkook becomes the first Korean singer ever to be featured in it.

Past subjects in the series have included icons such as Taylor Swift and Lionel Messi. This year, Jungkook was selected alongside Dua Lipa, Selena Gomez, and Cristiano Ronaldo, further cementing his standing on the global stage.

The book describes Jungkook as 'a solo superstar beyond BTS,' emphasizing that the world agrees: 'Jungkook is extraordinary!' It portrays him as 'the face of BTS,' praising his natural talent and precise vocal control.

The book also details how US music publication Rolling Stone named him one of the greatest singers in history. His major career milestones are highlighted as well — including being the first Korean solo artist to land 7 songs on the Billboard Hot 100, and the first Korean singer to perform the official soundtrack for a FIFA World Cup opening ceremony.

The book further spotlights his dominance on social media platforms such as TikTok and Google, calling him a 'social media superstar.'

Beyond being a pop star, Jungkook is recognized as someone who shares his life philosophy and personal growth story with children around the world. He is currently focused on the North American leg of BTS's large-scale world tour, 'Arirang,' meeting fans across the globe.
