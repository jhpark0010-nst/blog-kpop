LE SSERAFIM released their lead single 'CELEBRATION' on April 24, 2026 at 1 PM KST, ahead of their second full-length album PUREFLOW pt.1. Their agency Source Music made the announcement.

'CELEBRATION' is a song that celebrates the moment of acknowledging fear and finding the inner strength to face it. The track blends melodic techno and hardstyle — a type of electronic dance genre that originated in the late 1990s — and is characterized by an upbeat, addictive beat and melody.

The five members, who delivered the message 'we are strong because we have no fear' in their debut track 'FEARLESS,' use their second full album to show an expanded perspective: 'we became even stronger because we came to know fear.'

Through their agency, LE SSERAFIM shared: 'From the very first time we heard this song, we pictured everyone enjoying it together at a party or a concert venue, and we couldn't wait to share it.' They added, 'We feel that the exciting energy carries our story, which makes it an even better fit for the theme of celebration.'

They also said, 'We put care into making sure the message of the song comes through just through our expressions and atmosphere, so we hope you pay attention to that when watching the stage.' They continued, 'Just like the title of the song, we hope everyone can come together and enjoy it.'

In the 'CELEBRATION' performance, LE SSERAFIM express the drive to move forward even in the face of fear. Contemporary dancers join them on stage during sections where emotional expression is especially important.
