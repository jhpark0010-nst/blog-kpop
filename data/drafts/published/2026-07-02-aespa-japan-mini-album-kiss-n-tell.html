aespa will release their first Japanese mini album, 'KISS N TELL,' on July 24, SM Entertainment announced on July 2. The album marks the group's debut Japanese mini album release.

The album includes the title track 'KISS N TELL' along with a total of 6 original Japanese songs.

Ahead of the new album's release, aespa will hold a fan meeting titled 'MY CLASSMaeTE' at Keio Arena Tokyo in Japan for three days, from July 18 to July 20.

aespa's second full-length album 'LEMONADE,' released on May 29, has surpassed cumulative sales of 1 million copies.
