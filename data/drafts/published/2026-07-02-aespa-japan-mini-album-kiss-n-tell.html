aespa is set to showcase a new side of themselves with their first Japanese mini album, following the sour-flavored concept of their first full-length album 'LEMONADE'. The group has confirmed they will release their first Japanese mini album 'KISS N TELL' on July 24. This marks their Japan comeback after approximately two years.

The new release consists of a total of six original Japanese tracks, including the title track 'KISS N TELL'. Having already received much love for their Japanese debut single 'Hot Mess', anticipation is building for the new look aespa will present with these new songs.

Teaser images released simultaneously with the release announcement via aespa's official social media channels captured a fantastical and mysterious world fitting for the title track 'KISS N TELL'. The images also drew attention for the members' iconic visuals, featuring retro-future sensibilities and lovely pink-toned styling.

Meanwhile, aespa will hold the fan meeting 'MY-J presents aespa JAPAN FANMEETING 2026 "MY CLASSMaeTE"' at Keio Arena Tokyo in Japan over three days from the 18th to the 20th.
