BTS has kicked off the North America leg of their 'Arirang' World Tour at Raymond James Stadium in Tampa, Florida on April 25–26 and 28 (local time), Big Hit Music announced on April 26. This marks the group's first U.S. concert since their Las Vegas shows in April 2022 — a gap of four years.

Following Tampa, BTS will perform across 12 cities in North America, including El Paso, Mexico City, and New York, for a total of 31 shows.

Local U.S. broadcasters have scheduled special programming to mark BTS's visit to the region. Tampa-area TV channel FOX 13 Tampa Bay is airing a special program titled 'K-Pop: The Seoul Reach' on both April 25 and 26.

Another broadcaster, 10 Tampa Bay News, projected that the concerts will generate an economic impact of approximately $800 million to $900 million (roughly 1.2 to 1.3 trillion Korean won) for the Tampa area, forecasting a significant ripple effect on the local economy.
