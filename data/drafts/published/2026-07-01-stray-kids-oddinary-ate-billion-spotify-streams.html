Stray Kids have achieved a major streaming milestone: their albums ODDINARY and ATE have each surpassed 1 billion streams on Spotify, the world's largest music streaming platform. JYP Entertainment announced the news on July 1, 2026.

ODDINARY was released in March 2022, while ATE was released in July 2024.

With this achievement, Stray Kids now hold a total of six albums with over 1 billion Spotify streams. The other four are IN生 (IN Life), NOEASY, GO生 (GO Life), and ★★★★★ (5-STAR).

Stray Kids are set to kick off their new world tour, 'Stray Kids World Tour RUN IT', on July 25 at KSPO Dome in Songpa-gu, Seoul. They will also release a new mini-album titled THIS & THAT on August 7.
