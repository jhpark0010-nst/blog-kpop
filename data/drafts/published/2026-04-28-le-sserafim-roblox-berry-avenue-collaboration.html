LE SSERAFIM has launched a collaboration with global game creation platform Roblox. On April 25, Roblox unveiled a variety of content created in partnership with LE SSERAFIM — Kim Chaewon, Sakura, Huh Yunjin, Kazuha, and Hong Eunchae — within the Seoul map of the game 'Berry Avenue.' 'Berry Avenue' is one of the most popular role-playing games on Roblox.

Users can experience elements related to LE SSERAFIM's second full-album lead single 'CELEBRATION' throughout spaces decorated with the team's logo. In the dance studio, players can practice the song's choreography. A vegetable truck that appeared in the music video has been recreated as an in-game vehicle. Most notably, avatars of the five members appear at a performance venue and perform to 'CELEBRATION,' and game users can also join them on stage. The event runs until May 9, with more content planned for a second phase of the collaboration.

LE SSERAFIM has been receiving partnership offers from various global companies for some time. In 2023, the group carried out their first collaboration with Blizzard Entertainment's team-based free-to-play action game Overwatch 2, co-producing the music video for their English digital single 'Perfect Night,' which generated significant buzz. Last year, a second collaboration with Overwatch 2 featured a promotion using 'So Cynical (Badum)' from their fifth mini-album. The music video for another track from that album, 'Come Over,' was produced together with Google's mobile operating system Android, with members using the AI assistant Gemini in the video — noted as the first music video Google Android had produced with a K-pop artist.

During their North America tour last year, LE SSERAFIM also partnered with Amazon Music to hold an offline pop-up event. They were the first K-pop girl group chosen by the platform — one of the three major music streaming services in the U.S. market — to host an offline pop-up. Through these collaborations spanning gaming, IT, and beyond, LE SSERAFIM continues to demonstrate their broad influence.

Meanwhile, LE SSERAFIM is set to officially release their second full album 'PUREFLOW' pt.1 on May 22. The album marks a new chapter for the group, depicting the changes and growth the five members have experienced as they came to understand fear after once shouting 'FEARLESS.' Their lead single 'CELEBRATION,' released on April 24, is a song celebrating the moment they gained the strength to acknowledge and face that fear. It charted on the Worldwide iTunes Song Chart for four consecutive days and appeared on the iTunes Top Songs chart in a total of 35 countries and regions as of 6 a.m. on April 28.
