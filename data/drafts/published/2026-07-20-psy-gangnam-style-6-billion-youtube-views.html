PSY's global hit music video 'Gangnam Style' has become the first K-pop video in history to surpass 6 billion views on YouTube, his agency P NATION announced on July 20. The milestone was reached on the afternoon of July 17, exactly 14 years after the video was first released on July 15, 2012.

The 'Gangnam Style' music video previously crossed the 5 billion view mark on December 30, 2023, meaning it added another 1 billion views in just 2 years and 7 months.

'Gangnam Style' took the world by storm with its comedic lyrics, memorable horse-dance moves, and humorous visuals. In December 2012, it became the first video in YouTube history to surpass 1 billion views, and it spent 7 consecutive weeks at number 2 on the US Billboard Hot 100, marking a turning point that sparked the K-pop wave in Western mainstream music markets.

In a past interview with Yonhap News, PSY reflected on the YouTube phenomenon surrounding 'Gangnam Style,' saying, 'YouTube changed the landscape of the Korean Wave.' He added, ''Gangnam Style' was the first case where YouTube was well utilized.'

P NATION stated, 'The 'Gangnam Style' music video surpassing 6 billion views is a symbolic record in K-pop history and a testament to the enduring power of content that has been consistently loved across borders and generations.'

PSY celebrated the 6 billion view milestone the day before the announcement by taking the stage at his 'PSY Soaking Show Summer Swag 2026' concert in Gwacheon, wearing the outfit from the 'Gangnam Style' music video. He told the crowd, 'I will upload today's stage footage to YouTube as a thank-you video for 6 billion views.'
