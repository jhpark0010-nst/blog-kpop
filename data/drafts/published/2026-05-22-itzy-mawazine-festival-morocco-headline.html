Girl group ITZY will perform as a headliner at Mawazine, a major music festival held in Rabat, Morocco, on June 23 (local time). Their agency JYP Entertainment announced the news on May 22.

Mawazine, which was first held in 2001, is a renowned music festival that draws more than 2.5 million people each year. Global stars such as Will Smith, 50 Cent, Becky G, and Lil Baby have previously taken the stage. Among K-pop acts, ATEEZ performed at the festival in 2024.

This year, ITZY will be joined by Tiësto, Nicky Jam, Rema, and others for performances at the festival.

JYP Entertainment stated, 'ITZY will connect with global audiences through their outstanding dance skills and unique stage energy.'

ITZY made their comeback on May 18 with their new mini-album 'Motto'. On the same day, they also released a remix version of the title track 'Motto'.
