BABYMONSTER captivated global fans with an overwhelming live performance right from their very first music show stage. The group appeared on MBC 'Show! Music Core' (aired May 9) to unveil the title track 'CHOOM' from their third mini album for the first time. With YG having already built comeback excitement through a self-produced performance video, anticipation for this music show stage was running high.

The moment BABYMONSTER stepped out, they commanded the stage with an intense presence. An elaborate set reminiscent of the music video, paired with hip styling, amplified the song's dynamic atmosphere and drew viewers deeper into the performance.

The members' accumulated stage experience from their world tour and their noticeably upgraded performance skills stood out throughout. Vocals and rap flowed without pause over a hard-hitting beat, while powerful and dynamic choreography instantly locked eyes on the group. Addictive point choreography making full use of both arms and legs added an extra layer of fun to the stage.

Above all, their steady hand-mic live singing through high-difficulty choreography drew genuine admiration. In the group formation dance with backup dancers in the latter half of the stage, the members unleashed free-spirited swagger and explosive energy, maximizing the lively charm that 'CHOOM' carries — a moment that once again proved their standing as performance powerhouses.

BABYMONSTER will continue their comeback activities by taking the stage on SBS 'Inkigayo' on May 10, following their 'Show! Music Core' appearance. They also plan to actively engage with global fans across various platforms including MCN content and radio.
