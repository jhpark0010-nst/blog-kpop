8TURN has confirmed their comeback date. According to a scheduler revealed through their official account on the 23rd, 8TURN will release their new single '8.X' on July 21. This marks their first new release in approximately six months, following their single album 'BRUISE' (불씨) released last January.

Following the comeback, 8TURN will kick off a solo tour across eight cities in Europe in September, continuing on to a North America tour in October.

Meanwhile, 8TURN is an eight-member boy group under MNH Entertainment. They debuted on January 30, 2023, marking their third debut anniversary this year.
