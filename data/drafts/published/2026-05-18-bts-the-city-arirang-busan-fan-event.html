BTS is set to hold a large-scale offline fan event called 'BTS The City Arirang - Busan' in Busan next month, their agency Big Hit Music announced on May 18. This marks the first time 'The City' event has been held in Busan since 2022, making it a four-year return to the city.

'The City' is an event that offers fans a variety of BTS-related experiences in the concert host city, held around the time of the performances. This edition will take place around BTS's world tour 'ARIRANG' Busan concerts, which run for two days on June 12 and 13. The event also carries special meaning as it coincides with BTS's debut anniversary on June 13.

In line with the two concert dates, a drone light show will be held at Gwangandaegyo Bridge, one of Busan's most iconic landmarks.

At Busan Eurasia Platform, fans can enjoy a media art wall from June 5 to 21. From June 10 to 14, an 'ARMY Madang' (fan gathering space) will be set up at The Bay 101 Gallery Hall.

In addition to these, various programs including a Busan city tour and yacht tour are also being prepared for attendees.

The agency noted that the event was planned with the active cooperation and support of Busan Metropolitan City.
