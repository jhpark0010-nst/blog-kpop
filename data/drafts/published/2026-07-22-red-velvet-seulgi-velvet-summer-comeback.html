Red Velvet's Seulgi showed off a stunning beach look in a series of photos shared on her SNS on July 19. The post was captioned: 'Big Wave #SEULGI, Red Velvet Summer Mini Album (Velvet Summer), 2026.08.03 6PM (KST),' signaling the group's upcoming summer comeback.

In the photos, Seulgi poses against a backdrop of blue ocean waters, exuding a captivating and mature charm. Her look was highlighted by a natural makeup style with freckles added, maximizing a fresh yet distinctive appeal.

Seulgi wore a pink vertical-striped monokini swimsuit, showing off a toned, well-defined figure. A heart tattoo on her shoulder added a touch of both sexiness and sweetness. She gazed into the camera with a deep, alluring expression and struck natural poses, embodying the image of a summer goddess.

Fans who saw the photos flooded the comments with reactions such as 'You're so beautiful,' 'It looks like you're in love with summer,' and 'This is why I love Seulgi,' praising her visuals and overall atmosphere.

Red Velvet, which also includes Wendy, will make their comeback on August 3 at 6PM KST across global music platforms with their new summer mini album 'Velvet Summer.' Known as the 'Summer Queens' behind hit songs like 'Red Flavor,' 'Power Up,' and 'Umpah Umpah,' the group is expected to make a big impact once again. Seulgi's healthy and charismatic image has heightened anticipation for the album's concept.

Ahead of the album release, Red Velvet will also hold the '2026 Red Velvet FAN-CON' (fan concert) at Korea University's Hwajung Gymnasium in Seongbuk-gu, Seoul, from July 31 to August 2, giving fans an early chance to meet the group.
