BTS member Jungkook has been selected as the sole subject of a US elementary school educational biography, marking a historic first for a Korean singer. According to entertainment industry sources on May 6, Jungkook has been included in the 2026 lineup of the American educational book series Brain Candy Books.

Brain Candy Books is a biography series published by US educational publisher Capstone, which selects influential and role-model figures for contemporary children and dedicates one volume to each subject. Previous cover subjects have included Taylor Swift, Billie Eilish, and Lionel Messi.

Joining Jungkook in this year's series are Dua Lipa, Selena Gomez, and Cristiano Ronaldo. This is reported to be the first time a Korean singer has been featured as a subject in the series.

The book introduces Jungkook's childhood, his path since debut, and his personal motto. It describes him as 'THE FACE OF BTS,' highlighting his innate musical sense and precise pitch control, and cites the fact that Rolling Stone magazine named him one of the 'greatest singers of all time.'

His cultural influence is also covered. The book describes Jungkook as a 'sold-out king,' noting that every item he wears sells out, and reportedly gives him the label of 'social media superstar' based on his 2024 Pinterest, Google, and Weverse search volumes as well as cumulative TikTok hashtag counts.

Meanwhile, BTS, the group Jungkook belongs to, is currently carrying out the North American leg of their world tour 'Arirang.'
