NCT WISH has fired the opening shot of a successful comeback. The title track 'Ode to Love' from NCT WISH's first full-length album, released on April 20, immediately climbed to No. 3 on Melon TOP100 and No. 1 on HOT100 based on peak rankings, setting new personal chart bests for the group. All tracks from the album also entered major domestic music charts including Melon and Bugs, signaling the group's emergence as a dominant force on the charts.

The album recorded first-day sales of 1,285,184 copies on the Hanteo Chart (as of April 21), achieving million-seller status in a single day and setting the group's own fastest record. This marks the beginning of what promises to be an unstoppable upward trajectory for the album.

With this achievement, NCT WISH has now become a million-seller for the third time overall, following their second mini-album 'poppop' and third mini-album 'COLOR' released last year, continuing a dazzling career-high streak.

Beyond Hanteo, the album swept the daily No. 1 spot on major domestic album charts including Yes24 and the Circle Chart Retail Album Chart. Internationally, it topped the QQ Music digital album sales chart in China and earned a 'Double Gold' certification — awarded when sales exceed 500,000 yuan — as well as No. 1 on the integrated K-pop chart across five Tencent Music platforms, No. 2 on Kugou Music's digital album sales chart, No. 1 on Japan's AWA real-time trending chart, and No. 2 on Line Music's real-time album ranking.

The music video for 'Ode to Love' has also been well received for its visuals embodying what fans call 'Wishcore,' maximizing NCT WISH's distinctive sensibility. The music video for album track 'Sticky,' pre-released on April 13, topped South Korea's YouTube daily trending music video chart and ranked No. 1 on QQ Music's weekly Korean music video chart in China, once again demonstrating the high level of global fan interest in this album.

NCT WISH's first full-length album 'Ode to Love' consists of 10 tracks in total, including the title track of the same name, and captures a more defined musical identity for the group.
