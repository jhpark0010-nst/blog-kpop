NMIXX will release their fifth mini album 'Heavy Serenade' on May 11, according to JYP Entertainment. The album features a title track written by singer-songwriter Hanroro, marking a notable collaboration for the group's latest comeback.

According to JYP Entertainment's announcement on April 23, Hanroro wrote the lyrics for the title track 'Heavy Serenade.' Hanroro is a musician regarded as a representative voice of the MZ generation, who made a major impact last year with the release of the mini album 'Grapefruit Apricot Club' and a novel of the same name.

The album contains a total of six tracks, including 'Crescendo' and 'Different Girl,' both of which feature lyrics written by NMIXX members Lily and Bae.

Before the album's official release, NMIXX will give fans an early preview of select tracks. On April 24, they will perform the album track 'Superior' at the pre-event of the e-sports festival 'LCK Team Road Show: T1 Home Ground.' On April 28, the music video for 'Crescendo' will be released.
