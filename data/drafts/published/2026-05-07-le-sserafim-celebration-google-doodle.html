Girl group LE SSERAFIM has collaborated with Google for their new song 'CELEBRATION,' their agency Source Music announced on May 7. To mark May as Asian American, Native Hawaiian, and Pacific Islander (AANHPI) Heritage Month, Google unveiled a new Google Doodle featuring 'CELEBRATION.' A Google Doodle is a campaign that decorates the search engine's main page around anniversaries, historical events, and other themes.

Clicking the main page image on Google plays an animation of people from around the world dancing to the music of 'CELEBRATION.' LE SSERAFIM is set to make their comeback on the 22nd with their second full-length album 'PUREFLOW pt.1,' which includes 'CELEBRATION.'

Band Soran will hold a concert titled 'Go Young-bae's Mild Dopamine Addiction Treatment Concert Goseumdochikon' at Yeonnamjang, a multi-purpose cultural space in Mapo-gu, Seoul, from the 15th to the 17th, their agency MPMG Music announced. The concert is a project personally planned by Go Young-bae through his personal YouTube channel 'Goran's Soyoungbae.' It is being held with the intention of reducing stimulation inside the concert venue, as a contrast to daily life accustomed to fast and intense stimulation.

In keeping with this intention, cheering, clapping, and the use of electronic devices will be restricted at the concert. Go Young-bae said, 'I am preparing with even more excitement, as this is a concert I have always wanted to put on.' He added, 'I hope the audience will also have a time to immerse themselves in dopamine addiction treatment.'

Girl group BABYMONSTER's challenge videos for their new song 'CHOOM' are rapidly increasing across various platforms, their agency YG Entertainment announced. According to YG, as of May 7, videos using the 'CHOOM' track on major platforms including YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok have surpassed 100,000.

YG stated, 'Dance challenge videos for the chorus section, in which music fans from around the world have directly participated, make up a large portion,' and added, 'YG's general producer Yang Hyun-suk personally participated in the chorus choreography. The choreography commission, which is usually given to 2 to 3 teams, was this time exceptionally expanded to a scale of 10 teams.' BABYMONSTER will also kick off their second world tour 'CHOOM' at Seoul's Jamsil Indoor Stadium from June 26 to 28.
