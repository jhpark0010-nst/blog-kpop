CORTIS's second mini album 'GREENGREEN' sold 2,313,291 copies in its first week of release, according to Hanteo Chart data. Label Big Hit Music announced the figure on May 11. This is more than five times the 436,367 copies recorded by the group's debut album 'COLOR OUTSIDE THE LINES' in September of last year.

First-week sales are widely used as a key indicator of a fandom's size and an artist's popularity. The approximately 2.31 million copies CORTIS achieved ranks second among all albums released this year, behind BTS's fifth album 'ARIRANG,' which recorded 4.17 million copies.

On Spotify, the world's largest music streaming platform, CORTIS's monthly listener count increased by more than 40% over the past month, reaching 10,322,254 listeners as of May 10.

Singer BIBI will release her new single 'BUMPA' on May 20 at 6 p.m., according to her label Feelghood Music. 'BUMPA' is a song featuring a seasonal feel ahead of summer and a lively sound. The track was first unveiled at BIBI's solo concert 'Won't You Come?' in March 2023, and following continued requests from fans for an official release, it has been decided to release it officially more than three years later. Feelghood Music stated, 'BIBI, who held a world tour last year, is set to be active in music this year as well, showcasing her talents as a singer-songwriter and all-around musician.'

NCT will hold a pop-up event called 'NEO GROUND' at Pie Factory in Gwangjin-gu, Seoul from May 15 to 23 to mark the group's 10th anniversary, label SM Entertainment announced. 'NEO GROUND' is a space that embodies NCT's identity and energy through a 'Futurism Sports' concept, featuring diverse interactive content, photo zones, and special merchandise. Every day during the exhibition period, from noon to 1 p.m., the event will operate as an exclusive session for official fan club members of NCT 127, NCT DREAM, WayV, and NCT WISH.
