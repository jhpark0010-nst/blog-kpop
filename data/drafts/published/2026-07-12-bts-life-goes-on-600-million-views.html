BTS's music video for 'Life Goes On' has surpassed 600 million views on YouTube, their agency Big Hit Music announced on July 12. The milestone was reached at approximately 12:34 a.m. KST that day.

'Life Goes On' is the title track of BTS's mini album 'BE (Deluxe Edition)', released in November 2020. Member Jungkook participated as the music video's director.

The video warmly captures the longing and regret of being unable to meet fans in person during the COVID-19 pandemic, featuring scenes of the members performing in an empty concert venue with no audience.

In other news, Ador announced that 'Right Now', a track from NewJeans' Japanese debut single 'Supernatural', has surpassed 100 million streams on Spotify, the world's largest music streaming platform.

Released in June 2024, the song recorded 100,054,018 streams as of July 10. It marks the group's 16th song overall to surpass 100 million streams on Spotify.

'Right Now' is characterized by its drum and bass beat and the members' lively vocal tones. Upon release, the song gained popularity for its comfortable yet sophisticated sound, charting alongside the title track 'Supernatural' in the upper ranks of major domestic music charts in South Korea.
