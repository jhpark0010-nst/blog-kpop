SEVENTEEN's Hoshi has released a new song titled 'SNAPBACK' at 6 p.m. on June 15, 2026 — coinciding with his birthday. His agency, Pledis Entertainment, made the announcement while Hoshi is currently serving in the military.

'SNAPBACK' features old-school-style guitar sounds and a polished rhythm that creates an upbeat atmosphere. The track witfully expresses an irresistible attraction toward someone.

Hoshi also participated in writing and composing the new song.

He enlisted in the Army in September of last year and is currently serving as an active-duty soldier.

SEVENTEEN, the group Hoshi belongs to, will hold their tenth fan meeting, 'SEVENTEEN in CARAT LAND,' at Incheon Asiad Main Stadium on June 20 and 21. The event has sold out all seats.
