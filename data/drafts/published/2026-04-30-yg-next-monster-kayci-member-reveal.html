YG Entertainment has officially revealed KAYCI as the third member of its upcoming new girl group, tentatively titled 'Next Monster.' On April 30, YG posted a video titled 'YG NEXT MONSTER | KAYCI - When We Were Young' on its official blog, drawing widespread attention from global fans eagerly following the next girl group to debut from YG after BLACKPINK and BABYMONSTER.

KAYCI is the third member to be introduced, following Ivelyi and Chanya, with her reveal coming 10 months after the previous member introduction. According to YG, KAYCI was born to a Korean father and a Chinese mother, and grew up in China. She speaks Korean and Chinese at a native level and is also fluent in English, making her trilingual and a globally oriented member of the group.

In addition to her language abilities, KAYCI holds an all-rounder position, capable of both singing and rapping while also possessing exceptional dance skills. In the video released on the day, she immediately captivated listeners with a clean yet soulful vocal performance. Her husky yet delicate vocal tone added depth to the song, and her remarkable emotional control — which stands in contrast to her youthful appearance — drew admiration.

KAYCI further demonstrated her overwhelming vocal ability by delivering stable mid-to-low register notes as well as powerful high notes at the song's climax. She has firmly established her presence as a 'monster rookie' poised to lead the next generation of YG, and as a well-prepared all-rounder.

Born in 2012, KAYCI is just 13 years old, yet has already proven her boundless potential. Within just one and a half years as a trainee, she mastered vocals, dance, and rap across all areas to emerge as a true all-rounder. With Ivelyi, Chanya, and now KAYCI confirmed, the team's lineup is coming into clearer focus, and attention is now turning to the final remaining member needed to complete Next Monster's full lineup.
