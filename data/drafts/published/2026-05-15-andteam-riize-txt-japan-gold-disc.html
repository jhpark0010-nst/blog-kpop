&TEAM, RIIZE, and other K-pop acts have received Gold Disc certifications from the Recording Industry Association of Japan (RIAJ), according to an announcement made on May 15, 2026.

&TEAM's Japanese mini album Vol. 3, 'We on Fire,' surpassed a cumulative shipment of 750,000 copies as of April, earning the Gold Disc 'Triple Platinum' certification.

The RIAJ awards monthly certifications based on cumulative album shipment figures: Gold (100,000 copies or more), Platinum (250,000 or more), Double Platinum (500,000 or more), Triple Platinum (750,000 or more), and Million (1,000,000 or more).

In addition, RIIZE's Japanese single Vol. 2, 'All of You,' received 'Double Platinum' certification, while TOMORROW X TOGETHER's mini album Vol. 8, '7TH YEAR: When the Wind Briefly Stopped in the Thorns,' received 'Platinum' certification.

NiziU, a Japan-localized group created by JYP Entertainment, earned 'Gold' certification with their mini album Vol. 2, 'GOOD GIRL BUT NOT FOR YOU.'
