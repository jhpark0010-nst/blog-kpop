BTS has claimed their 6th No. 1 on the Billboard Global (excl. US) chart in 2026, surpassing Taylor Swift's record for the most No. 1s on that chart in a single year. According to the latest Billboard charts dated May 23, released on May 19, 'SWIM' — the title track from BTS's fifth full-length album ARIRANG — held the top spot for the second consecutive week.

On the Global 200, BTS landed at No. 2 for the third week in a row. All 13 vocal tracks from the album have remained on both charts for eight consecutive weeks.

ARIRANG continues to perform strongly on the main album chart, the Billboard 200, holding at No. 8 for eight straight weeks in the top 10. On sub-charts, the album ranked No. 5 on Top Album Sales and No. 16 on both Top Streaming Albums and Vinyl Albums. 'SWIM' entered the Hot 100 at No. 34 for the eighth consecutive week, and appeared across multiple airplay charts: Adult Contemporary (No. 16), Pop Airplay (No. 11), Adult Pop Airplay (No. 18), and Radio Songs (No. 29).

For the second consecutive week, four BTS albums charted on World Albums. 'Proof,' released in 2022, rose one spot to No. 3 and marked its 205th week on the chart, demonstrating remarkable longevity. Also charting were the Japanese fourth full-length album 'MAP OF THE SOUL: 7 ~ THE JOURNEY ~' (No. 11), the third full-length album 'LOVE YOURSELF 轉 Tear' (No. 16), and the fifth mini album 'LOVE YOURSELF 承 HER' (No. 25).

Meanwhile, BTS is currently on their North American tour, 'BTS WORLD TOUR ARIRANG IN NORTH AMERICA.'
