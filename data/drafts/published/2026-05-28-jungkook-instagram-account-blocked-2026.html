BTS member Jungkook's Instagram account was blocked on May 27, 2026. The account had amassed 21.61 million followers at the time of the sudden suspension.

On the 27th, Jungkook posted a single screenshot on his TikTok account along with a '?' expressing his bewilderment. The screenshot displayed a notice reading, 'JK, your account has been blocked.' The notice also included a warning stating that 'if you do not request a review within 180 days, your account will be permanently deactivated,' with the block date listed as May 27, 2026.

Instagram explained the reason for the block as follows: 'Your account, or activity on your account, does not comply with our terms of service regarding intellectual property rights, including our policy on trademark rights.'

Jungkook had previously left his original Instagram account — which had 52.4 million followers — in February 2023, and opened a new account in July of last year. His new Instagram account had surpassed 21.61 million followers until it was abruptly blocked.

Meanwhile, BTS, the group Jungkook belongs to, won three awards at the 2026 American Music Awards (2026 AMAs), held on the 25th (local time) at the MGM Grand Garden Arena in Las Vegas, USA — one of the three major popular music awards ceremonies. The awards included Artist of the Year, Song of the Summer, and Best Male K-Pop Artist.
