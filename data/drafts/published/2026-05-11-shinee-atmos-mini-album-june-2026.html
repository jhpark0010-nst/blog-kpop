SHINee is set to make their comeback on June 1, 2026 with their sixth mini album 'Atmos'. The group will release the full album at 6 PM KST across major music platforms on the same day. The mini album includes a total of 6 tracks spanning various genres, with the title track also titled 'Atmos'.

This new release marks approximately one year since SHINee dropped their single 'Poet | Artist' in May 2025, making it a highly anticipated return for global fans. The comeback also signals the resumption of activities for member Key, who had previously been embroiled in controversy over alleged illegal medical procedures.

SHINee previously earned widespread praise through their eighth full-length album 'HARD' in 2023, showcasing a new musical direction and powerful performances. With 'Atmos', the group is said to be returning to their signature refined and sophisticated mood — described as 'the most SHINee-like' musical world yet.

SHINee has long been celebrated for their unrivaled concept versatility, solid live vocals, and dynamic performances. Fans are eagerly anticipating what new charms the group will bring through this latest album.
