BTS is adding one more show to the Melbourne leg of their 'Arirang' world tour in February 2027, label Big Hit Music announced on June 2. The newly added performance is scheduled for February 10 (local time), ahead of the previously planned Melbourne dates.

BTS had originally been set to perform in Melbourne on February 12–13 and in Sydney on February 20–21. The additional Melbourne date on February 10 expands the Australian run to three shows.

With this extra show added, the 'Arirang' world tour — which kicked off in April — has grown to a total of 86 performances across 34 cities worldwide.

This will mark BTS's first concerts in Australia since May 2017, a gap of approximately nine years and ten months.

The Sydney shows will be held at Accor Stadium, one of Australia's largest and most iconic concert venues. Artists such as Adele and Oasis have previously performed there. BTS will be the first Korean act ever to perform at Accor Stadium.
