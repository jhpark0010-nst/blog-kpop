Red Velvet is set to make a full-group comeback in August 2026. On June 15, SM Entertainment announced that the group has confirmed August as the release month for their new album, marking approximately 2 years and 2 months since 'Cosmic,' which was released in June 2024. The agency noted that anticipation among global listeners is running high ahead of the full-group return.

Red Velvet will also be meeting fans in person. The '2026 Red Velvet FAN-CON' is scheduled to be held on August 1 and 2 at Korea University's Hwajeong Gymnasium. August 1 is Red Velvet's debut anniversary, making this a particularly meaningful occasion as the group marks their 12th debut anniversary.
