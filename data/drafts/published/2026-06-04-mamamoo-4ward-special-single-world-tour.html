Girl group MAMAMOO released their special single '4WARD' at 6 p.m. on June 4, according to their label RBW. This marks the group's first new music as a complete unit in 3 years and 8 months, since their twelfth mini-album in October 2022.

The single title '4WARD' combines the number '4,' which symbolizes the group's four members, with the word 'FORWARD,' representing progress and advancement.

The title track '4 Flower' is a medium-tempo pop song featuring sensory guitar work and a dense drum sound. Through the song, the members express values and relationships that remain unchanged over time — likening themselves to flowers that bloom and fade brilliantly yet are ultimately connected by a single root.

MAMAMOO will kick off their new world tour, also titled '4WARD,' at Olympic Hall in Olympic Park in Seoul from June 19 to 21. Starting from Seoul, the group will tour major cities across Asia and the Americas.

MAMAMOO debuted in June 2014 and have released hit songs including 'You're the Best,' 'Egotistic,' and 'Starry Night.'
