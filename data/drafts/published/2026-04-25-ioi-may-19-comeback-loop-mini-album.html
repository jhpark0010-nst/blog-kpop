I.O.I has confirmed a May 19, 2026 comeback. On April 21, Swing Entertainment announced that I.O.I — consisting of Im Nayoung, Chungha, Kim Sejeong, Jung Chaeyeon, Kim Sohye, Yoo Yeonjung, Choi Yoojung, Kim Doyeon, and Jeon Somi — will release their third mini album 'I.O.I : LOOP' on May 19.

Alongside the announcement, a promotion scheduler image for 'I.O.I : LOOP' was revealed on the group's official social media, maximizing fan excitement. The soft pink-toned image is packed with the group's comeback schedule, building anticipation ahead of the release.

According to the scheduler, I.O.I will roll out a series of promotional content starting with a mood film on April 22, followed by concept photos, a track list, an album preview, a music video teaser, and audio snippets, released in sequence.

I.O.I, who recently drew attention with the announcement of the '2026 I.O.I Concert Tour: LOOP', will also unveil the concert's main poster during the promotion period. Additionally, the group will release a pre-release track on May 4 alongside a YouTube live session to connect with fans, before the full album drops on May 19.

I.O.I is a project group formed through public voting on Mnet's 'Produce 101' in 2016, debuting that same May. The group scored multiple hits in a short period, including 'Very Very Very' and 'Downpour', before wrapping up their official activities after a concert in January 2017. Now, approximately 9 years later, their reunion has been realized, drawing an outpouring of support from long-waiting fans.

In addition to the album release, I.O.I will hold the '2026 I.O.I Concert Tour: LOOP' at Seoul's Jamsil Indoor Stadium for three days starting May 29, celebrating their 10th debut anniversary with a range of activities.
