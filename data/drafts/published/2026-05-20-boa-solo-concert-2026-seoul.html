BoA will hold her first solo concert since going independent, following her departure from SM Entertainment last year. The singer announced through social media on May 20 that she will host a fan concert titled 'BoA the MIC' on June 27 and 28 at Yonsei University Grand Auditorium in Seoul.

Ahead of the concert, BoA will release a new digital single titled 'Ain't No Hard Feelings' at midnight on May 30. Both the new single and the upcoming concert mark her first music releases since moving to a new agency.

BoA left SM Entertainment in December of last year after 25 years with the label. She then founded Baypal Entertainment in March of this year.

At the time of the company's launch, Baypal Entertainment stated, 'BoA chose a new structure in order to more clearly realize her own musical direction,' explaining the background behind her decision to go independent.
