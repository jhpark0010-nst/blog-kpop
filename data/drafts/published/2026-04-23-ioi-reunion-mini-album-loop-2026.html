I.O.I is officially making their long-awaited comeback — nine years in the making. Swing Entertainment announced on April 21 that the reunited group will release their third mini album, 'I.O.I : LOOP', on May 19, 2026. The reunion marks the first time the group has come together since their 2017 concert, and it coincides with the 10th anniversary of their debut.

I.O.I originally debuted in 2016 as a project group formed through Mnet's 'Produce 101', one of the most influential K-pop survival shows of its era. The group's 10th debut anniversary served as the catalyst for this reunion, bringing together members who have since gone on to successful solo and group careers.

The reunion lineup will consist of nine members: Im Na-young, Chungha, Kim Se-jeong, Jung Chae-yeon, Kim So-hye, Yoo Yeon-jeong, Choi Yoo-jung, Kim Do-yeon, and Jeon So-mi. Members Kang Mina and Joo Kyulkyung will not be participating due to prior scheduled commitments.

Ahead of the full album release, I.O.I will drop a pre-release track on May 4, giving fans an early taste of the new era. The mini album will then arrive on May 19, building anticipation for what comes next.

Following the album release, I.O.I will hold a three-night concert at the Jamsil Indoor Stadium in Seoul from May 29 to May 31. The venue is a well-known mid-capacity arena in the heart of Seoul, making it an intimate yet significant setting for the group's reunion performances. Fans who have waited nearly a decade for this moment will finally get to see the nine members back on stage together.
