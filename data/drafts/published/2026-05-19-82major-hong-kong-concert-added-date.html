82MAJOR — consisting of members Nam Seongmo, Park Seokjun, Yoon Yechan, Jo Seongyil, Hwang Seongbin, and Kim Dogyun — has added a second date to their Hong Kong solo concert after selling out the first show. The group confirmed a June 28 performance following the sellout of their June 27 concert, 'Bibeom: BE 범,' in Hong Kong.

Tickets for the Hong Kong concert went on sale on the 15th and sold out almost immediately, demonstrating intense interest from global fans. Due to the explosive response and an overwhelming number of requests from local fans, the agency decided to add the additional June 28 show.

82MAJOR has built a strong track record, successfully completing tours in South Korea, Japan, and five European countries. Notably, both their Seoul concert and their Amsterdam show during the European tour sold out completely, showcasing the group's powerful ticket-selling ability.

Their global momentum has also extended to their latest release, the fifth mini-album 'FEELM.' All members directly participated in writing lyrics, composing, and arranging the album, and it achieved a career-high first-week sales record, surpassing their previous releases. The album also reached No. 1 on overseas iTunes charts and entered the top rankings on China's NetEase Music, demonstrating the strength of the group as self-producing artists.
