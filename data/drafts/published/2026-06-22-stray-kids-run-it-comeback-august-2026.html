Stray Kids are set to kick off a series of releases and live events, beginning with a new single on June 24, followed by a full album and a world tour. JYP Entertainment revealed the news at midnight on June 22, posting a teaser for 'RUN IT' on Stray Kids' official social media channels and announcing the digital single will drop on June 24 at 1:00 PM KST.

'RUN IT' is described as a track capturing the energy of Stray Kids pushing forward into a bigger world. The song will be released first as a digital single and will later be included on their upcoming album 'THIS & THAT', set for release on August 7 at 1:00 PM KST.

'THIS & THAT' marks Stray Kids' first album of 2026, arriving approximately nine months after 'SKZ IT TAPE' — 'DO IT', which was released in November 2025.

In addition, Stray Kids will embark on the Stray Kids World Tour 'RUN IT'. In September, they are scheduled to perform at Rock in Rio in Brazil, and during the same month they will hold a new festival called STRAYCITY across three locations in Latin America.

Stray Kids are an eight-member boy group under JYP Entertainment who debuted in 2018. The members are Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N.
