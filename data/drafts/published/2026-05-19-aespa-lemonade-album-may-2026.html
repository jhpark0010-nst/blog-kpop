aespa is set to showcase a cool attitude that turns even adversity into something to enjoy. The group's 2nd full album, 'LEMONADE', drops on May 29 at 1 PM KST and will include a total of 11 tracks — among them the title track 'LEMONADE', the pre-release single 'WDA' (Whole Different Animal) featuring G-Dragon, and a featuring version of 'LEMONADE'.

The title track 'LEMONADE' is an electronic dance song driven by an intense and trendy synth-bass sound. Its lyrics are inspired by the saying 'If life gives you lemons, make lemonade,' hinting at a new season within the group's lore while delivering a positive message about turning any hardship or trial into an opportunity.

Prior to this release, aespa unveiled 'WDA (Whole Different Animal)', which featured dark teaser content and a weighty hip-hop-based performance. With 'LEMONADE', the group is expected to present an entirely different visual concept and a charm distinct from what fans have seen before.
