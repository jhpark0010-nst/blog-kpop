ADOR, the agency of NewJeans, announced on May 7, 2026 that discussions regarding member Minji's future are continuing in a positive direction, responding to rumors of her return to the group.

In response to the speculation about Minji's comeback to NewJeans, ADOR stated, 'We are continuing internal discussions regarding future activities.'

The agency added, 'We do not have a separate position to share regarding specific details at this time,' but noted, 'We would appreciate it if you understood that discussions are ongoing in an overall positive direction.'

Earlier that day, NewJeans' official social media accounts posted several photos showing Minji making cookies, accompanied by the message 'HAPPY MINJI DAY.' The NewJeans account on fan platform Weverse also displayed an image celebrating Minji's birthday on May 7 on its main screen.

Recently, online accounts emerged of Minji making a surprise visit to a fan-organized birthday cafe, where she reportedly handed out handwritten letters and cookies. The comeback rumors gained traction after ADOR celebrated Minji's birthday through its official accounts.

Following their dispute with the agency, NewJeans members Hanni, Haerin, and Hyein returned to ADOR in November of last year. Minji's situation has not yet been decided, while Danielle continues a legal dispute with ADOR after leaving the group.
