DAY6 — consisting of Sungjin, Young K, Wonpil, and Dowoon — will close out their 10th anniversary tour at KSPO DOME (Olympic Gymnastics Arena) in Seoul. JYP Entertainment announced the finale concert, officially titled DAY6 10th Anniversary Tour 'The DECADE' FINALE in SEOUL, on the group's official social media channels at midnight on May 18.

The finale will consist of three solo concerts held over three days, from July 3 to 5, at KSPO DOME in Olympic Park, Songpa-gu, Seoul. This marks the final stop of the 10th anniversary tour, which launched in August 2025 at Goyang Stadium in Gyeonggi Province and spanned 27 shows across 16 cities in Korea and abroad.

The tour, titled 'The DECADE,' was held to celebrate DAY6's 10th debut anniversary and drew enthusiastic responses from audiences with a rich setlist covering the group's full discography. The domestic leg of the tour kicked off in Daegu on February 7-8, continued through Gwangju on March 14-15, Daejeon on March 21-22, and concluded in Busan on May 16-17.

Throughout the tour, the four members delivered the setlist — which captured various moments encountered in everyday life through DAY6's own sensibility and expression — as a live band performance, connecting deeply with audiences at each stop.
