aespa is generating buzz by showcasing their signature universe-building style. On April 28 at midnight, the group released teaser images and videos for their second full album 'LEMONADE' across various SNS channels, heightening anticipation for their comeback with visuals that emphasize 'Lemonade' as the album's main concept.

The teaser content depicts a singularity occurring in the settings of previous works — 'Savage', 'MY WORLD', 'Armageddon', and 'Whiplash' — hinting at cracks formed as the various worlds aespa has experienced begin to collide and intertwine, foreshadowing the new universe that will unfold in the upcoming release.

Additionally, an interactive experience has launched on the official website (aespa.com), where fans can create their own P.O.S. (cracks) through a game involving smashing the world with a hammer and throwing lemons, offering a unique and fun way to engage with the approaching comeback.

Through their second full album 'LEMONADE', aespa plans to unfold a new chapter in their universe. Their new world tour 'SYNK : COMPLaeXITY', which begins at Seoul's Gocheok Sky Dome in August before traveling through North and South America and Europe, will also center on the keyword 'CRACK', presenting performances, stages, and visual compositions that are differentiated from the existing 'aeXIS LINE'.

aespa's second full album 'LEMONADE', set for release on May 29, contains a total of 10 tracks spanning diverse genres including the title track. It is set to present a more solidified universe concept while also showcasing a more mature musical color from aespa.

aespa's second full album 'LEMONADE' will be released on May 29 at 1 PM KST across various music platforms, and pre-orders are currently available at both online and offline music stores.
