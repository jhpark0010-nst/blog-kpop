BTS continued their chart run in the UK, with their fifth studio album ARIRANG charting for a sixth consecutive week on the UK Official Albums Chart Top 100. According to the Official Charts dated May 1 (local time), ARIRANG entered at No.17, dropping four spots from the previous week.

The album's title track SWIM also remained on the UK Official Singles Chart Top 100 for a sixth straight week, landing at No.41 — down seven places from the prior week.

HYBE's Korean-American girl group KATSEYE placed two songs on the same singles chart: 'Pinky Up' at No.42 and 'TOUCH' at No.82.

The original soundtrack 'GOLDEN' from the Netflix animated film 'K-Pop Demon Hunters' held steady at No.36, marking its 45th consecutive week on the chart.

Meanwhile, on Spotify's Weekly Top Songs Global chart, BTS's SWIM ranked at No.2, and all 14 tracks from ARIRANG appeared on the chart for a sixth consecutive week. The album also came in at No.2 on Spotify's Weekly Top Albums Global.
