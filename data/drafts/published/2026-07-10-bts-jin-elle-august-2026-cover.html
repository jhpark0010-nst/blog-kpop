BTS member Jin has graced the cover of Elle magazine's August 2026 issue. The cover shoot was accompanied by an in-depth interview in which Jin spoke about meeting audiences around the world through the BTS WORLD TOUR 'ARIRANG'.

In the interview, Jin shared what stands out most to him about the tour so far. 'Starting this world tour in Goyang is what I remember most,' he said. 'It rained so heavily that day. Rain always tends to leave a strong impression on me. It was such a fun moment — the kind where you think, when else in your life would you get soaked like that?'

Marking 13 years since his debut, Jin was asked what music has given him. Without a moment's hesitation, he answered: 'I think I've gained everything. Music is what gave me my life as it is now.'

He also reflected on the word 'Jin-like' — the term that best describes who he is today. 'I think I'm someone who wants to live the way I want to,' he said. 'Of course, I can't do everything — but still. (laughs)'
