Pop star Justin Bieber will share the stage with BTS, Madonna, and Shakira. The New York Times reported on July 8 (local time) that Justin Bieber and Burna Boy will perform at the halftime show of the 2026 FIFA World Cup Final, to be held on July 19 at New York New Jersey Stadium.

Bieber said, 'The World Cup brings the world together in a way no one else can,' adding, 'I am thrilled to be part of the halftime show.'

BTS, Madonna, and Shakira had already been confirmed as co-headliners for the halftime show. Coldplay, conductor Gustavo Dudamel, and the P.S. 22 Chorus — a choir made up of elementary school students — are also set to participate.

Characters from the children's program Sesame Street have also been added to the lineup.

The show, with performers selected by Coldplay member Chris Martin, will run for a total of 11 minutes and be broadcast live around the world.

The halftime show format was traditionally associated with the NFL championship game, the Super Bowl, but this marks the first time it has been introduced to a World Cup Final. The show aims to raise $100 million USD, which will be used for children's education and soccer training around the world.
