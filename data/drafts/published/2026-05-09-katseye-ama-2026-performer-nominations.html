Global girl group KATSEYE will take the stage as a performer at the American Music Awards (AMA), HYBE and Geffen Records announced on May 9. The AMA, considered one of the three biggest popular music awards shows in the United States, will be held on May 25 (local time) at the MGM Grand Garden Arena in Las Vegas.

KATSEYE has also been nominated in three categories: New Artist of the Year, Best Music Video, and Breakthrough Pop Artist.

In other news, KOZ Entertainment announced on May 9 that BOYNEXTDOOR will release their first full-length album, HOME, on June 8. The group opened the official website for the album on May 8 to announce their comeback.

The comeback marks approximately eight months since BOYNEXTDOOR released their fifth mini album, 'The Action,' in October of last year — an album with which they surpassed one million copies sold, achieving million-seller status.

F&F Entertainment announced on May 9 that group Ahop (아홉) will hold their first Asia tour, 'THE FIRST SPARK,' beginning with Seoul concerts on May 30 and 31 at Blue Square in Yongsan-gu, Seoul. The title 'THE FIRST SPARK' carries the meaning that Ahop's first steps will become a single spark, leaving a powerful impression on the world.

Following the Seoul performances, Ahop will hold a total of 10 shows across 8 cities, including Osaka, Japan on June 25, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia on July 4, and Taipei, Taiwan on August 2.
