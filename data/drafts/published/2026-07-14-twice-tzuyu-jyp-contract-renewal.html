TWICE member Tzuyu is currently in contract renewal discussions with JYP Entertainment. On July 14, a JYP Entertainment representative officially responded to reports of a failed re-signing, telling MyDaily: 'TWICE is in a contract renewal discussion period. We will inform you as soon as the matter is confirmed.'

Earlier that same day, News1 reported that Tzuyu had agreed not to renew her contract with her current agency, JYP Entertainment. However, the report also noted that TWICE's full-group activities are expected to continue.

Tzuyu, who is originally from Taiwan, debuted as a member of TWICE in 2015. All TWICE members previously renewed their contracts with JYP Entertainment once before, in 2022.
