T.O.P has announced the start of his first Asia fan meeting tour to mark his 20th debut anniversary. Fans who packed the venue in Yokohama, Japan, reunited with him after a long wait, chanting 'We are each other's T.O.P.'

The former BIGBANG member held a free fan meeting on July 9 at Pia Arena MM in Yokohama, Japan, for approximately 10,000 members of his official Japanese fan community. As his first-ever solo fan meeting in the 20 years since he began his entertainment career in 2006, the venue was filled with excitement even before the event started.

Videos and photos shared by fans on social media showed banners reading 'We are each other's T.O.P' displayed throughout the venue, drawing attention. Fans welcomed T.O.P's return with unwavering support despite his long absence.

T.O.P took the stage dressed in an all-black suit, showcasing his signature charisma. He performed a live rendition of his solo track 'Completely Crazy! (Studio54)', demonstrating that his rap skills and stage presence remain as sharp as ever.

Fans responded with thunderous cheers to his explosive energy, and the venue was instantly filled with heat. Reacting to a performance that made his long hiatus feel irrelevant, fans commented that 'the BIGBANG DNA is not dead.'

T.O.P will continue his first Asia fan meeting tour, titled T.O.P PRE-STUDIO 2026, starting with this Yokohama show. Further details on upcoming performance dates and locations will be announced sequentially through official channels. Meanwhile, T.O.P was indicted on marijuana charges in 2017 and officially departed from BIGBANG in 2023. Following his period of reflection, he drew attention for playing the role of Thanos in the Netflix series 'Squid Game Season 2.' In press interviews at the time, he expressed remorse and stated clearly that he has no intention of rejoining BIGBANG.
