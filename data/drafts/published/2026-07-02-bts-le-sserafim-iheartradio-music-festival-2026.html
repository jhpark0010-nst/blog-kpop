BTS and LE SSERAFIM have been confirmed to perform at the 2026 iHeartRadio Music Festival. According to the iHeartRadio website, the event will be held on September 18 and 19 at T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas, Nevada.

The two K-pop acts will share the lineup with artists including Snoop Dogg, Cardi B, Muse, and Weezer. BTS is scheduled to take the stage on September 18, while LE SSERAFIM will perform on September 19.

This marks a historic moment for K-pop girl groups, as LE SSERAFIM becomes the first K-pop girl group ever to perform at the iHeartRadio Music Festival.

The iHeartRadio Music Festival is an annual event hosted by iHeartRadio, the American online radio platform. Performances will be broadcast live across more than 150 iHeartMedia radio stations and will also be available to stream on Disney+ and Hulu.
