82MAJOR (82메이저) will hold a solo concert in Bangkok, Thailand, following their Hong Kong show. The six-member group — Nam Seong-mo, Park Seok-jun, Yoon Ye-chan, Jo Seong-il, Hwang Seong-bin, and Kim Do-gyun — will perform at IDEA LIVE, BRAVO BKK on July 4 at 6 p.m. (Bangkok time). The concert is titled 'Bibeom : BE 범.'

The Bangkok show was made possible by the overwhelming support of global fans, following the June Hong Kong concert, which sold out all seats including additional performances. The group also sold out their Amsterdam show during a recent European tour, demonstrating strong ticket power. General ticketing for the Bangkok concert opens on May 30 at 12 p.m. Bangkok time through official ticketing channels.

82MAJOR recently released their fifth mini album FEELM, setting a new personal best in album sales and proving their step-by-step growth. The album reached No. 1 on the iTunes charts in Spain and Vietnam, made a reverse-charting entry on the French iTunes chart, and also ranked highly on China's NetEase Music.

In response to this popularity, 82MAJOR plans to deliver powerful dance performances and steady live vocals built on the experience they have accumulated through domestic and international solo concerts. The group has expressed their determination to fully captivate local fans at the Bangkok concert as well.

Meanwhile, 82MAJOR is currently active on music broadcast programs with their title track 'Sign.' They plan to continue their fifth mini album promotions through various music shows and online and offline content, while also accelerating preparations for the concert. 82MAJOR is a six-member boy group that debuted in 2023.
