ATEEZ added their 6th Daesang trophy to their collection at the 35th Seoul Music Awards (서울가요대상), held on June 20 at Inspire Arena in Incheon. The group swept the night by winning both the Bonsang (main award) and the Daesang (grand prize), the highest honor at the ceremony.

Upon receiving the Daesang, ATEEZ shared a heartfelt statement expressing gratitude to their fans. 'We are most grateful to ATINY above all else. We believe this award belongs to them,' they said. 'It feels surreal to receive an award we have dreamed of for so long. We will never lose our original passion and will sing until our voices give out. We take this as a sign to keep pushing forward and will work hard on stage.'

ATEEZ has been on a remarkable global run. Their 13th mini album debuted at No. 3 on the Billboard 200, setting a new personal record for first-week album sales in the United States. The achievement also marked their 6th consecutive top-3 entry on the Billboard 200, cementing their status as a leading global K-pop act.

The group further demonstrated their commercial power with first-week sales (known as 'chodon') of over 1.54 million copies, making it their 6th million-selling album overall. The Seoul Music Awards Daesang win adds yet another milestone to what has been an extraordinary run for the group.

Meanwhile, ATEEZ is set to release their 14th mini album, 'Golden Hour: Part 5,' on June 26 at 1 p.m. KST.
