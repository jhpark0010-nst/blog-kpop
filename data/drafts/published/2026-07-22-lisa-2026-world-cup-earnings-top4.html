BLACKPINK's Lisa is estimated to have earned approximately 9 million pounds (around 17.8 billion Korean won) through the 2026 North America World Cup, according to a report by British outlet The Sun citing analysis from brand and culture experts. The figure includes earnings from advertisements and global brand ambassador deals during the World Cup period.

This places Lisa at 4th in the ranking of World Cup-related artist earnings. She follows Shakira at 15 million pounds (approximately 29.7 billion won), and Justin Bieber and Timothée Chalamet tied at 2nd with 12 million pounds each (approximately 23.7 billion won). Among all the celebrities covered in The Sun's report, Lisa was the only K-pop artist featured.

Last month, Lisa performed the official World Cup anthem 'GOALS' alongside Anita and Rema at the tournament's opening ceremony held in Los Angeles, USA. By taking the stage at the opening of the world's greatest sporting event, she expanded her presence beyond music to sports fans around the globe.
