Girl band QWER has announced their second tour. On July 12, QWER — consisting of members Chodan, Magenta, Hina, and Siyeon — posted the official poster for 'QWER 2nd TOUR ROCKATION: ROCKET LAUNCH!!' (hereafter 'ROCKET LAUNCH!!') on their official social media channels.

The revealed poster features a guitar-shaped rocket soaring powerfully against a space backdrop. Cat characters symbolizing each of the four members appear dressed in spacesuits, drawing attention.

According to the announcement, QWER will visit nine major Asian cities in sequence: Seoul on September 12–13, Taipei on October 10–11, Fukuoka on October 13, Osaka on October 15, Tokyo on October 17, Kuala Lumpur on November 7, Bangkok on November 14, and then continuing into 2027 with Hong Kong on January 2 and Singapore on January 8–9.

'ROCKET LAUNCH!!' — as the name suggests — is a tour filled with the ambition to imprint QWER's music on a wider world, like a rocket launching into space. The group plans to showcase their grown musical capabilities as well as QWER's uniquely vibrant imagination on stage.

QWER previously heated up the Americas and Asia through their debut world tour, '2025 QWER 1ST WORLD TOUR ROCKATION,' solidifying their fanbase both at home and abroad. The group plans to use that musical journey as a springboard to reach an even wider stage.

The tour kicks off at Korea University's Hwajeong Gymnasium in Seoul on September 12–13. Tickets will be available via Melon Ticket, with fan club pre-sales beginning August 12 at 8 p.m. and general ticketing opening August 13 at 8 p.m.
