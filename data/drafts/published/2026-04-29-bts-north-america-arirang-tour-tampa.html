BTS drew approximately 190,000 fans over three nights — April 25, 26, and 28 (local time) — at Raymond James Stadium in Tampa, Florida, as part of their world tour 'ARIRANG.' Big Hit Music announced the figures on April 29.

At the concerts, BTS performed 'Hooligan' from their fifth studio album ARIRANG, along with hit songs including 'MIC Drop' and 'FAKE LOVE.'

During the performance of 'Body to Body,' the agency noted that the audience sang along together when the Korean folk song 'Arirang' melody embedded in the track played out.

The members said at the concert, 'We were a little worried since this was the first show of the North America tour, but thanks to all of you, those worries completely disappeared.' They added, 'You made the start of the North America tour the best it could be, and now we are even more excited for the shows ahead.'

BTS will next hold concerts on May 2 and 3 at Sun Bowl Stadium in El Paso, Texas — becoming the first Korean artist to perform at that venue. The North America tour spans 31 shows across 12 cities, including Mexico City, Stanford, and Las Vegas, with every show sold out.
