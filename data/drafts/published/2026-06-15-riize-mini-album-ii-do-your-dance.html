RIIZE made their comeback on June 15, 2026 at 6 p.m. KST with their second mini album 'II', according to their agency SM Entertainment. The release marks the group's first Korean album of the year.

The new album includes six tracks: the title song 'Do Your Dance', along with 'SOAR', 'D-D-Done', 'Overdrive', 'Like a Bomb', and 'In a Loop'.

Member Anton shared his thoughts through the agency, saying, 'Since this is the first album RIIZE is releasing in Korea this year, it feels like our activities are finally starting in earnest.' He added, 'If we've mostly shown RIIZE going through the worries of growing and realizing our dreams, with this album you'll be able to meet a RIIZE that moves through action rather than worry. The journey toward going higher and further has begun.'

'Do Your Dance' is an uptempo dance track combining hip-hop beats with electronic pop sound. The members express free-spirited energy and confidence throughout the song, with a striking contrast between an intense distortion 808 bass and a casually repetitive chorus. Member Eunseok described the title track as 'perfect to listen to when you want to feel an incredible boost of confidence, or when you want to forget all your worries and just have fun.' Anton, referencing the lyric 'Like a pro' in the song, said, 'I think overcoming difficulties while enjoying yourself is a pro mindset. While preparing for this comeback, I learned new things again, and thanks to that I gained more confidence. I want to give that same confidence to everyone who listens.'

Member Wonbin commented on the choreography for the title track: 'Among all of RIIZE's performances so far, this is the first time we've had a chorus choreography this relaxed. Because the point is to hold your breath, stay composed, and strip away a lot of the movements, I think it will actually feel more charming.'

RIIZE held a countdown live on their YouTube and TikTok channels at 4:30 p.m. KST on the same day, with the music video dropping alongside the audio release at 6 p.m. Member Sungchan reflected, 'Since this is an album we prepared after finishing our first world tour, I naturally felt growth and change without even realizing it. While recording, I thought more about how to shape my sound to suit the song, and I sang while imagining what it would be like to stand on stage.' RIIZE debuted in 2023 with 'Get A Guitar' and went on to gain popularity with tracks including 'Love 119', 'Boom Boom Bass', and 'Fly Up'. Last year, they released their first full-length album 'ODYSSEY' and embarked on their first concert tour.
