ILLIT member Moka has once again announced a temporary halt to all activities due to deteriorating health, just shortly after returning to the group. BELIFT LAB confirmed the news through an official notice on June 19, 2026.

BELIFT LAB stated, 'Since the preparation period for the Mini 4th Album comeback, Moka has been undergoing treatment and recovery due to excessive tension and anxiety symptoms.' The agency added, 'Based on the judgment that more stable rest is needed for sufficient recovery, it has been decided that she will suspend all activities for the time being and focus on regaining her health.'

This second suspension comes after a heartfelt effort by Moka to return to the stage. She had originally halted activities on April 20 after receiving a medical opinion that she required treatment and rest due to a sudden health issue. As a result, ILLIT's Mini 4th Album 'MAMIHLAPINATAPAI' comeback promotions were carried out as a four-member unit without Moka.

However, driven by a strong desire to meet fans, Moka announced her return in late May — approximately one month later — and briefly rejoined the group for the final week of music show (music broadcast) performances. BELIFT LAB explained, 'Due to the artist's strong will to meet fans, she participated in some of the final week's music broadcasts, but after careful discussion, we chose rest once again, prioritizing the artist's wellness above all else.'

Following this decision, ILLIT will once again operate as a four-member unit for the time being. BELIFT LAB said, 'We are deeply sorry to deliver this news to the fans who have been waiting, and Moka herself also feels greatly disappointed.' The agency added, 'ILLIT will continue activities as a four-member group for the time being, and we will spare no support necessary for Moka's recovery and stability so that she can stand before fans again.'

Below is the full official statement from BELIFT LAB. Moka has been undergoing treatment and recovery due to excessive tension and anxiety symptoms since the Mini 4th Album comeback preparation period. Although she participated in some of the final week's music broadcasts driven by her strong will to meet fans, careful discussions were held with the artist and members, leading to the decision that more stable rest is needed. As a result, Moka has decided to suspend all activities for the time being and focus on her health. ILLIT will continue as a four-member group, and the timing of Moka's return to activities will be announced at a later date.
