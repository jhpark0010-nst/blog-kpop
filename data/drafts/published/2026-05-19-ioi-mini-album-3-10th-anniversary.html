I.O.I is releasing their third mini album, 'I.O.I : LOOP', on May 19, 2026 at 6 p.m. KST, marking their first new album in 9 years. Swing Entertainment announced the release as the group celebrates their 10th debut anniversary. I.O.I was originally formed through Mnet's 'Produce 101' in 2016 and concluded their activities after approximately 8 months the following year.

The new album looks back on the time that has passed and authentically captures the meaning of reuniting now. The title track 'Gapjagi' (Suddenly) is a synth-pop song that traces the lingering images of longing and memories that arrive unexpectedly, without warning. Member Jeon Somi participated in writing the lyrics.

I.O.I will also hold a solo concert at Jamsil Indoor Stadium in Songpa-gu, Seoul from May 29 to 31.

Band Wave to Earth will kick off a world tour on September 4 in Vancouver, Canada, followed by stops in Seattle, New York, Bangkok, Seoul, and more — spanning a total of 30 cities, according to their agency Wavy. The tour includes performances at iconic American venues Radio City Music Hall and The Greek Theatre. Wave to Earth, who released their new single 'heaven and hell' on May 15, have performed over 100 shows on the global stage, including at Lollapalooza.

ATEEZ will headline British Summer Time Hyde Park in the UK on June 28, KQ Entertainment announced. British Summer Time Hyde Park is one of the UK's major music festivals, held every summer in Hyde Park in central London. ATEEZ will take the stage alongside global pop stars including Maroon 5 and Garth Brooks. The group was previously invited to the world-renowned Mawazine festival in 2024 as the first K-pop artists to perform there.
