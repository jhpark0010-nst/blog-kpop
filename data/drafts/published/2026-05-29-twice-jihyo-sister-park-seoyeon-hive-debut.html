Park Seoyeon, the youngest sister of TWICE member Jihyo (real name Park Jihyo), is preparing to debut as a member of a new girl group under HIVE. According to music industry sources on May 29, the group will be launched in the second half of 2026 under ABD, a newly established label within HIVE.

Park Seoyeon is the youngest of three sisters. ABD has already begun promoting the upcoming debut by posting a short teaser video on its official social media accounts.

Jihyo's second sister, Park Jiyoung, is currently active as a model under the name Lee Haeum.
