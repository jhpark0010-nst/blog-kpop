A long-rumored collaboration between three of HYBE Labels' leading groups has officially been confirmed. LE SSERAFIM, ILLIT, and KATSEYE have joined forces on a digital single, set to be released simultaneously across global music markets on June 12, 2026.

Source Music, Belift Lab, and HYBE-Geffen Records announced on June 8 via Weverse that the three groups' collaborative digital single, 'ICONIC BY MISTAKE,' will drop worldwide on June 12 at 1 PM.

A joint performance has also been announced. One day ahead of the single's release, on June 11, the three groups will appear together on Mnet's M Countdown to unveil the performance stage for 'ICONIC BY MISTAKE' for the very first time.
