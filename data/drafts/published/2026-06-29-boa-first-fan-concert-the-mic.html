BoA successfully held her first fan concert, 'BoA THE MIC,' on June 27 and 28 at the Grand Auditorium of Yonsei University's Sinchon Campus in Seoul. Her agency, Bayfal Entertainment, announced the news on June 29, describing the two-day event as a major success.

The concert marked the first time BoA met fans after leaving SM Entertainment — where she had spent 25 years — at the end of last year to establish her own one-person agency, Bayfal Entertainment.

During the performances, BoA sang her signature hit songs including 'Atlantis Princess' and 'Only One,' as well as her new track 'Ain't No Hard Feelings,' which was released last month. She also shared candid stories with the fans who have been with her for many years, making eye contact with the audience throughout.

Bayfal Entertainment said, 'We hope this fan concert becomes the first page of the countless memories that BoA will build with her fans on her new journey.'
