Actor Byeon Woo-seok is set to hold an Asia fan meeting tour. According to his agency, Baro Entertainment, on the 9th, Byeon Woo-seok will launch the 2026 ByeonWooSeok Asia Fanmeeting Tour and embark on a full-scale tour across the region.

This tour marks his second Asia fan meeting tour, coming two years after 'Summer Letter' in 2024. Starting in Seoul, the tour will visit major Asian cities including Bangkok, Yokohama, Taipei, Singapore, Manila, Jakarta, and Hong Kong, where he will meet global fans who have shown him unwavering love and support over the years.

The poster released alongside the fan meeting announcement features Byeon Woo-seok in a space filled with books. His refreshing visuals and mysterious atmosphere draw attention with just a single gaze toward the camera.

In particular, the message 'inviting fans to a secret library' beneath the fan meeting title is stirring excitement among fans. Anticipation is growing for the new charm Byeon Woo-seok will show up close in the special space of the 'secret library,' created exclusively for fans.

Detailed information about the 2026 ByeonWooSeok Asia Fanmeeting Tour, including cities and schedules, will be announced sequentially through social media at a later date. Meanwhile, Byeon Woo-seok has recently been meeting global viewers through the Netflix original variety show 'Yoo Jae-seok Camp,' his first fixed variety program since his debut.
