K-pop group Hearts2Hearts continues to build global momentum with their single 'RUDE!', racking up 3.6 billion cumulative views on TikTok challenge videos and over 2.8 million user-created videos using the track's audio.

The 'RUDE!' challenge has been spreading in a variety of ways — fans recreating the song's signature point choreography, which captures the group's playful and bold charm and bouncy energy, as well as using member Stella's English narration part, which has drawn an enthusiastic response.

'RUDE!' also topped the K-pop weekly chart on QQ Music, China's largest music platform, for a total of 7 consecutive weeks, and was named the best K-pop single of Q1 on the Trend Peak chart, reflecting the group's intense global popularity.

Even two months after its release, 'RUDE!' continues to hold top positions across major music charts, and the music video has surpassed 88 million views, showing no signs of cooling down.

Meanwhile, Hearts2Hearts won the 'Best Overseas Rookie Group of the Year' award at the WEIBO International Entertainment Awards held on April 25. The group is also set to appear at KCON JAPAN 2026, taking place on May 8 at Makuhari Messe in Chiba Prefecture, Japan.
