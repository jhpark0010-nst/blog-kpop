K-pop group CORTIS has been invited to perform at the Formula One Singapore Grand Prix 2026, held at the Marina Bay Circuit in Singapore from October 9 to 11 (local time). Big Hit Music announced the news on June 25.

Formula One is recognized as one of the world's premier motorsport events and large-scale entertainment spectacles. Among its races, the Formula One Singapore Grand Prix stands as the largest motorsport event in Asia.

CORTIS is scheduled to take the stage at the main venue, the Padang Stage, on October 9, the opening day of the event.

They are the only K-pop group to be included in this year's lineup. CORTIS will appear alongside American pop star Janet Jackson, popular Singapore-born singer Lin Junjie, and French electronic musician DJ Snake.

CORTIS is also set to perform at Lollapalooza Chicago, a major American music festival, running from July 31 to August 1.
