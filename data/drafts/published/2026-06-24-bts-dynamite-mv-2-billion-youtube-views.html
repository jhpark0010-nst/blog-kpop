BTS's signature track 'Dynamite' surpassed 2.1 billion views on YouTube at around 8 a.m. on June 24, according to Big Hit Music. The milestone marks a new all-time record for the group.

The 'Dynamite' music video had previously crossed 2 billion views in September of last year. It added another 100 million views in the nine months since, breaking BTS's own best record.

'Dynamite' was the first all-English song BTS ever released, and is known for its bright and upbeat charm.

With this song, BTS became the first Korean act to reach number one on the Billboard main singles chart, the Hot 100, and held the top spot for a total of three weeks.

The track also remained on the Hot 100 for 32 weeks in total, setting the record for the longest charting run on that chart by any K-pop artist.
