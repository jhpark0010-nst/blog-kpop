LE SSERAFIM's 'CRAZY' has once again proven its staying power on overseas charts. The title track from the group's fourth mini album entered the UK Official Physical Singles Chart (April 17–23) at No. 93, as announced on April 17 local time. This chart tallies weekly sales of CDs, vinyl, and other physical formats. With this entry, 'CRAZY' has now charted a total of 56 times, demonstrating its consistent popularity.

Released in August 2024, 'CRAZY' captures the feeling of encountering someone who drives you wild. LE SSERAFIM — composed of Kim Chaewon, Sakura, Huh Yunjin, Kazuha, and Hong Eunchae — showcased their reputation as the top girl group performers by taking on Voguing, a style that combines right-angle arm and leg movements with model-style walking. Notably, 'CRAZY' was the only K-pop group track at the time to appear on the Billboard Hot 100 for two consecutive weeks, setting a career high and proving the group's overwhelming presence in the global market.

LE SSERAFIM has recently announced the release of their second full-length album, 'PUREFLOW' pt.1. The album explores the changes and growth the five members experienced while learning to acknowledge their fears. As their first full album since 'UNFORGIVEN' in 2023 — roughly three years later — it is drawing significant attention from fans worldwide.

Comeback preparations are also underway. On April 17, the group released the trailer 'We walkin' here' for the second full album. Under the keyword 'Chosen Family,' the video portrays the five members' strong bond and has heightened anticipation for the new release. Featuring the members' acting, multilingual narration, and walking sequences, the video garnered a warm response and surpassed 600,000 YouTube views as of 9 a.m. on April 18.

Ahead of the full album's release on May 22, LE SSERAFIM will first drop the lead single 'CELEBRATION' on April 24 at 1 p.m. The track carries a message celebrating the act of acknowledging one's fears and building the inner strength to face them.
