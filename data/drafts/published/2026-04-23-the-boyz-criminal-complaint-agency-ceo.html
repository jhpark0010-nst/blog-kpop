THE BOYZ have filed a criminal complaint against the CEO of their agency, 100% Label, following their earlier demand to terminate their exclusive contracts. The group's legal representatives announced on April 21 that they had filed a complaint against 100% Label CEO Cha Ga-won for alleged violation of the Act on the Aggravated Punishment of Specific Economic Crimes (embezzlement). The lawsuit was filed by nine members, excluding member New.

THE BOYZ signed with 100% Label in December 2024. In February 2026, nine members filed for an injunction to suspend the validity of their exclusive contracts.

At the time, the members claimed that the agency had not paid settlement funds since July of the previous year and had refused to allow them to review documents needed to verify the transparency of the settlements.

In response, 100% Label stated that it could not accept THE BOYZ's demand to terminate their exclusive contracts.

Despite the ongoing dispute with their agency, THE BOYZ will proceed with their scheduled solo concert at KSPO Dome (formerly the Gymnastics Arena) in Olympic Park, Songpa-gu, Seoul, from April 24 to 26.
