BTS's fifth studio album 'ARIRANG' has entered the UK Official Chart's mid-year album recap, 'The Official Biggest Albums of 2026 So Far (Top 40)', landing at No. 20. The chart was announced on July 16 (local time).

The ranking is based on cumulative sales from January 2026 to the present, combining physical CD sales, digital downloads, and audio and video streaming figures.

According to the chart, BTS's 'ARIRANG' placed at No. 20, with Michael Jackson's 'THRILLER' following right behind at No. 21. Jackson's biopic 'Michael' is currently in theaters, boosting the classic album's numbers.

Among albums released this year, only six acts broke into the chart, including BTS alongside Drake, Harry Styles, and Olivia Rodrigo.

BTS is a seven-member boy group under BIGHIT MUSIC that debuted in 2013. The group consists of RM, Jin, SUGA, j-hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Beginning with Jin in 2022, the members fulfilled their mandatory military service one by one, completing the hiatus in June last year when SUGA was discharged. The group then released their fifth studio album 'ARIRANG' in March of this year.
