BTS is set to hold an offline fan event called 'BTS The City Arirang - Las Vegas' (referred to as 'The City') from May 20 to 31 (local time) in connection with the Las Vegas leg of their world tour 'Arirang.' Big Hit Music announced this on May 8.

'The City' is an event that transforms the city hosting a concert into a space where fans can experience the artist's music and message. BTS first introduced this event in Las Vegas, USA, in 2022.

On May 23, the opening night of the BTS concerts, the Las Vegas Strip — the city's famous tourist and commercial district — will be bathed in red lighting, the key color of their new album 'ARIRANG.' Fireworks will also be held at the MGM Grand Hotel on the same day.

The BTS concerts will take place over four days total — May 23-24 and May 27-28 — at Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas.
