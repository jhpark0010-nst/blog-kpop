BTS's 'I NEED U' Original Version music video has surpassed 200 million views on YouTube, their agency Big Hit Music announced on June 23. The video crossed the 200 million mark at approximately 2:56 a.m. KST that day.

'I NEED U' is the title track of BTS's third mini-album 'The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Pt. 1,' released in May 2015. The electro hip-hop track incorporates an urban style, blending a lyrical main theme with raw hip-hop energy.

With this song, BTS earned their first No. 1 on a terrestrial broadcast music program since their debut in 2013. The music video captures the delicate emotional performances of all seven members, centered on the theme of wandering youth.

The Original Version carries a rating restricted to viewers aged 19 and older, portraying raw youth and moments of aimlessness in a more realistic light. It runs approximately two minutes longer than the standard version. The original music video for 'I NEED U' has also recorded 250 million views.

BTS held the Busan leg of their world tour 'ARIRANG' at Busan Asiad Main Stadium on June 12 and 13, meeting domestic fans. They are continuing their European tour, beginning in Madrid, Spain.
