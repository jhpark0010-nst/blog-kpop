BLACKPINK's Jennie raised anticipation for her new single 'Less than a Lover' by releasing a series of sensual preview clips featuring Taiwanese model Kai, just one day before the single's official release on July 24.

On July 23, Jennie posted a series of short videos set to the new track 'Less than a Lover' on her official social media and TikTok. The clips, which previewed select scenes ahead of the full music video release, drew significant attention from fans.

In the videos, Jennie is styled in a black bikini with a white shirt draped over her shoulders as she glides across the blue sea on a classic boat, embodying a fresh summer mood. She gazes into the camera with a smile and a dreamy expression, creating her signature atmosphere.

Kai, the male lead of the music video, also appears alongside Jennie. He naturally wraps his arms around her from behind, and the two create a romantic mood that evokes the feeling of a real-life couple. In another clip, the pair spend time together on a beach at sunset — Jennie peels an orange and hands it to Kai, who leans in close to take it, completing a scene that feels straight out of a film.

Earlier, Jennie had shared a Polaroid photo featuring a man whose face was covered with a heart sticker, sparking curiosity among fans. It was later revealed that the person was Kai, a Taiwanese model active in South Korea, which generated even more buzz. A teaser video of the two running through a field was also released, further building excitement for the new track.

'Less than a Lover' is a song that Jennie participated in directly, from the lyrics and music production to the direction of the music video. It was first performed at major overseas music festivals including the Governors Ball Music Festival 2026 in the United States, Roskilde Festival in Denmark, Open'er Festival in Poland, and Mad Cool Festival 2026 in Spain, receiving a strong response. Following continued requests from fans for an official release, the single was confirmed for release. Jennie's new single 'Less than a Lover' will be available on major music platforms at 1 p.m. on July 24.
