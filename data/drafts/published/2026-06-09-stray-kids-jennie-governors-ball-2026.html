Stray Kids and BLACKPINK's Jennie took the stage as headliners at The Governors Ball Music Festival 2026 in New York from June 5 to 7 (local time), delivering electrifying performances at one of America's premier summer music festivals.

Stray Kids performed on day two of the festival, singing fan favorites including 'DOMINO,' 'Chk Chk Boom,' and 'CEREMONY' to a crowd of enthusiastic fans singing along and cheering throughout the set.

Jennie opened her set on day three with 'Filter,' then performed a total of 17 songs live over approximately 60 minutes, including 'Damn Right,' 'Mantra,' and 'Handlebars.' She also gave a surprise preview of an unreleased new track before closing the show with her hit 'like JENNIE.'

The Governors Ball is a large-scale outdoor festival held in New York every summer. Past headliners have included Eminem, Travis Scott, Kendrick Lamar, Lizzo, and Tyler the Creator.

HYBE and Geffen Records' Korean-American girl group KATSEYE performed on the first day of the festival, June 5, singing 14 songs including hits 'PINKY UP,' 'Gabriela,' and 'Gnarly.'

KATSEYE expressed their gratitude to the crowd, saying 'Your energy is truly incredible' and 'We can feel every bit of love you are sending us. New York, we love you.'
