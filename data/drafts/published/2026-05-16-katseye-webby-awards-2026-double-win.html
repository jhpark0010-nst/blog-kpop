KATSEYE, the Korea-U.S. joint girl group under HYBE, won two awards at the American digital awards ceremony the Webby Awards on May 16, 2026, according to their label HYBE-Geffen Records.

KATSEYE took home the Webby Award (main prize) for their appearance in fashion brand GAP's 'Better in Denim' campaign, which drew widespread attention last year.

The group also won the People's Voice Award in the Fashion, Beauty & Lifestyle, Branded Entertainment Video & Film category, which is determined by public vote.

The Webby Awards is an international awards ceremony held annually since 1996 by the International Academy of Digital Arts and Sciences in the United States. It recognizes websites, social media, apps, podcasts, advertisements, AI works, and individuals that have had significant global influence.

GAP's 'Better in Denim' campaign, released in August of last year, put diversity at its core and made a major impact, recording 8 billion impressions and 400 million views. On the strength of that campaign, KATSEYE ranked 2nd in Google's 'Year in Search 2025' for Trending Musicians in the United States, and reached No. 1 on TikTok's '2025 Global Artist of the Year.'

KATSEYE is currently gearing up for the release of their third mini-album WILD, set to drop in August.
