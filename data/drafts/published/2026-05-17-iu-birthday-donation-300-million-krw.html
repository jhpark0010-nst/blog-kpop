Singer and actress IU donated a total of 300 million KRW on the occasion of her birthday. Her agency, EDAM Entertainment, announced on May 16 that 'IU donated a total of 300 million KRW under the name IU-ANA (a combination of IU and her official fan club name, UAENA) to mark her birthday.'

According to EDAM Entertainment, the donation was distributed as follows: 100 million KRW to Seoul Asan Hospital, and 50 million KRW each to 'Hamkke Geotneun Aideul' (Children Walking Together), 'Milk Delivery Asking After the Elderly,' the Korea Single Mothers' Families Association, and 'Ddatteuthan Donghaeng' (Warm Companionship).

The donated funds are set to be used for practical support across various groups, including financial assistance for patients in economic hardship, education and cultural support for children and youth, care for elderly people living alone, support for single-mother households in vulnerable circumstances, and provision of advanced assistive devices for people with disabilities.

IU has consistently continued her charitable giving under the name IU-ANA on meaningful occasions such as her debut anniversary, birthday, and the year-end holiday season, spreading a positive influence. Most recently, she also donated 100 million KRW for children and youth on Children's Day. In addition, last March she donated in support of wildfire damage relief in the Gyeongsang region.
