LE SSERAFIM has revealed the title track of their second full-length album 'PUREFLOW pt.1' ahead of its release. At midnight on May 6, the group — comprising Kim Chaewon, Sakura, Huh Yunjin, Kazuha, and Hong Eunchae — posted the title track name and a full promotional timetable on the group's official SNS and global superfan platform Weverse. The title track is 'BOOMPALA'.

'BOOMPALA' is a word not found in any dictionary, newly coined by LE SSERAFIM for this comeback — just like the album name itself. The album title 'PUREFLOW' is an anagram of 'POWERFUL' (a word game in which the letters of a word are rearranged to form a new word or phrase). The meaning behind 'BOOMPALA' and the mood of the track remain a mystery, sparking curiosity among fans.

According to the promotional timetable released alongside the announcement, a variety of content will roll out leading up to the album's release date of May 22. First, on May 8, a video titled 'Just BOOMPALA' will drop, offering a glimpse of the track's atmosphere. On May 11, 'BOOMPALA Pavilion' — with 'pavilion' meaning an exhibition space — is set to be unveiled. From May 12 to 14, 'BIRCH SCAR', 'YUSU LILY', and 'PEONY ROOM' will be revealed in sequence. The specific meaning of each piece of content has not been disclosed, adding to the fun of speculation. On May 15, information related to the compact version of the album will be shared.

Starting May 18, a preview containing hints about the title track will kick off a period of heightened attention on the music. On May 19, a highlight medley giving a feel for all the tracks on the album will follow. Finally, music video teasers for the title track will be released on two separate occasions, on May 20 and 21.

Prior to the official release of the second full album, LE SSERAFIM dropped their lead single 'CELEBRATION' on April 24. The track, which lists Kim Chaewon and Huh Yunjin in the credits, features an upbeat and addictive beat that has been winning over listeners worldwide. Notably, on the latest Billboard charts announced on May 5 (dated May 9), the song entered at No. 170 on the Global (Excluding U.S.) chart and No. 2 on the World Digital Song Sales chart.

LE SSERAFIM's second full album 'PUREFLOW pt.1' arrives on May 22 at 1 p.m. The album is described as a new chapter for LE SSERAFIM, capturing the changes and growth the members have experienced as they came to understand fear — having once boldly declared 'FEARLESS' at the start of their debut.
