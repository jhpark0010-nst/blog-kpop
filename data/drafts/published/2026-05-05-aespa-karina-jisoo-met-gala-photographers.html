A controversial incident unfolded at the 2026 Met Gala when photographers on the red carpet repeatedly called out 'Jisoo' at aespa member Karina, mistaking her for BLACKPINK's Jisoo. Karina attended the Costume Institute Benefit at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York on May 4 (local time).

For the event, Karina wore a white satin custom dress featuring a tablier neckline intricately embroidered with jet beads and silver crystal. She paired it with a clutch in the same fabric and silver leather sandals, completing the look with jewelry adorned with white gold, peridot, amethyst, and diamonds, maximizing her signature elegant and mysterious charm.

The incident occurred while Karina was posing on the red carpet. Some photographers at the scene repeatedly called out 'Jisoo' at her in loud voices, causing widespread bewilderment.

After footage of the moment was made public, netizens criticized the photographers for their lack of professionalism and rudeness. The prevailing view was that failing to verify the name of a celebrity who had been officially invited to one of the world's most prestigious fashion events before shooting was a clear dereliction of duty and thoroughly unprofessional conduct.

Despite the rude situation, Karina showed no sign of flustered reaction and continued posing with a composed expression.

Fans praised Karina for her unshaken composure, saying she 'showed the qualities of a true professional' as she skillfully posed through every moment without wavering.
