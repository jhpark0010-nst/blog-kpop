YG Entertainment officially announced on July 22 that it will hold the '2026 YG Global Audition' over approximately six months, from July to December 2026.

The global audition kicked off successfully in Beijing, China on July 19. It will then visit major Chinese cities including Chengdu (July 25), Shanghai (August 1), and Shenzhen (August 9), followed by Tokyo, Japan (August 9) and Osaka (September 20).

From October, the audition expands to the United States, Taiwan, and Hong Kong. In November, it moves to Thailand and Canada, and in December it reaches Australia and New Zealand, with the agency heading directly to aspiring talent around the world.

YG Entertainment, which has produced artists including BIGBANG, 2NE1, BLACKPINK, WINNER, iKON, TREASURE, and BABYMONSTER, is drawing attention from hopefuls worldwide as fans and aspiring artists alike wonder what new talent the label will discover through its distinctive producing expertise.
