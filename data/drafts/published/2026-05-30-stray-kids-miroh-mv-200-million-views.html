Stray Kids' music video for 'MIROH' has surpassed 200 million views on YouTube, their agency JYP Entertainment announced on May 30. The milestone was reached on the afternoon of May 29.

'MIROH' is the title track from the mini-album 'Cle 1 : MIROH', released in March 2019. The music video crossed the 200 million view mark the previous afternoon.

Also on the morning of May 30, the music video for 'Easy', a track from Stray Kids' first full-length album, surpassed 100 million YouTube views. This marks the 21st Stray Kids music video to reach 100 million views or more.

In addition, Stray Kids has surpassed a cumulative shipment of 40 million copies across 28 albums released in South Korea and Japan.

The group also held their sixth fan meeting at Inspire Arena in Incheon during March and April of this year.
