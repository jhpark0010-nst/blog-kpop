IVE has surprise-dropped the title track 'LUCID DREAM' ahead of their fourth Japanese album's official release on May 27. Their agency Starship Entertainment released the track across major online music platforms at midnight on May 20.

The released track 'LUCID DREAM' captures a distinct atmosphere compared to IVE's previous Japanese album title tracks. A hopeful and upbeat melody is layered with the members' delicate vocals, while dreamy falsettos add a mysterious, ethereal quality.

'LUCID DREAM' is a song inspired by the concept of 'dreams,' portraying dreams not merely as an escape from reality but as a space to face oneself and be honest about one's emotions. It features IVE's signature hopeful attitude and swelling melodies, maintaining the group's established color while showcasing a new charm not seen before.

Following the surprise early release, IVE is set to drop the 'LUCID DREAM' music video at midnight on May 26, keeping anticipation high. With the new-toned track now out, attention is focused on what visuals and story the MV will bring.

The fourth Japanese album 'LUCID DREAM,' releasing May 27, will also include Japanese-language versions of 'REBEL HEART' and 'ATTITUDE' — the pre-release double title tracks from their third mini album 'IVE EMPATHY,' which charted on Melon's annual chart last year — as well as the album track 'Thank U.'

IVE previously topped Billboard Japan's Top Album Sales chart with their third Japanese album 'Be Alright,' and surpassed 200 million cumulative streams on Billboard Japan with 'After LIKE.' They have also performed at Rock in Japan Festival 2025, one of Japan's four major rock festivals, and appeared on NHK's popular music program 'Venue 101' and TBS variety show 'Snow Man ni Yara Sete Kudasai SP,' steadily building their presence in Japan. Most recently, they performed at Kyocera Dome Osaka as part of their second world tour 'SHOW WHAT I AM,' with a return to Tokyo Dome scheduled for June.
