Lexi, a member of Saint Satin — the new global girl group from HYBE and Geffen Records — is departing from the group before their official debut. The announcement was made on July 14, 2026, via Weverse, the fan communication platform used by HYBE artists.

HYBE and Geffen Records stated on Weverse: 'After in-depth discussions about Lexi and the group's future activities, both parties have agreed to part ways by mutual agreement.'

They added: 'Lexi will no longer remain a member of Saint Satin or a part of HYBE and Geffen Records, but we sincerely wish her all the best going forward.'

Despite the membership change, Saint Satin will continue preparations for their debut as planned.

Saint Satin is the second global girl group to be launched by HYBE and Geffen Records, following KATSEYE. The members who had been announced for debut were Lexi (Sweden), Emily (USA), Samara (Brazil), and Sakura (Japan).
