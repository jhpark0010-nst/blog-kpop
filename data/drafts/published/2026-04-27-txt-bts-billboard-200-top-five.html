TOMORROW X TOGETHER (TXT) and BTS have landed side by side on the Billboard 200, the main U.S. album chart, charting at No. 3 and No. 4 respectively this week.

According to a chart preview article published by Billboard on April 26 (local time), TXT's eighth mini album '7TH YEAR: 가시덤불에 잠시 바람이 멈췄을 때' (7TH YEAR: When the Wind Briefly Stopped in the Thorns) debuted at No. 3 on this week's chart.

BTS's fifth full-length album 'ARIRANG' dropped one spot from the previous week to land at No. 4, but has now entered the top 5 for five consecutive weeks since its release.

Both groups are signed to Big Hit Music, making it a label sweep as labelmates shone together on the Billboard 200.

In addition, the original soundtrack for the Netflix animated film 'K-Pop Demon Hunters' climbed from No. 16 the previous week to No. 7, re-entering the top 10.
