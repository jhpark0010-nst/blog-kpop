Taeyang is set to hold a special album exhibition to celebrate his comeback. THE BLACK LABEL announced that an album exhibition will be held for fans to mark the release of Taeyang's fourth full-length album, 'QUINTESSENCE.'

The exhibition is designed as a space that visually expands the message of 'QUINTESSENCE,' his first full-length album in approximately nine years. It will be organized so that fans can experience Taeyang's musical world in a more three-dimensional way through various content combining music and visuals. The exhibition is expected to serve as a special gift for K-pop fans who have long awaited his comeback.

The exhibition will run from May 18 to May 31 at WORKING WITH FRIEND in Yongsan-gu, Seoul. Operating hours are 11 a.m. to 8 p.m. daily, with entry divided into a total of nine sessions per day.

Prior to the release, Taeyang has been building anticipation for his comeback by sequentially unveiling a trailer, main photos, a poster, a visual film, and teaser photos for 'QUINTESSENCE.' Expectations are high for what kind of music and sensibility will fill this new album, which aims to capture Taeyang's own essence and quintessence.

Meanwhile, Taeyang's fourth full-length album 'QUINTESSENCE' will be released on May 18 at 6 p.m.
