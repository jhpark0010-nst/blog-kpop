KARA — consisting of members Park Gyuri, Han Seungyeon, Nicole, Kang Jiyoung, and Heo Youngji — has cancelled all four of their scheduled Japan fan meeting sessions. According to MyDaily reporting on June 1, the group has indefinitely postponed all four fan meetings that were planned for July 4 and 5 in Japan.

According to a representative, the postponement was due to local circumstances on the ground. Full refunds will be processed for all tickets already purchased. Some observers have interpreted the cancellation as stemming from poor ticket sales, noting that the group had planned to hold a fan meeting — rather than a concert — at an arena-scale venue with a capacity of approximately 10,000 people.

KARA had been scheduled to meet Japanese fans on July 4 and 5 at TOYOTA ARENA TOKYO for the fan meeting event titled '2026 KARA JAPAN FANMEETING : Hello, KAMILIA!' The event was cancelled just one month before the planned dates.

KARA reunited in 2022 for a 15th debut anniversary album, and has since been actively holding tours in Japan in 2024 and last year.
