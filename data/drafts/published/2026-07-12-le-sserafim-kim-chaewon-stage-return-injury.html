LE SSERAFIM member and leader Kim Chaewon shared her feelings about returning to the stage. On July 12, LE SSERAFIM held the third day of their '2026 LE SSERAFIM TOUR PUREFLOW IN INCHEON' concert at Inspire Arena in Yeongjong-gu, Incheon. The two-day run held on July 11 and 12 was also streamed online via live broadcast.

This concert marked Kim Chaewon's first performance since sustaining a throat injury. Due to the injury, LE SSERAFIM had performed as a five-member group — without Kim Chaewon — during their music show (TV music program) promotions for their second full-length album.

LE SSERAFIM performed 'Boom Pala' together with Kim Chaewon, delivering a festival-like show. Member Hong Eunchae then remarked, 'The reaction to Ssam Pala, which we performed yesterday, was amazing,' adding, 'Fearnot (the fandom name) must have waited a really long time for this.'

Kim Chaewon also shared her feelings, saying, 'I am so happy that I can finally show you this.'

Kim Chaewon then performed the much-talked-about intro of 'Boom Pala' in front of the fans. She asked the crowd, 'It was a big challenge for me — did you like it?' Members Hong Eunchae and Kazuha also joined in to perform the 'Boom Pala' intro together.

Following the PUREFLOW IN INCHEON concerts, LE SSERAFIM is set to meet global fans across a total of 23 cities and 32 shows spanning North America, Europe, and Asia.
