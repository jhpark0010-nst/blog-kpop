BIGBANG will fire the opening shot of their 20th debut anniversary world tour in Goyang, South Korea, set to paint the world purple. On June 23, YG Entertainment revealed a poster via official social media announcing the fan club pre-sale for BIGBANG's 20th anniversary world tour Goyang concert.

The pre-sale is open exclusively to Weverse (Bestaige) BIGBANG V.I.P MEMBERSHIP subscribers who have completed membership verification in advance. Domestic tickets will be available through the Coupang Play mobile app starting at 7 PM on June 24, while global tickets can be purchased via NOL World starting at 8 PM. General ticketing for non-members opens on June 25 at the same times.

BIGBANG will hold 'BIGBANG 2026 WORLD TOUR IN GOYANG with Coupang Play' at Goyang Stadium for three days, from August 21 to 23. As the starting point of the long-awaited 20th anniversary world tour and the stage marking the return of a K-pop legend group, global fans are showing intense interest.

This concert carries added significance as it opens a world tour under the BIGBANG name for the first time in approximately nine years. Fans can expect a parade of beloved hit songs as well as a stage that distills the group's unparalleled musical depth built over 20 years. Attention is also focused on what kind of performances BIGBANG will deliver on this tour, following their recent appearance at the 2026 Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival in the United States.

Starting from the Goyang concerts, BIGBANG will carry out their world tour across a total of 18 cities and 31 shows, covering North America, Europe, Oceania, and Asia. They are scheduled to meet fans around the world at massive stadiums and dome-class venues including MetLife Stadium, Stade de France, Tottenham Hotspur Stadium, Kaohsiung National Stadium, and Tokyo Dome.
