BTS has added one additional show each to the Jakarta, Indonesia and Bulacan, Philippines legs of their world tour ARIRANG, their agency Big Hit Music announced on June 17. With these additions, the ARIRANG tour has expanded to a total of 88 shows.

BTS will now perform at Gelora Bung Karno Stadium in Jakarta on December 29 (local time), in addition to the previously scheduled December 26 and 27 dates. At the Philippine Sports Stadium in Bulacan, the group will also take the stage on March 16 of next year, on top of the already-announced March 13 and 14 dates.

Big Hit Music stated, 'BTS performances are having additional dates added in multiple cities amid tremendous popularity,' and explained, 'Shows in Tampa, Stanford, and Las Vegas in the United States, as well as Lima in Peru, Santiago in Chile, Buenos Aires in Argentina, and Melbourne in Australia have each had one date added previously.'

BTS will kick off their European tour in Madrid, Spain on June 26 and 27. The European leg spans a total of 10 shows across Madrid, Brussels in Belgium, London in the United Kingdom, Munich in Germany, and Paris in France — all of which are already sold out.

The BTS world tour ARIRANG is unfolding on the largest scale in K-pop history. Japan and Middle East dates are still set to be added, meaning the tour is expected to grow even further in size.

At their concert held at Busan Asiad Main Stadium on June 13 — their 13th debut anniversary — BTS told fans, 'Let's keep going together from here. We will repay you with even better stages and music.'
