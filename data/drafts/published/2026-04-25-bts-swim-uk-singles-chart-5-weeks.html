BTS has entered the UK Official Singles and Albums Charts Top 100 for five consecutive weeks with their fifth studio album ARIRANG. According to the UK Official Chart dated April 24 (local time), the album's title track 'SWIM' ranked at No. 34 on the singles chart, a drop of nine places from the previous week.

KATSEYE's 'PINKY UP' charted at No. 24, marking its second consecutive week on the chart after reaching a personal best of No. 14 the week prior. Another KATSEYE track, 'TOUCH', also entered the chart at No. 88.

'GOLDEN', the original soundtrack from the Netflix animated film K-Pop Demon Hunters, came in at No. 36. The track has now remained on the chart for 44 consecutive weeks.

On the UK Official Albums Chart, BTS's fifth studio album ARIRANG placed at No. 13.
