BTS held three nights of their world tour 'ARIRANG' at Stanford Stadium in the United States on May 16–17 and 19 (local time), drawing approximately 152,000 fans in total. Their agency Big Hit Music announced the figures on May 22.

BTS became only the second musical act to perform at the stadium since its opening in 1921, following Coldplay.

A surprise fan event took place on the final night. When the melody of the Korean folk song 'Arirang' played during the performance of 'Body to Body' — a track from BTS's fifth studio album ARIRANG — fans waved South Korean flags they had prepared in advance and sang along together in a mass sing-along, according to the agency.

Toward the end of the concert, the members said, 'We are living the best moments of our lives right now. We were truly moved by the event you showed us.' They added, 'We will remember every single moment. We would like to express our gratitude as we look forward to meeting you again.'

BTS is currently carrying out a world tour spanning 34 cities across the globe, with a total of 85 performances scheduled.
