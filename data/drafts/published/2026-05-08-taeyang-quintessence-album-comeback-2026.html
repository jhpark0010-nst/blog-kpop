BIGBANG member and solo artist Taeyang is making his comeback with a diverse range of concepts showcasing a bold visual transformation. His fourth full-length album, 'QUINTESSENCE', is set for release on May 18, 2026 at 6 PM KST — marking his first studio album in approximately nine years.

The Black Label released the second set of teasing photos for 'QUINTESSENCE' via official social media on May 7. The images capture Taeyang's hip mood and photogenic poses, drawing attention with a mix of monochrome fashion that conveys restrained charisma, along with vibrant hair colors and bold styling that maximize his individuality.

Through this comeback, Taeyang is set to captivate global K-pop fans both with his one-of-a-kind musical world and his varied visual reinvention.

Prior to this, Taeyang had been steadily building anticipation for 'QUINTESSENCE' by releasing a trailer, main photos, posters, and a visual film in sequence. Fans are eager to see what colors and essence Taeyang will bring to this new record.

'QUINTESSENCE' is Taeyang's first full studio album since 'WHITE NIGHT', released in August 2017 — a gap of roughly nine years. The album is expected to fully demonstrate the depth and range of Taeyang as an all-around solo artist.
