BABYMONSTER has claimed the top spot on the global YouTube daily music video chart for two consecutive days with the music video for their new song 'CHOOM.' According to the latest chart data (May 5, 2026) released by YouTube on May 7, 'CHOOM,' the title track of BABYMONSTER's third mini album, debuted straight at No. 1 and has held that position for a second day running.

The 'CHOOM' music video shot to No. 1 on YouTube Worldwide Trending immediately upon release and was named the most-viewed video within 24 hours, signaling strong early momentum. It surpassed 15 million views in just half a day, and has since continued to climb, now recording over 36 million views.

The track has earned broad popularity across the globe, charting at No. 7 in the UK and No. 9 in the US — both considered the two major music markets — as well as No. 1 in Australia, No. 4 in Canada, and No. 4 in New Zealand. Additionally, it claimed No. 1 on QQ Music's MV chart, China's largest music streaming platform, on the day of release and has maintained that position ever since.

The album itself is also posting remarkable results. The third mini album 'CHOOM' topped the iTunes Album Chart in 19 regions and reached No. 1 on the iTunes Worldwide Album Chart. According to the Hanteo Chart, first-day physical sales reached 387,871 copies — a new personal best for the group and approximately 1.5 times the first-day sales of their previous release 'WE GO UP.'

The 'CHOOM' performance video, released on May 6, is also drawing significant attention. Featuring dynamic choreography performed alongside 30 dancers in a spectacular group routine, the video surpassed 10 million views in just two and a half days, continuing to ride the wave of popularity alongside the main music video.
