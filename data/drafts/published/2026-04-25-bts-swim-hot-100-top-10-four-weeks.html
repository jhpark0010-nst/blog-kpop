BTS has achieved a fourth consecutive week in the Top 10 of the Billboard Hot 100, the United States' main song chart. According to a chart preview published on Billboard's official website on April 21, 'SWIM' — the title track of BTS's fifth full-length album ARIRANG — landed at No. 10 on the Hot 100 dated April 25. Since debuting at No. 1 on the April 4 chart, the song has held its ground in the upper ranks for a full month amid a wave of new releases from major pop acts, cementing its status as a global hit. On the Digital Song Sales chart, 'SWIM' has remained at No. 1 for four consecutive weeks.

The Hot 100 reflects a balanced combination of U.S. streaming figures, radio airplay scores, and sales data, making it the most intuitive indicator of mainstream popularity. A sustained run on the Hot 100 signals that BTS's music has reached a significantly expanded listener base. The result is seen as the new album's universal emotional message connecting with a broad global audience.

Album performance has also been remarkable. According to a chart preview released by Billboard on April 20, ARIRANG debuted at No. 3 on the Billboard 200 main album chart and has remained in the Top 3 for four consecutive weeks. This surpasses the group's previous best fourth-week record on the Billboard 200, which was set by the mini album 'MAP OF THE SOUL : PERSONA' at No. 8, marking a new career high. A month of sustained performance across both of Billboard's main charts reflects BTS's standing as a global musician.

The group's proven mainstream popularity is also translating into sold-out concert dates. BTS WORLD TOUR 'ARIRANG' has sold out 46 shows across Korea, Japan, North America, and Europe. The tour launched on April 9 at Goyang Stadium's main arena, and has already drawn more than 240,000 attendees across Goyang and Tokyo. The full tour spans 85 shows across 34 cities worldwide, and anticipation is high for the milestones it will set in the live music industry.

Meanwhile, BTS is expanding the 'BTS THE CITY ARIRANG' project globally, transforming entire cities into festival venues on the back of the world tour's momentum. Having launched in Seoul, 'BTS THE CITY ARIRANG' will move to Las Vegas, USA in May, followed by Busan, Korea in June.
