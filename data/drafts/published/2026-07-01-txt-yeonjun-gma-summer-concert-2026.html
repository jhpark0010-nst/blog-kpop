TXT member Yeonjun is set to appear on Good Morning America's (GMA) Summer Concert Series on August 7, 2026 (local time), according to an announcement made by his label Big Hit Music on July 1. GMA is ABC's flagship morning news program in the United States.

The Summer Concert Series is an annual summer performance event hosted by GMA each year. Yeonjun will be only the second Korean solo artist to take the stage at this event, following BTS's Jungkook, who appeared in 2023.

Big Hit Music stated, 'Yeonjun is being recognized for his global influence as a solo artist, beyond his activities as part of the group.'

Yeonjun is scheduled to release his second solo mini-album, 'NO LABELS: PART 02,' on the 10th of this month at 1 p.m. It will be his first solo album release in eight months, following 'NO LABELS: PART 01,' which came out in November of last year.
