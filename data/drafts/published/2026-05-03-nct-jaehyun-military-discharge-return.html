NCT's Jaehyun (age 29) has returned to fans after completing his mandatory military service. On May 3, he was officially discharged from the Army Military Band in good health, finishing his full term of duty.

Jaehyun entered the Army Training Center on November 4, 2024, and faithfully served for 18 months as a military band soldier. With his discharge, he becomes the second NCT member to complete his military service obligation, following Taeyong, who was discharged in December of last year.

Jaehyun debuted with NCT in 2016 and has been active across multiple units, including NCT 127, NCT U, and NCT DoJaeJung. Before enlisting, he released his first solo album 'J' and also successfully transitioned into acting through the film 'You Will Die in 6 Hours' and the drama 'Dear M'.
