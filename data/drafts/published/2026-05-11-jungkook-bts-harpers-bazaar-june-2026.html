BTS member Jungkook has showcased a bold, rugged masculinity on the June 2026 cover of fashion magazine Harper's Bazaar Korea, revealed on May 11. The issue features six different cover images of Jungkook, each highlighting his striking visuals.

Across the six cover images, a daring Jungkook commands attention with a distinct charm and unique atmosphere. The concept — described as an 'extraordinary ordinary day,' like a day in the life of an eccentric star spending time at home in anything but a typical way — brings together his confident gaze, relaxed poses, and a modern, refined style.

In the accompanying interview, Jungkook shared his thoughts on the shoot: 'The atmosphere, timing, and pace of the shoot were all to my liking. I usually find photo shoots a bit difficult — I just never quite get used to them. Still, shooting with Bazaar has become a fond memory for me.'

On the world tour resuming after approximately four years, kicked off by the Goyang concert 'BTS WORLD TOUR ARIRANG IN GOYANG,' Jungkook said: 'It had been a really long time since I last performed at a concert, and because there was a process of putting our hearts into the preparation, I didn't have any particular nerves or worries — but it did feel like I had briefly stepped into another world and come back.'

He continued: 'Right now I'm excitedly staying up through the nights thinking about traveling around on tour and meeting audiences all over the world. When I'm on stage dancing and singing while hearing the cheers of the audience, I definitely feel most like myself. When my throat hurts and I'm drenched in sweat and everything is in full chaos — that's actually when I feel most alive.'
