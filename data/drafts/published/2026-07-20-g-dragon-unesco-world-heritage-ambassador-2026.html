G-Dragon attended the opening ceremony of the 48th UNESCO World Heritage Committee at BEXCO in Busan on July 19, 2026, where he received an official appointment as promotional ambassador from the Cultural Heritage Administration of Korea. His management company Galaxy Corporation announced the appointment on July 20.

At the opening ceremony, G-Dragon delivered a speech centered on the preservation of world heritage and peace, emphasizing the value of cultural heritage and the importance of conservation for future generations.

Speaking about the host city of Busan, he said, 'This is a remarkable city that transformed the scars of war into peace and hope, and it is home to the world's only United Nations Memorial Cemetery. Here, where those who devoted themselves to peace across borders now rest, I want to begin a new commitment to protecting world heritage together.'

He continued, 'Protecting heritage is not simply about preserving the past — it is about creating peace for the future. Art has the power to move everyone's hearts, and when those movements come together, they become a powerful force that changes the world. As an artist, I will contribute to protecting world heritage sites that are at risk of disappearing due to war and the climate crisis.'

G-Dragon is set to meet fans on August 21 to 23 at Goyang Stadium in Goyang, Gyeonggi Province, for BIGBANG's new world tour 'XX : COSMOS,' marking the group's 20th debut anniversary. It will be BIGBANG's first concert in nine years since 2017.
