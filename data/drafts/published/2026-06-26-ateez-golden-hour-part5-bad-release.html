ATEEZ has returned to the summer music scene with an even hotter golden energy. On June 26 at 1 PM KST, the group released their 14th mini album 'GOLDEN HOUR : Part.5' across major music streaming platforms.

'GOLDEN HOUR : Part.5' continues the 'GOLDEN HOUR' series, which captures ATEEZ's most brilliantly shining moments. As with previous installments, members Hongjoong and Mingi participated in writing the lyrics for all tracks.

The title track 'BAD' is a Brazilian funk track that blends an addictive chorus with a groove-filled beat. Hollywood actor Chase Infiniti also appears in the music video.

The album additionally includes 'MAMACITA', a Latin trap-flavored track; 'TOXIN', which carries an R&B mood; 'Fallin'', an EDM-based song; and 'Body', a sensuous R&B track.

ATEEZ is an 8-member boy group under KQ Entertainment that debuted in 2018. Their 13th mini album 'GOLDEN HOUR : Part.4', released in February, reached No. 3 on the Billboard 200, the main US Billboard chart.

With that achievement, ATEEZ placed six consecutive albums in the top 3 of the Billboard 200, starting from their 9th mini album 'THE WORLD EP.2 : OUTLAW' through their 13th mini album.
