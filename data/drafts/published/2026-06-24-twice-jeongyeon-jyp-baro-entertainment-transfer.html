TWICE member Jeongyeon has been swept up in transfer rumors, with reports emerging on June 24 that she has held a recent meeting with Baro Entertainment, the agency home to actor Byun Woo-seok.

A representative from Baro Entertainment confirmed to MyDaily: 'It is true that we recently held a meeting with TWICE's Jeongyeon.' However, the representative added, 'There is nothing confirmed regarding a contract.'

In response, a representative from her current agency JYP Entertainment stated: 'TWICE is currently in a contract renewal discussion period, and the company and its members are continuing careful discussions while respecting each other's wishes. We will inform you as soon as matters are confirmed.'

Jeongyeon debuted with TWICE in 2015 and has received wide love through hit songs including 'Cheer Up,' 'TT,' and 'What is Love?' In July 2022, all TWICE members renewed their contracts approximately three months before the previous contract's expiration.

Meanwhile, Jeongyeon recently confirmed her appearance in the film 'Sinbyeong: The Movie,' signaling a new chapter as an actress. Baro Entertainment's roster includes actress Gong Seung-yeon — Jeongyeon's older sister — as well as Byun Woo-seok, Lee Chae-min, and Lee Hong-nae. Attention is now focused on whether Jeongyeon, who has been with JYP Entertainment for 11 years, will join the same agency as her sister.
