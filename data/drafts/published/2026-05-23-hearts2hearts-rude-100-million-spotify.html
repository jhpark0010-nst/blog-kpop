Girl group Hearts2Hearts' song 'RUDE!' has surpassed 100 million streams on Spotify, the world's largest music streaming platform, SM Entertainment announced on May 23. The milestone was achieved just three months after the track's release.

This also marks the first song released by a K-pop girl group in 2026 to reach 100 million streams on Spotify.

With this achievement, Hearts2Hearts now holds a total of three songs with over 100 million Spotify streams — 'The Chase,' 'STYLE,' and 'RUDE!' — all within just one year and three months since their debut.

'RUDE!' was released in February and is a house-based dance track capturing the cute rebelliousness and bold charm of a free-spirited girl who refuses to be bound by set rules. The song is characterized by a rhythmic groove and a bouncy synthesizer sound.

Hearts2Hearts is set to perform at Hanyang University's festival on May 29, and will also take the stage at the 'M Countdown X Mega Concert' held at Inspire Arena in Incheon Yeongjongdo on May 30.
