NewJeans surprised fans on their 4th debut anniversary, July 22, 2026, by releasing a special film without prior notice through their official YouTube channel at midnight. The video features all four current members — Minji, Hyein, Hanni, and Haerin — appearing together, marking the first time the group has produced team-based content in one year and four months.

The last time NewJeans appeared together as a group was at their independently organized Hong Kong ComplexCon performance in March of last year, where they announced a halt to all activities, stating they would 'comply with the court's decision and stop all activities.'

The conflict between NewJeans and their agency ADOR began following the dismissal of former ADOR CEO Min Hee-jin. In November 2024, the members held a press conference to declare the termination of their exclusive contracts. However, courts ruled in favor of ADOR in both the injunction case and the main lawsuit, blocking the members from pursuing independent activities.

Subsequently, members Haerin and Hyein returned to ADOR in November of last year, with Hanni officially rejoining the agency a month later in December. Regarding Minji, ADOR had previously stated that negotiations were proceeding in a positive direction. As for Danielle, ADOR held her responsible for the group's departure and notified her of contract termination in December of last year.

Industry observers are interpreting NewJeans' release of content featuring all current members through ADOR — for the first time in one year and four months — as a green light for a potential comeback. There is also speculation that Minji's status, for which no official announcement has yet been made, may be moving toward her returning to the agency as a NewJeans member.

ADOR commented on the video, saying, 'This video is commemorative content created to celebrate NewJeans' 4th debut anniversary and to enjoy with fans.' They added, 'Specific activity plans and methods for NewJeans will be announced after discussions are fully concluded.' NewJeans debuted on July 22, 2022, with the music video for 'Attention,' and went on to score hits including 'Hype Boy,' 'Ditto,' 'OMG,' and 'Super Shy.' In August 2023, their second mini-album 'Get Up' reached number one on the Billboard 200, making them only the second K-pop girl group to achieve that feat. Their last release was the Japanese single 'Supernatural' in June 2024.
