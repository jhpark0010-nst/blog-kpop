Girls' Generation member, solo artist, and actress Tiffany Young will release a full-length studio album on August 20. This marks her first solo full album since her debut.

On July 13, Tiffany Young revealed the schedule poster for her first solo full album, 'Edge of Calm,' through her official social media channels.

The unveiled poster features the typographic logo of 'Edge of Calm' alongside Tiffany Young's distinctively restrained styling and a gaze that radiates a deep aura. The overall mood of the album — hovering between tense anticipation and calm — is visualized through sophisticated visual direction, heightening curiosity about her first-ever solo full-length release.

According to the release plan, a variety of teaser content will roll out sequentially: pre-orders open on July 30, followed by an audio snippet on August 5, visual photos on August 7 and 11, and a music video teaser on August 17. The album and music video will then be unveiled at 6 p.m. on August 20, marking Tiffany Young's full comeback.

'Edge of Calm' captures the subtle tension, focus, and emotional balance that exist in the moments just before stillness. 'Edge' represents tension and immersion, while 'Calm' conveys emotional balance and tranquility — both woven throughout the album's sound, visuals, and overall sensibility. Notably, Tiffany Young participated directly in the entire production process, including overall concept planning, styling, visual direction, and music video story development, infusing the project with her own distinct musical color.

Meanwhile, Tiffany Young filed her marriage registration with actor Byun Yo-han in February, making them legally married.
