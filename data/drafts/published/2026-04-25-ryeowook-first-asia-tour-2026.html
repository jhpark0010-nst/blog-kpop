Super Junior's Ryeowook is embarking on his first-ever Asia tour to celebrate the 10th anniversary of his solo debut. Marking the milestone, Ryeowook will hold '2026 RYEOWOOK'S AGIT CONCERT : DIVE TO BLUE' and visit four regions across Asia — Seoul and Bangkok in July, followed by Macau, and Taipei in August.

The tour kicks off in Seoul, where Ryeowook will perform on July 11 and 12 at the Ticketlink 1975 Theater in Gwangjin-gu. Detailed information including ticket reservation schedules will be announced later through the official Super Junior fan club community and SNS accounts.

This concert marks the fourth installment of Ryeowook's concert brand 'AGIT CONCERT,' and comes approximately seven months after 'RYEOWOOK'S AGIT CONCERT : Special Rucky draW,' which was held in December 2025.

The concert's theme, in keeping with its subtitle 'DIVE TO BLUE,' has been planned around the concept of 'the ocean in an intimate theater.' Through music spanning a variety of moods, audiences are expected to experience a special 'refuge' between reality and dreams, away from everyday life.

Meanwhile, Ryeowook has established himself not only as a member of K-pop legend group Super Junior, but also as a trusted vocalist with hit songs from the moment of his solo debut in January 2016 with his first mini-album 'The Little Prince' (어린왕자). He has long been beloved for his outstanding vocal ability and distinctive emotional sensibility.
