Nine members of THE BOYZ — Sangyeon, Jacob, Younghoon, Hyunjae, Juyeon, Kevin, Q, Sunwoo, and Eric — have won their injunction case against their agency ONE HUNDRED. The court granted the members' application to suspend the exclusive contract on April 23, 2026.

Attorney Kim Moon-hee of law firm Yulchon, representing the nine members, released an official statement: 'The court today issued a decision granting the artists' application to suspend the exclusive contract.' The statement continued: 'Accordingly, the artists have received confirmation through the court's ruling that their exclusive contract with ONE HUNDRED Label has been lawfully terminated and is no longer in effect.'

The court cited the agency's breach of core obligations as the basis for its decision. Yulchon stated: 'The court recognized that the agency violated its obligation to pay settlement funds, failed to fulfill its obligation to provide settlement documents necessary to verify the appropriateness of the settlement, and further failed to meet core contractual obligations including management support and artist protection duties — concluding that, due to the agency's fault, the trust between the parties had broken down to an irrecoverable degree.'

Yulchon further stated: 'The agency had argued in the media and during the injunction proceedings that the signing fee was in the nature of an advance payment, but the court explicitly rejected this claim.' The statement added: 'The court made clear that the payment of the signing fee and the distribution of settlement funds arising from revenue are governed by separate clauses in the exclusive contract, and that no agreement exists allowing the already-paid signing fee to substitute for settlement funds newly owed to the artists. The court also clarified that the signing fee is distinct from settlement funds, as it was separately paid to the artists as compensation for their entertainment activities in order to sign artists who had already secured considerable popularity and recognition at the time the exclusive contract was concluded.'

The statement added: 'For schedules already confirmed prior to the termination of the exclusive contract, the artists plan to fulfill them responsibly and faithfully, in order to honor their promises to fans and minimize harm to innocent third parties.'

Meanwhile, nine members of THE BOYZ — excluding New — had previously notified ONE HUNDRED of the termination of their exclusive contract, then filed the injunction lawsuit. The members claimed the agency had not paid settlement funds for activities since July 2025, had not responded to requests to review settlement records, and had also failed to provide basic support necessary for their entertainment activities.
