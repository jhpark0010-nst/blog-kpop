PLAVE will become the first virtual (avatar) idol group to hold a solo concert at a stadium venue. Their agency Blast announced on June 15 that PLAVE will launch their first world tour, 'KEEP IT MANIC,' at the main stadium of Incheon Munhak Stadium on September 12 and 13, 2026.

The announcement marks a rapid rise for the group. PLAVE made their debut at Gocheok Sky Dome, a large dome venue in Seoul, in November of last year, and just ten months later they are set to perform at a stadium — a milestone that underscores their growing popularity.

Blast described the upcoming Incheon shows, saying: 'PLAVE will offer fans a special memory through a diverse setlist, elevated performances, and a highly polished stage.'

Following the Incheon opener, PLAVE will visit Kanagawa in Japan, Kaohsiung in Taiwan, Bangkok in Thailand, Singapore, Taipei in Taiwan, and Macau, in that order. Additional tour stops are set to be announced at a later date.
