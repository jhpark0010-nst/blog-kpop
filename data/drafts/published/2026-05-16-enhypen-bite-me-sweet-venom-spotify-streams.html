ENHYPEN's 'Bite Me' and 'Sweet Venom' have each surpassed 500 million and 200 million streams respectively on Spotify, the world's largest music streaming platform, according to an announcement by their label Belift Lab on May 16.

As of May 14, 'Bite Me' had accumulated a total of 500,195,420 streams on Spotify. This marks the first ENHYPEN song to reach the 500 million stream milestone on the platform.

On the same day, 'Sweet Venom' was played for the 200,228,930th time, becoming the group's seventh song overall to surpass 200 million streams on Spotify.

'Bite Me' is a pop track depicting the longing to reunite with a fated partner and the wish to be bitten again by that girl. 'Sweet Venom' is a punk-pop song expressing the resolve to stay by someone's side even as venom spreads through the body.

ENHYPEN has now produced a total of 19 songs that have each surpassed 100 million streams on Spotify.

ENHYPEN will be holding their world tour 'BLOOD SAGA' across 21 cities worldwide for a total of 32 performances, including stops in South America and North America in July and August, Macau in October, and Japan's four major domes from December through February of next year.
