BABYMONSTER made their comeback on May 4 at 6PM KST with their third mini album 'CHOOM,' according to their agency YG Entertainment. The members expressed their ambition to turn the entire world into one giant dance floor, just as the album title suggests.

The new album includes four tracks spanning hip-hop, dance, and R&B genres: the title track 'CHOOM,' along with 'MOON,' 'I LIKE IT,' and 'LOCKED IN.'

Member Ahyeon shared through the agency, 'I wanted to come back quickly and meet our Monsties (fandom name) who have been waiting for us.' She added, 'Above all, I am so excited to be presenting an album full of new charms.'

Chiquita described the title track, saying, 'The sound is full of energy and highly addictive.' She continued, 'If we have been showing hip-hop-based music that showcases our intense charisma, this time we prepared a song where everyone can literally dance together and have fun. Personally, I think the chorus part, which kicks in with a sudden beat change, is the killing part.'

Ruka also said, 'From the intense and hip title track that awakens your dance instincts, to sensory slow songs, the variety means it will be an album that puts you in a good mood no matter when you listen.'

BABYMONSTER will kick off their second world tour, also titled 'CHOOM,' at Seoul's Jamsil Indoor Stadium from June 26 to 28, before heading to Asia, Oceania, Europe, North America, and South America. Member Pharita added, 'We learned and felt so much during the first world tour, so we want to create an even better stage. There are stages waiting at this world tour that we have never shown before, so please look forward to it a lot.'
