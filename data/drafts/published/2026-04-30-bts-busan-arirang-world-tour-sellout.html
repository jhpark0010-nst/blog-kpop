BTS has sold out both nights of their 'Arirang' world tour concert in Busan, scheduled for June 12–13, 2026 at Busan Asiad Main Stadium. Big Hit Music announced on April 30 that all seats for the two-night run were sold out through a pre-sale held on NOL Ticket on April 29.

The Busan dates follow the group's three-night run in Goyang, which began on April 9 and also sold out completely. The back-to-back sellouts mark a strong reception for the 'Arirang' world tour across both cities.

The Busan concerts will mark BTS's return to Busan Asiad Main Stadium for the first time in 3 years and 8 months, since their 'Concert for the Bid of Busan 2030 World Expo' held in October 2022. That venue holds special significance as the site of their last full-group performance before the members began their mandatory military service.

The second night of the Busan run, June 13, carries additional meaning as it falls on the group's debut anniversary. Fans can also look forward to 'BTS The City Arirang,' an offline fan event taking place across Busan from June 5 to June 21.
