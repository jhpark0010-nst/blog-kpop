BTS's fifth studio album 'ARIRANG' has entered the top 3 on Spotify's weekly album chart for nine consecutive weeks. According to the streaming platform Spotify, 'ARIRANG' ranked No. 2 on the Weekly Top Albums Global chart for the tracking period of May 15 to May 21.

The album's title track 'SWIM' also charted at No. 4 on the Weekly Top Songs Global. All 12 vocal tracks included on the new album have now appeared on the chart for nine weeks in a row.

Meanwhile, BTS will attend the American Music Awards, held at MGM Grand Garden Arena on May 25 (local time). The group is competing for Artist of the Year, the ceremony's top honor.
