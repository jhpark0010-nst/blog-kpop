HYBE officially announced the establishment of ABD, a new label specializing in girl group production, on May 8, 2026. The label's name stands for its brand slogan 'A Bold Dream,' and HYBE explained that it embodies a flexible and lively creative spirit — one that imagines 'D instead of C after A and B.' The label's goal is to pursue the pure joy of music while expanding new frontiers of K-pop, grounded in a deep understanding of both fans and artists.

No Ji-won, who previously served as Head of Artist Planning at Pledis Entertainment, has been appointed as CEO of ABD. Having built her label management expertise at Pledis and More Vision, the newly appointed CEO will oversee overall management and work to establish a stable foundation for the new label's growth.

ABD was established as part of HYBE's multi-label strategy, which aims to strengthen the expertise and autonomy of each individual label. HYBE has built a system in which each label operates independently based on its own distinct color and production capabilities, reinforcing its competitiveness in the global music market. ABD likewise plans to present differentiated IP centered on an organization and creator team specialized in girl group production, positioning itself as a dedicated girl group label.

ABD will introduce its first rookie girl group in the second half of this year. Han Sung-su, a Master Professional (MP) who has produced for numerous K-pop artists including SEVENTEEN, After School, IZ*ONE, and TWS, will serve as overall producer, leading the new girl group's music, concept, and performance in their entirety.

A HYBE representative stated, 'The establishment of ABD is a strategic move to secure IP diversity and further advance K-pop production capabilities.' They added, 'Through the new rookie girl group, we will propose new musical attempts and concepts, and create fresh change in the global music market.'
