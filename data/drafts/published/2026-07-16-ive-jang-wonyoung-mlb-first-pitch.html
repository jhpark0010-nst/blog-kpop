K-pop group IVE has been officially invited to attend the New York Mets vs. Los Angeles Dodgers game at Citi Field in New York on July 26 (local time). Starship Entertainment announced the news on July 16, confirming the group will be present at the Major League Baseball (MLB) matchup.

At the game, IVE member Jang Wonyoung is scheduled to take the mound as the ceremonial first pitch thrower.

Jang Wonyoung shared her thoughts on the occasion, saying, 'It is an honor to be able to throw the first pitch at such a major game that baseball fans around the world are watching.' She added, 'I will do my best to deliver good energy to the audience and the players.'

During the MLB visit, IVE is also set to film content alongside former baseball players Kim Byung-hyun and Dustin Nippert.

IVE will be holding a North America tour from July 21 through August 9, with stops in Toronto, Canada, and Oakland, USA, among other cities.
