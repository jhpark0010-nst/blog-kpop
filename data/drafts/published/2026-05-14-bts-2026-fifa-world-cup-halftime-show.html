BTS will take the stage at the 2026 FIFA North America World Cup Final halftime show. On May 14 (local time), Global Citizen and FIFA announced that BTS will serve as co-headliners for the halftime show at the 2026 FIFA World Cup™ Final, set to be held on July 19 at New York New Jersey Stadium in the United States.

This marks the first time a halftime show has ever been held at a FIFA World Cup™ Final. FIFA and Global Citizen planned the event to combine sports, music, and culture into a stage that connects the world. BTS will appear as co-headliners alongside legendary pop stars Madonna and Shakira, placing them at the center of a moment watched by the entire globe.

Through their agency BIGHIT MUSIC, BTS shared their thoughts on the performance: 'It is a great honor to stand on such a meaningful stage shared by the entire world. We believe music is a universal language that conveys hope and unity. It is even more significant that through this World Cup, we can share that message with global audiences and contribute to expanding educational opportunities for children.'

Global Citizen, an international civic movement organization, will produce the halftime show. The organization runs the world's largest movement aimed at ending extreme poverty, and carries out large-scale campaigns and music events to address challenges including climate change, global health, and expanding access to education. The show is expected to help raise awareness and funds for the FIFA Global Citizen Education Fund, which was established to expand access to quality education and sports for marginalized children around the world.

The halftime show will be broadcast live worldwide, with Coldplay's Chris Martin serving as curator. Characters from Sesame Street and The Muppets have also been announced as part of the lineup, promising a show that audiences of all generations can enjoy.
