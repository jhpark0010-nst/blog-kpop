BABYMONSTER's debut music video 'SHEESH' crossed 400 million views on YouTube on April 17, 2026, according to YG Entertainment. This milestone makes 'SHEESH' the fastest-reaching 400 million view debut MV in K-pop girl group history, a remarkable achievement for the group since their debut.

The record-breaking pace has been consistent throughout the MV's journey. 'SHEESH' previously set all-time speed records for K-pop girl group debut MVs at every major milestone — reaching 100 million views in just 10 days, 200 million views in 33 days, and 300 million views in 186 days. Each benchmark shattered the previous fastest record in the category.

Looking ahead, BABYMONSTER is set to release their third mini album 'CHOOM' on May 4, 2026 at 6 p.m. KST. Fans are eagerly anticipating the group's next chapter following the continued global momentum of their debut track.

In other K-pop news, BOYNEXTDOOR has contributed the OST track 'No Doubt' to the MBC drama '21st Century Grand Princess.' Announced by Kakao Entertainment, the song was released on April 17 at 6 p.m. KST. 'No Doubt' blends warm, lyrical country-pop sounds with modern guitar melodies and emotional harmonies. BOYNEXTDOOR also has a new single release planned for next month.

Additionally, Thai singer and actor Bilkin is set to hold his first-ever solo concert in South Korea on June 20, 2026 at KINTEX in Goyang. Organized by ON Planning, this marks his return to Korea for the first time in two years since the 2024 Busan International Film Festival. The concert will feature songs from his existing discography as well as tracks from his new album 'Grow With The Flow.'
