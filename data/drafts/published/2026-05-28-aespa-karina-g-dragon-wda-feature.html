aespa's Karina opened up about the collaboration with G-Dragon on May 28 at a press conference held at the Sofitel Ambassador Seoul Hotel in Songpa-gu, Seoul, celebrating the release of aespa's second full-length album, LEMONADE. The group — consisting of Karina, Winter, Giselle, and Ningning — gathered to mark the occasion.

The pre-release track 'WDA' (Whole Different Animal) drew significant attention for featuring G-Dragon. Karina spoke about the collaboration, saying, 'From the moment we received the demo, it was a song with a featuring in mind,' and added, 'We were wondering who would bring it to life well, and G-Dragon sunbaenim gladly agreed to do it.'

She continued, expressing her gratitude: 'It was a part we loved and had high hopes for, and sunbaenim brought it out in his own style so well that it became even cooler.'

aespa's second full-length album, LEMONADE, is set to be released on May 29 at 1 PM.
