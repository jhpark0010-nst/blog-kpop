NewJeans may be gearing up for a comeback after evidence emerged of a Copenhagen recording studio booking under the name ADOR. A document circulating in online communities appears to show a studio schedule at a recording facility in Copenhagen, Denmark, with ADOR listed as the booking party from the 13th to the 16th of this month. ADOR is NewJeans' agency. The discovery has sparked speculation that NewJeans may have been working on a new album in Copenhagen.

It was previously reported that NewJeans members Haerin, Hyein, and Hanni visited Copenhagen, Denmark together with ADOR staff, drawing significant attention. ADOR also confirmed the visit.

Meanwhile, NewJeans notified ADOR in November 2024 of the termination of their exclusive contracts, citing a breach of management obligations. However, in October of last year, the group lost a first-instance court ruling in a lawsuit filed by ADOR to confirm the validity of those exclusive contracts.

Following the ruling, all members expressed their intention to return, but as of now only three — Haerin, Hyein, and Hanni — have rejoined ADOR. Danielle has been notified of contract termination, and no separate statement has yet been made regarding Minji's status.
