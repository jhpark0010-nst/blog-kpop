BLACKPINK appeared as a complete group at the 2026 Met Gala, marking the first time all four members — Rosé, Jennie, Lisa, and Jisoo — have attended the event together. The group also joined in on the Met Gala's famous bathroom selfie tradition, making their presence impossible to miss.

On May 5, Indian model Babita Mandava posted a photo to her social media account with the caption: 'Posting because I thought it was cute. Might delete later.' The image captured a warm group moment among attendees at the 2026 Met Gala.

In the photo, alongside Mandava, Rachel Sennott — widely known for the HBO series 'The Idol' — and others showcased a variety of looks in line with the 'Costume Art' theme. BLACKPINK members Rosé, Jennie, Lisa, and Jisoo were all pictured together, drawing significant attention.

The Met Gala is an annual charity gala hosted by American Vogue and the Costume Institute at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York. This year's event featured Anna Wintour, Beyoncé, and Nicole Kidman as co-hosts.

Also in attendance were Sam Smith, Doja Cat, Sabrina Carpenter, Anok Yai, Amanda Seyfried, and Emily Blunt, among other Hollywood stars. Korean celebrities present alongside BLACKPINK included Ahn Hyo-seop, Jung Ho-yeon, aespa's Karina and NingNing, and Lee Jae.
