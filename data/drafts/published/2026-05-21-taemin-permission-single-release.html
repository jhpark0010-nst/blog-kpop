SHINee member Taemin will release a new digital single, 'PERMISSION,' on May 22, according to his agency Galaxy Corporation, which announced the news on May 21.

'PERMISSION' is the song Taemin first performed at Coachella, the world-renowned music festival, in April. The track contains Taemin's autobiographical story of choosing and taking charge of his own life.

His agency described the song as 'a track that lets you see the artistic direction Taemin pursues.'

Following the release of the new single, Taemin will hold a solo concert together with his fellow SHINee members from May 29 to 31 at KSPO Dome in Olympic Park, Songpa-gu, Seoul.
