2PM drew 85,000 fans to their 15th anniversary concert 'THE RETURN' held at Tokyo Dome on May 9 and 10, according to their agency JYP Entertainment on May 13. It marks the group's first performance at the iconic venue in 10 years, since 2016.

Over the course of approximately three hours, the members performed 25 songs, including their Japanese debut single 'Take Off' and Korean hit 'I'm Your Man.'

2PM shared, 'Being able to meet again at Tokyo Dome, our dream stage, feels like a miracle.' They added, 'This is a day we will never forget. We will definitely be back.'

After the concert, member Taecyeon conveyed his feelings through the agency, saying, 'Standing on stage as all six members together after such a long time and facing the audience made me truly feel that I am a very happy person.'

2PM is set to hold their first full-group domestic concert in three years at Inspire Arena in Incheon on August 8 and 9.
