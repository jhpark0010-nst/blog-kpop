NewJeans member Danielle (21, Daniel Marsh) shared an update with fans on July 22, the group's 4th debut anniversary. The former NewJeans member posted two photos on her SNS along with the message: 'Life can sometimes get cloudy, but the light always comes back. Keep shining until then.'

In the photos, Danielle is seen posing in various ways against a backdrop of a blue sky and lake, dressed in casual attire. She paired a cap, a zip-up hoodie, and shorts for a natural look, showcasing her signature fresh charm. The post drew particular attention as it fell on the exact date of NewJeans' 4th debut anniversary.

Meanwhile, the other four members — Minji, Hanni, Haerin, and Hyein — uploaded a video together through their official channel, raising anticipation for a return to the stage. Danielle, by contrast, is currently in a legal dispute with her agency ADOR over alleged exclusive contract violations. The damages claim was originally 43.1 billion KRW but has reportedly been adjusted down to 33.09 billion KRW.

Attention is now focused on Danielle's next steps, as she delivered her message to fans separately from NewJeans as a group. Separately, NewJeans surprised fans on the same day by releasing a special film featuring all four members — Minji, Hyein, Hanni, and Haerin — without prior notice through their official YouTube channel at midnight. It marks the first time in one year and four months that NewJeans has produced and released new content as a team.

NewJeans debuted on July 22, 2022, with the music video for their debut track 'Attention.' They went on to score hit after hit with songs including 'Hype Boy,' 'Ditto,' 'OMG,' and 'Super Shy,' achieving syndrome-level popularity. In August 2023, their second mini-album 'Get Up' reached No. 1 on the Billboard 200, making them only the second K-pop girl group to top the chart. The group has not released new music since their Japanese single 'Supernatural' in June 2024.
