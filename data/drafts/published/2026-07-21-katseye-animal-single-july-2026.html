HYBE Labels announced on July 21 that KATSEYE, the global girl group formed by HYBE and Geffen Records, will release their new single 'Animal' on July 24 at 1:00 PM KST.

This marks KATSEYE's first new release in three months, following 'PINKY UP,' which came out in April. The group also has their third mini-album, 'WILD,' scheduled for release on August 14.

KATSEYE is a global girl group introduced by HYBE in 2024, having made their debut in the United States.

In less than two years since their debut, the group has built a strong presence by performing at major music festivals such as Lollapalooza and Coachella, as well as at prominent awards show stages.

In May, KATSEYE achieved a triple win at the 2026 American Music Awards — one of the three major popular music awards in the United States — including the award for New Artist of the Year.
