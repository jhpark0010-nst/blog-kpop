Wanna One will appear at KCON LA 2026, held in Los Angeles from August 14 to 16. CJ ENM announced on July 7 that the group has been confirmed for the event.

As part of the KCON program, Wanna One will take the stage at the M Countdown show on August 14 at Crypto.com Arena.

This marks their first KCON appearance in nine years, since 2017.

Nine members will perform together. Kang Daniel, who is currently fulfilling his mandatory military service, and Lai Guanlin, who is working as a film director in China, will not be joining.

Wanna One debuted in 2017 through Mnet's audition program 'Produce 101' Season 2 and concluded their activities as a project group in 2019. The members recently reunited for the first time in five years through the reality variety show 'Wanna One Go: Back to Base,' and will also deliver a performance at KCON.

CJ ENM stated, 'The members have long missed sharing the same stage, and they came together with one heart despite busy schedules. They will give fans who have waited a truly unforgettable moment.'
