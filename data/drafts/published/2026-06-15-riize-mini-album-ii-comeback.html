RIIZE has announced their confident comeback with the release of their second mini album 'II' on June 15 at 6PM KST. The album includes a total of 6 tracks: the title song 'Do your dance,' along with 'SOAR,' 'D-D-Done,' 'Overdrive,' 'Like a Bomb,' and 'In a Loop' — all reflecting the group's free-spirited energy and self-assurance.

The title track 'Do your dance' is an uptempo dance song that blends intense hip-hop beats with sleek electronic pop sounds. Key highlights include a raw distortion 808 bass driving the track and a coolly repetitive hook. The performance also introduces signature hand gestures that visually mirror the lyrics, along with distinctive pointing choreography designed to heighten visual immersion. The music video, set to drop simultaneously on the YouTube SMTOWN channel at 6PM, captures RIIZE's dynamic choreography in a one-take format.

Prior to the official release, RIIZE held a countdown live on their YouTube and TikTok channels at 4:30PM KST. The following Q&A covers the members' thoughts on the album and comeback.

Asked about their feelings returning with a new album after the single 'Fame,' Wonbin said, 'Because all the songs are great, I think it is a masterpiece. I feel like we can promote with just as much confidence. More than any words, I just want to show everyone on stage quickly.' Sohee added, 'This is a comeback after 7 months, so I am excited — thank you to BRIIZE (official fan club name) for waiting. All of our members have enormous confidence in this album, so please look forward to it and listen a lot.'

When asked to define the second mini album 'II' in their own words, Sohee said, 'II is a fun album! From the first time I heard the songs to the process of preparing, I enjoyed everything. Even as time passes, I think this album will remain in my memory as fun and joy — I hope that feeling reaches everyone too.' Anton said, 'II is movement. Simply put, this is the first album RIIZE is releasing in Korea this year, so it feels like activities are truly beginning in earnest. If we have mainly shown RIIZE's worries in the process of growing and realizing things, through this album you will be able to meet a RIIZE that moves through action rather than worry. I wanted to convey the feeling that RIIZE's journey toward going higher and further has begun.' Eunseok said, 'II is a crayon wearing colorful shades. All 6 songs on the album have different styles, so I think it is enough if you enjoy them however you like.'

On the title track 'Do your dance,' Shotaro said, 'From the first impression I thought it was trendy. There are a lot of fun parts in both the performance and the lyrics — Wonbin's line 'scratch it and it won't leave a mark, My youth,' and my own part 'Break charts, break hearts, all this RIIZE on your feed' — I hope you find the kind of fun that suits your taste.' Eunseok said, 'It is a song that fits anytime and anywhere, but the timing I recommend listening is when you want to gain enormous confidence or when you want to forget all your worries and just have fun.' Sungchan said, 'The performance has a lot of killing parts. Especially the section right before the chorus where we stop our movements in time with the beat and snap our heads — tap! tap! tap! — even during practice, when we nailed it together it felt so satisfying. I think it will leave a strong impression on viewers as well.' Wonbin said, 'You can feel our passion that is enough to beat the hot summer. The song has a hooky point, 'Head, hips, shoulders, toes,' that will keep ringing in your ears, and you will keep wanting to see more of RIIZE's best performances.' Sohee said, 'As I mentioned in the album introduction, I hope it is remembered by the keyword of having fun. Each song has a wonderful meaning, but please do not try to analyze it too much — just feel and enjoy RIIZE!' Anton said, 'I think 'Like a pro,' which is also a lyric, is the core of the song. To me, it is a mindset. Even if you are not a professional in any field, I think overcoming difficulties while enjoying yourself is a pro-like mindset. While preparing for this comeback I also learned new things and gained more confidence, and I want to give that confidence to listeners as well.'

Asked which B-side track they are most attached to, Eunseok said, 'The song that hooked me right away was 'Like a Bomb.' Sohee really loves this song too. The structure is clear and the melody is well-crafted — it is personally my style. Compared to the other tracks it has a more mainstream feel, so I think many people will love it.' Sungchan said, 'Definitely 'D-D-Done.' It is my all-time favorite among all RIIZE songs. It was my taste from the demo, and I think I will keep coming back to it over time. It is an easy-listening track so you can enjoy it in a chill way anytime.' Shotaro said, 'D-D-Done and Overdrive. I originally like house and R&B hip-hop music, and they are that kind of style. I thought they were perfect for summer. I also imagined the stages — 'D-D-Done' would be great at a festival, and 'Overdrive' at a concert or somewhere with big speakers.'

On their sources of confidence, Shotaro said, 'Definitely the amount of practice. Practice is important. And outfits are important too — when you like what you are wearing, your confidence shoots up (laughs). So while practicing 'Do your dance' I even picked out and gifted outfits that would suit each member.' Eunseok said, 'I also feel confident when I have practiced enough.' Sungchan said, 'Above all, it is BRIIZE's reactions.' Wonbin said, 'That is right. When BRIIZE responds well, it gets even more exciting. Having great hair, makeup, and styling also gives me confidence.' Sohee said, 'A mirror. When I dance I look in the mirror, and when I monitor I look in the mirror — without a mirror my confidence drops. (But there is no mirror on stage?) When I am on stage, BRIIZE is there in place of a mirror!' Anton said, 'On stage, it is the members. Just having the members beside me gives me confidence.'

When asked what they want to hear from this promotion, Shotaro said, 'These guys are cool.' Eunseok said, 'Now even being called cool is not enough. I want to earn the title of a group that delivers on stage.' Sungchan said, 'I will go with a pledge — everyone, you have worked hard to get here, and I will really show you something proper.' Wonbin said, 'I want to hear: RIIZE can do this too, they have great momentum, and even non-fans want to see RIIZE's stage.' Sohee said, 'Wow! RIIZE performs so well!' Anton said, 'As expected, RIIZE.'
