TOMORROW X TOGETHER (TXT) member Yeonjun has written a new chapter in his Japanese career. According to the latest weekly Oricon charts announced on July 22, Yeonjun's second mini album 'NO LABELS: PART 02' entered the Weekly Combined Album Ranking (dated July 27 / tallied July 13–19) at No. 2.

With this result, Yeonjun improved on the No. 3 position he achieved with his first solo release last year, reaching a new career high. The Weekly Combined Album Ranking is calculated by combining CD sales, digital download counts, and streaming figures.

'NO LABELS: PART 02' also topped the Weekly Western Music Album Ranking for the same period, and appeared on the Weekly Album Ranking (No. 2) and the Weekly Digital Album Ranking (No. 5). The title track 'Ice Cream' charted for two consecutive weeks on the Weekly Combined Singles Ranking, Weekly Digital Singles Ranking, and Weekly Streaming Ranking, demonstrating strong and sustained influence.

The album's popularity was also confirmed on the latest Billboard Japan charts (tallied July 13–19). 'Ice Cream' ranked No. 7 on the Hot Shot Songs chart and appeared on multiple additional charts including the Japan Hot 100, Streaming Songs, and Download Songs, proving high local interest.

Yeonjun is also making his mark on global charts. On the latest U.S. Billboard chart dated July 25, 'NO LABELS: PART 02' debuted at No. 16 on the main album chart, the Billboard 200. This is the highest position achieved by a Korean solo artist's album released this year. Yeonjun demonstrated his consistent power by charting with two consecutive solo releases.

Yeonjun released 'NO LABELS: PART 02' on July 10. With the title track 'Ice Cream,' he claimed two music show wins — No. 1 trophies on KBS2's Music Bank and MBC's Show! Music Core. He is also set to perform on Mnet's M Countdown on July 23.
