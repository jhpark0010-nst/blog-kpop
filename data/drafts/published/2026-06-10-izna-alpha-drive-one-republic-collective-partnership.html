Groups izna and ALPHA DRIVE ONE are set to make a full-scale push into the global market. According to WAKEONE on June 10, izna and ALPHA DRIVE ONE have signed a partnership with prominent US music label REPUBLIC Collective.

REPUBLIC Collective wields powerful influence in the global music industry, backed by an extensive global network and a top-tier artist lineup.

The partnership was made possible after REPUBLIC Collective took positive notice of the meaningful achievements, global growth potential, and authentic energy that izna and ALPHA DRIVE ONE — both born through global fan votes — have demonstrated.

izna, who released their third mini album 'SET THE TEMPO' on June 8 and are continuing a successful comeback, was named one of Grammy.com's 'K-pop Rookies to Watch in 2025' just two months after their debut. Italian weekly magazine PANORAMA introduced them as 'the discovery K-pop has been waiting for,' and UK music magazine NME listed 'Racecar' as one of the '25 Best K-pop Songs of 2025,' confirming their presence on the global stage.

izna has also surpassed 500 million cumulative views on their official YouTube channel and recorded 400 million YouTube Music streams, demonstrating the power of their global fandom. Beyond music, they have expanded their reach through drama and webtoon OST performances, MC appearances, and modeling for fashion and beauty brands, establishing themselves as a 'global icon.'

ALPHA DRIVE ONE made a strong entrance into the K-pop world upon their debut on January 12 of this year. Their debut mini album 'EUPHORIA' sold 1.44 million copies in first-week sales, ranking 2nd among K-pop artist debut first-week records. The album also charted at No. 7 on the Worldwide iTunes Album Chart immediately after release and entered the TOP 5 in 18 regions worldwide, including Japan, Singapore, Thailand, Malaysia, Taiwan, and Russia.

International media attention is also growing. Forbes and Vogue Latin America have spotlighted ALPHA DRIVE ONE's debut journey and growth story, highly evaluating their presence and potential both on and off stage. ALPHA DRIVE ONE released their prologue single 'No School Tomorrow' on May 26 and has been actively proving their global momentum.

Through this strategic partnership aimed at a successful global debut, ALPHA DRIVE ONE will carry out global activities alongside a major US music label from the earliest stages. The partnership is expected to expand ALPHA DRIVE ONE's reach to the world stage, create synergy with REPUBLIC Collective, and accelerate the growth of their global fandom.

A WAKEONE representative stated, 'We are delighted to be working with REPUBLIC Collective at this important moment when izna and ALPHA DRIVE ONE's global expansion is beginning in earnest.' They added, 'Going forward, we will work closely with REPUBLIC Collective to carry out wide-ranging activities that expand the artists' influence on the global stage.'
