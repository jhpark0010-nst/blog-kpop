Girl group (G)I-DLE marked their 8th debut anniversary on May 2, according to their agency Cube Entertainment. The group debuted on May 2, 2018, with the mini-album 'I am' and has since been loved for major hit songs including 'TOMBOY', 'Nxde', and 'Queencard'.

(G)I-DLE shared a message through their agency: 'Time feels like it has passed so quickly.' They added, 'The reason the past 8 years are filled with happy memories is all thanks to Neverland (their fandom name). We were so happy to be together with our members, and we will work hard for 80 more years.'

(G)I-DLE also hinted at their upcoming comeback, currently in the final stages of production with a target release in July. They said, 'It might be a little different from the image of (G)I-DLE that many people picture.' They continued, 'While trying a new style, we will still capture the side of us that fans love.'

Group CORTIS is approaching 2.4 million pre-orders for their second mini-album 'GREENGREEN', set for release on May 4, according to their agency Big Hit Music. According to distributors YG Plus and Universal Records, pre-orders as of April 30 stood at 2,397,188 copies.

Approximately 350,000 additional pre-orders were added after the album's title track 'REDRED' was released ahead of the full album on April 20. Pre-saves for 'GREENGREEN' on Spotify, the world's largest music streaming platform, reached 999,000 as of the same date. CORTIS will hold a release party at S-Factory in Seongdong-gu, Seoul at 8 p.m. on May 4, the album's release date, performing the new songs live.

Band Na Sanghyun Mr. Band announced through their agency January that they will release a new single 'Jeomun' at 6 p.m. on May 2. 'Jeomun' is a double single consisting of two new tracks, 'Jeo' and 'Mun', capturing the process of moving on to the next chapter amid the fading time of youth as one continuous flow. The band plans to perform at various university festivals and music festivals this spring and summer, and will also launch a nationwide tour in the summer.
