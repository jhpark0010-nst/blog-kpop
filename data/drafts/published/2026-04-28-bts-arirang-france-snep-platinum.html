BTS's 5th studio album 'ARIRANG' has been certified Platinum in the album category by the French music industry association SNEP (Syndicat National de l'Édition Phonographique), label Big Hit Music announced on April 28. The certification is awarded to albums that achieve sales equivalent to 100,000 units, combining physical album sales, downloads, and streaming.

With this certification, BTS now holds a total of four Platinum-certified albums in France in the group album category, following 'MAP OF THE SOUL : 7', 'Proof', and 'LOVE YOURSELF 結 ANSWER'.

Big Hit Music stated, 'ARIRANG received a Gold certification just 6 days after its release, followed by a Platinum certification approximately one month later.' The label added, 'Considering that MAP OF THE SOUL : 7 and Proof each took one year and two months to receive the same certification, it is clear that BTS's album power in France has been further strengthened.'

Meanwhile, 'Let Go', a track from BTS's Japanese 3rd studio album released in 2018, surpassed 100 million cumulative streams in March and received a Platinum certification in the streaming category from the Recording Industry Association of Japan (100 million streams or more). The new album 'ARIRANG' also received a Triple Platinum certification in Japan as of March, with cumulative shipments exceeding 750,000 copies.

BTS kicked off their North America tour on April 25 (local time) at Raymond James Stadium in Tampa, USA. They will continue the tour at Sun Bowl Stadium in El Paso on May 2 and 3.
