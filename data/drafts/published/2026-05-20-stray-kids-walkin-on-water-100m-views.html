Stray Kids' music video for 'Walkin On Water' has surpassed 100 million views on YouTube, their agency JYP Entertainment announced on May 20. The video crossed the milestone the previous afternoon, becoming the group's 20th music video to reach 100 million views.

'Walkin On Water' is the title track from the album 'HAP' (合), which reached number one on the Billboard 200, the main album chart in the United States. Stray Kids described the song as a metaphor for their confidence on stage — likening it to walking on water and enjoying the rush of rough currents.

The group is set to perform as headliners at The Governors Ball Music Festival in New York, USA on June 6, and at Rock in Rio in Brazil on September 11 (both local time).

In March and April, Stray Kids held their sixth fan meeting at Inspire Arena, where they met their fanbase known as STAY.
