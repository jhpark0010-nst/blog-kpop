TOMORROW X TOGETHER's Yeonjun is making his presence felt as a fashion icon. Luxury house Miu Miu announced on April 27 that Yeonjun has been named the brand's Friend of the House.

'Friend of the House' is a title given to partners who share the brand's aesthetic values and convey what the brand stands for to the public through their own distinctive personality and style. Yeonjun was selected as the brand's very first Friend of the House, demonstrating his global influence.

Yeonjun shared his thoughts, saying, 'I have liked and been interested in the brand for a long time.' He added, 'It is meaningful and exciting to be joining together. Please look forward to the journey ahead, where I will express the free and bold energy the brand holds in my own way.'

Last month, Yeonjun made a surprise appearance as a model at the F/W 26 show held in Paris, France, becoming the first K-pop male artist to walk the brand's runway and leaving a strong impression.
