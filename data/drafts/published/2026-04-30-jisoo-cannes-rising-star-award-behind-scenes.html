BLACKPINK's Jisoo shared behind-the-scenes photos from the Cannes awards ceremony along with her acceptance remarks. On April 30, Jisoo posted on her SNS: 'It is an honor to receive the Madame Figaro Rising Star Award at Cannes Series. It was an even more special moment because I could share it with everyone who supported me.' She added, 'I will cherish this memory for a long time.'

In the photos shared alongside the post, Jisoo wore her hair softly tied up with pink-toned makeup, exuding a graceful yet radiant charm. She pulled off a romantic pink dress flawlessly, creating an atmosphere reminiscent of a fairytale princess.

In another shot, she showcased a contrasting look in a black sleeveless dress. Frill details flowing along the neckline paired with a braided hairstyle emphasized an elegant mood, while her sharp facial features drew attention.

Jisoo had previously won the Madame Figaro Rising Star Award at the 9th Cannes International Series Festival held in Cannes, France on April 23. Immediately after receiving the award, she expressed her gratitude with a bright smile, saying 'Merci beaucoup' in French. The organizers cited her Netflix series 'Monthly Boyfriend' among other works, describing her as 'a figure with international influence and the potential for artistic growth.'

Meanwhile, Jisoo, a member of BLACKPINK, had also drawn a clear line regarding a recently raised controversy involving her family. She stated, 'This matter is unrelated to the artist and the agency,' and made clear that 'there has been no financial or legal support whatsoever, and there are no plans for any in the future.'
