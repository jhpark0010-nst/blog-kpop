SUPER JUNIOR-83z, the new unit formed by Leeteuk and Heechul, is already generating major buzz ahead of its official debut. The unit is set to make its formal debut in July 2026, and will embark on a fan concert tour titled '1983,' covering nine cities across Asia: Seoul, Tokyo, Bangkok, Hong Kong, Kuala Lumpur, Macau, Kaohsiung, Singapore, and Taipei.

The Seoul leg of the fan concert tour, which kicks off the '1983' tour, was originally scheduled for July 25 and 26 at Olympic Hall in Olympic Park, Songpa-gu. Following the ticket pre-sale opening last week, all sessions sold out completely, and due to the overwhelming response, an additional date on July 24 was added, underscoring the high level of interest in SUPER JUNIOR-83z.

With the added date, SUPER JUNIOR-83z's Seoul performances will now take place over three nights from July 24 to 26. Tickets for the newly added July 24 show will be available via Melon Ticket. A pre-sale for members of SUPER JUNIOR's official fan club E.L.F. opens on the 29th at 8 p.m., followed by general ticketing on the 30th at 8 p.m.

SUPER JUNIOR-83z is a new unit within SUPER JUNIOR, formed by Leeteuk and Heechul, who were both born in 1983. Despite sharing the same birth year, the two members are said to differ in virtually every other way, and fans can look forward to the unexpected chemistry that this contrast is expected to bring.
