Singer-songwriter Lee Jae, who rose to global stardom through the Netflix animated film 'K-Pop Demon Hunters,' has participated as a vocalist on 'DNA,' the official anthem of the 2026 FIFA North, Central America and Caribbean World Cup.

FIFA revealed the song on June 11 through its official social media channels. The anthem features Lee Jae alongside Andrea Bocelli, David Guetta, and Megan Thee Stallion.

Lee Jae drew attention by singing Korean-language lyrics in the latter part of the track. The line translates as: 'Even if I fall again, I will rise once more.'

FIFA also announced that Andrea Bocelli and Lee Jae are scheduled to perform at the opening ceremony, which will be held in Mexico City.
