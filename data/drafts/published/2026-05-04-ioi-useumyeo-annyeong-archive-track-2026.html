I.O.I has released an archive track, turning the first page of an emotional story set to continue after nine years. The group — comprising Lim Na-young, Chungha, Kim Sejeong, Jung Chaeyeon, Kim Sohye, Yoo Yeonjung, Choi Yoojung, Kim Doyeon, and Jeon Somi — dropped the pre-release single 'Useumyeo Annyeong (Recorded in 2016) (Prod. Jinyoung)' (hereafter 'Useumyeo Annyeong') at 6 PM KST on May 4 across major online music platforms, ahead of their third mini album 'I.O.I : LOOP'.

The pre-release track 'Useumyeo Annyeong' is an archive recording that preserves the members' unadorned voices and emotions exactly as they were captured in 2016. Producer Jinyoung, who has shared a deep musical bond with I.O.I over the years, participated in the production to bring out the song's distinctive lyrical quality to its fullest.

The lyrics carry the warm promise of that era — a choice to smile instead of cry, cheering each other on toward the future even in the face of an approaching farewell. The heartfelt goodbye from ten years ago, which instantly rewinds the stopped clock of time, is set to leave a deep, timeless impression on both I.O.I and their fans.

Having warmed up for their comeback with this pre-release single, I.O.I will announce their full return with the new album 'I.O.I : LOOP' on May 19. The album, which connects nine years of history into a single line, features members Jeon Somi, Chungha, and Yoo Yeonjung, among others, who took a leading role in writing and composing the title track 'Gapjagi' as well as the album's other tracks, adding to its musical authenticity.

There is greater interest than ever in the musical synergy the members — each of whom has grown fiercely in their own respective fields — will create upon reuniting.

Meanwhile, I.O.I, who are kicking off their 10th debut anniversary project with the pre-release single 'Useumyeo Annyeong', will follow the May 19 mini album release with the grand opening of their Asia tour '2026 I.O.I Concert Tour: LOOP', which runs for three days from May 29 to 31 at the Jamsil Indoor Stadium in Seoul.
