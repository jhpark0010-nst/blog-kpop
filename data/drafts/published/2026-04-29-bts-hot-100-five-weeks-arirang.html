BTS has landed two tracks from their fifth studio album ARIRANG on the Billboard Hot 100 for five consecutive weeks. According to Billboard's April 28 (local time) chart update, the album's title track 'SWIM' debuted at No. 22, while album cut 'Body To Body' came in at No. 95.

'SWIM' first entered the Hot 100 at No. 1, then charted at No. 2, No. 5, and No. 10 in subsequent weeks, making this its fifth straight week on the chart. 'Body To Body' also achieved five consecutive weeks on the Hot 100, having entered at No. 25.

Also on the Hot 100, 'Golden,' the original soundtrack from the Netflix animated film 'K-Pop Demon Hunters,' landed at No. 9 for its 44th consecutive week on the chart. Tame Impala's 'Dracula,' which features Jennie on a remix version, came in at No. 16, and KATSEYE's 'PINKY UP' charted at No. 49.

On the Billboard 200 main album chart, TOMORROW X TOGETHER's eighth mini-album '7TH YEAR: When the Wind Briefly Stopped in the Thorny Thicket' debuted at No. 3. BTS's ARIRANG fell one spot from the previous week to land at No. 4.

The 'K-Pop Demon Hunters' OST album rose nine spots from the previous week to reach No. 7. KATSEYE's second mini-album 'Beautiful Chaos,' released in June of last year, held steady at No. 95, the same position as the week prior.
