I.O.I has released a full-member group photo for the first time in nine years, sending fan excitement for their upcoming comeback to an all-time high. The nine-member group — Im Na-young, Chungha, Kim Sejeong, Jung Chaeyeon, Kim Sohye, Yoo Yeonjung, Choi Yoojung, Kim Doyeon, and Jeon Somi — unveiled the group image for their third mini-album 'I.O.I : LOOP' through their official social media at midnight on April 24.

I.O.I immediately captured attention with witty, expressive looks that highlight each member's individual personality. True to the caption printed at the bottom of the photo — 'We met again as who we became' — the members, each having grown in their own way, reunited to reveal their unfiltered, natural charm.

What stands out most about this group image is how naturally it captures the members just as they are. I.O.I's signature approachable charm is now layered with a deeper artistic sensibility, striking fans as both familiar and refreshingly new.

'I.O.I : LOOP' is the group's first new release in nine years, from a group originally formed through Mnet's 'Produce 101' in 2016. The album is set to complete the unfinished fragments of their youth. Reunited once more, the members aim to keep their promise to fans by showing the most authentically I.O.I version of themselves through this activity.

Alongside the album release, I.O.I will hold the world tour '2026 I.O.I Concert Tour: LOOP' to connect directly with global fans. The tour kicks off with three days of Seoul concerts beginning May 29, followed by Bangkok on June 6, and then Hong Kong on June 20 and 21, as the group brings their unique energy to major cities across Asia.

I.O.I's third mini-album 'I.O.I : LOOP' will be released across major online music platforms on May 19 at 6 p.m. A pre-release track will drop ahead of that on May 4.
