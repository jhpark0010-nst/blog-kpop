Super Junior, Stray Kids, and aespa are among the K-pop acts confirmed to appear at the '2026 TME Live International Music Awards' — known as the 2026 TIMA — a major Chinese-sphere music awards ceremony. The event will be held on August 22 and 23 at Kai Tak Stadium in Hong Kong.

According to the official 2026 TIMA social media accounts on July 14, a large number of leading K-pop artists have been added to the lineup for the two-day ceremony.

On the first day, August 22, the performing acts include 82MAJOR, Kiki, NOWZ, All Day Project, AlphaDrive1, and Hearts2Hearts. Also on the Day 1 lineup are NCT's Taeyong, aespa, BABYMONSTER, RIIZE, Super Junior, and TOMORROW X TOGETHER. TXT member Yeonjun will participate both as part of the group and as a solo artist.

On the second day, August 23, the stage will feature Kickflip, NEXZ, Unchild, ANDbl, Evan, Miao, NMIXX, PLAVE, WayV, IVE, NCT WISH, Stray Kids, and Girls' Generation's Taeyeon, among others. Global pop star Charlie Puth is also set to perform.

The 2026 TIMA is a music awards show newly established last year by Tencent Music Entertainment Group, China's largest music streaming platform company.
