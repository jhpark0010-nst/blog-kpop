ILLIT's Japanese member Moka is making a surprise return to music show stages on May 29, 2026, keeping her promise to fans after temporarily halting activities due to a sudden health issue. Agency Belift Lab officially announced on the 29th that Moka will join ILLIT for the final week of music show performances, beginning with KBS2's Music Bank airing that afternoon.

According to the agency, Moka had suspended all schedules and focused entirely on treatment and rest during her hiatus. She has since received a final medical opinion confirming that she may resume activities within a manageable range. Her own strong desire to reconnect with fans played a decisive role in this surprise return.

Belift Lab explained: 'The artist herself had a very strong wish to get back on stage and meet fans in person before the Mini 4th Album MAMIHLAPINATAPAI promotions came to a close.' They added: 'After thorough discussion, we decided she would rejoin activities starting with today's Music Bank stage.' The agency noted that some schedules may be managed flexibly depending on her condition, and that she will unfortunately not be participating in the 2026 Weverse Con Festival on June 6, as she was unable to take part in rehearsals during her absence.

Earlier, on the 20th of last month, Belift Lab had announced that Moka showed sudden symptoms of a health problem and would focus solely on treatment and rest for the time being, drawing concern from fans worldwide. As a result, Moka was unable to participate in the early stages of ILLIT's comeback promotions and title track activities following the release of Mini 4th Album MAMIHLAPINATAPAI on the 30th of last month. With ILLIT having continued their comeback as a four-member group in her absence, Moka's return is expected to bring a complete and memorable close to their final week of music show activities.

Below is the full official statement from ILLIT's agency. Hello, this is Belift Lab. ILLIT member Moka will be joining the final week of music show stages. Moka has been focusing on recovery through treatment and rest, and has received a medical opinion that she may resume activities within a manageable range. In particular, given her strong personal wish to take the stage and meet fans before the Mini 4th Album MAMIHLAPINATAPAI promotions conclude, we have decided she will begin participating in activities starting with today's Music Bank stage.

However, please understand that some schedules may be managed flexibly in consideration of the artist's condition as our top priority. Additionally, Moka will not be attending the 2026 Weverse Con Festival on Saturday, June 6, as she was unable to participate in rehearsals. We sincerely thank all fans who waited for Moka's recovery and sent warm support. We will do our best to ensure Moka can meet fans again in good health. Thank you.
