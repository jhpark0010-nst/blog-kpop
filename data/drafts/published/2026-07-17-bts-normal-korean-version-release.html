BTS released additional versions of 'NORMAL,' a track from their fifth studio album ARIRANG, on July 17 at 1:00 PM KST. The surprise drop adds new audio options for fans around the world.

The newly released audio comes in three versions total, including a Korean-language version and an instrumental version. The Korean version was first performed live at the BTS WORLD TOUR 'ARIRANG' IN BUSAN concert in June.

'NORMAL' previously entered the US Billboard main song chart Hot 100 at No. 41 on the April 4 chart, remaining on the chart for three consecutive weeks. The seven members expressed the emptiness and fear that exist behind the spotlight in a calm yet powerful way. World-renowned producer Ryan Tedder contributed to the track, and the song's smooth, conversational singing-rap style received widespread praise.

Meanwhile, BTS is set to take the stage at the 2026 FIFA World Cup North America™ Final halftime show on July 19 (local time) at New York New Jersey Stadium, alongside pop stars Madonna, Shakira, and Justin Bieber.
