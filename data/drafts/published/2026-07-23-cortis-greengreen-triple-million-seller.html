CORTIS's second mini album 'GREENGREEN' has surpassed 3 million copies in sales just 11 weeks after its release, according to an announcement by their agency Big Hit Music on July 23.

According to the latest weekly album chart released by the Circle Chart (covering July 12–18), 'GREENGREEN', released on May 4, has recorded cumulative sales of 3,099,000 copies.

With this achievement, CORTIS's album becomes the second album released domestically this year to reach 'triple million-seller' status, following BTS's fifth full-length album 'ARIRANG'.

The title track of the second mini album, 'REDRED', continues to hold a top position on major domestic music streaming platforms including Melon.

CORTIS will be embarking on their 'Put Your Phone Down' tour across 6 cities including Toronto, Canada and San Francisco, USA from August 4 to 16 (local time).
