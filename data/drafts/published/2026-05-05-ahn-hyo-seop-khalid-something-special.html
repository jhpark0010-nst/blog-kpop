Actor Ahn Hyo-seop and pop artist Khalid are set to release their global single 'Something Special' on May 22, according to an announcement made by The Present Company on May 5.

'Something Special' is a global collaboration project that blends K-pop and American R&B sensibilities. The project was made possible through a collaboration between domestic music platform MusicCow and global entertainment company Roc Nation.

Pre-saves for 'Something Special' are currently available on major global music streaming platforms ahead of its release.
