The 3rd Asia Star Entertainer Awards 2026 (ASEA 2026) was held on May 16 at Belluna Dome in Saitama Prefecture, Japan. On Day 1, the grand prize winners were ENHYPEN, &TEAM, and BLACKPINK.

ENHYPEN claimed the top honor of Artist of the Year. With their sixth mini-album 'DESIRE : UNLEASH', they also took Album of the Year and the main award THE PLATINUM, achieving a triple crown. ENHYPEN won a grand prize for the second consecutive year, reaffirming their dominant standing in the global music market.

&TEAM went beyond the main awards to claim their first-ever grand prize, Performance of the Year, marking a new career high as they continue their streak of three consecutive million-selling albums. BLACKPINK won Song of the Year with their track 'JUMP' (뛰어).

ALPHA DRIVE ONE achieved a double crown by winning both THE BEST NEW ARTIST and THE PLATINUM, demonstrating their status as a major rookie boy group. THE PLATINUM was also awarded to IVE and Japanese groups FRUITS ZIPPER and CUTIE STREET.

Actors Lee Junho and Ko Yoon-jung each took home THE BEST CHARACTER. Lee Junho also won THE BEST ARTIST, determined by netizen voting, while Ko Yoon-jung won THE BEST OTT ARTIST, making both of them double winners.

The awards ceremony continues on May 17, with TVXQ's Choikang Changmin and actress Lee Sung-kyung serving as MCs. Performers and presenters for Day 2 include ATEEZ, Hearts2Hearts, IKUTA TOMA, KWON EUNBI, NOWZ, THE RAMPAGE from EXILE TRIBE, WONHO, xikers, and Lee Chaemin. The full Day 1 winners list is as follows — ARTIST OF THE YEAR: ENHYPEN / ALBUM OF THE YEAR: ENHYPEN 'DESIRE : UNLEASH' / SONG OF THE YEAR: BLACKPINK 'JUMP' / PERFORMANCE OF THE YEAR: &TEAM / THE PLATINUM: ENHYPEN, IVE, &TEAM, ALPHA DRIVE ONE, FRUITS ZIPPER, CUTIE STREET / THE BEST NEW ARTIST: ALPHA DRIVE ONE / TOP TOURING ARTIST: MONSTA X / THE BEST ARTIST (ACTOR): Lee Junho, Kim Hyeyoon / THE BEST OTT ARTIST: Ko Yoon-jung / THE BEST CHARACTER: Lee Junho, Ko Yoon-jung / THE BEST PERFORMANCE: KYOKA / THE BEST SOLO: Lim Young-woong, Yuqi / THE BEST TROT: Lim Young-woong / HOT ICON: DXTEEN, FRUITS ZIPPER, CUTIE STREET / HOT TREND: IDID, VVUP, SWEET STEADY, CANDY TUNE / PRODUCER: KIMURA MISA / THE GREAT LEGACY ARTIST: SATO ATSUHIRO.
