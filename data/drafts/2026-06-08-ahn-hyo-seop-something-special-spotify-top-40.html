Ahn Hyo-seop's debut solo single 'Something Special,' a collaboration with global artist Khalid, entered the Spotify US Daily Chart Top 40 on June 8. As of June 7 (local time), the track reached No. 39 on the chart, marking a new personal best for the actor-turned-artist.

'Something Special' is Ahn Hyo-seop's official solo debut single and also marks the first time multi-platinum artist Khalid has collaborated with a Korean artist. Released on May 22, the track has been drawing steady attention from global listeners and continuing its upward climb.

The chart trajectory has been notable. 'Something Special' first entered the Spotify US Daily Chart at No. 124 on June 1, then rose to No. 117 on June 2 and No. 41 on June 4, before landing at No. 39 on June 7 to break into the Top 40. The rapid ascent demonstrates strong response from local listeners in a short period of time.

The Spotify US Daily Chart is particularly significant because it is compiled based on actual streaming data within the United States, directly reflecting consumption by local listeners beyond just the global fandom. This achievement is seen as evidence that actor Ahn Hyo-seop's global influence is now extending into the music market.

The project was released through Musicow's global project 'FANDOM.' Musicow developed the project in partnership with US entertainment company Roc Nation, and an official music video featuring the Ahn Hyo-seop and Khalid collaboration is scheduled for release in June.

This is not the first time Ahn Hyo-seop has made an impact through his voice. He gained global recognition by playing Jinwoo, the leader of Lion Boyz, in 'K-pop Demon Hunters,' the Netflix original film released last year that captivated audiences worldwide.
